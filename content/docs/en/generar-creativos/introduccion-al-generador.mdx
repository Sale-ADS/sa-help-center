## How the Generator Works

The generator is simple and straightforward. You don't need to know about design or artificial intelligence; you just need to know what you want to communicate:

1. **Indicate what you want to create** - Provide information about the image you need
2. **Receive the images** - The AI generates multiple options for you to choose from

**Example for a coffee shop:**
> "My smartphone on a marble table with natural light and plants in the background"

**Example for a wine shop:**

> "Wine bottle on an elegant table with glasses, cheese, and grapes, warm sunset lighting"

**Example for a gym:**
> "Person exercising in a modern gym, energetic lighting, motivational atmosphere"

**Just indicate** what image you need (and optionally upload a product photo); I'll take care of the rest.

## When to Use the Generator

The decision is simple: if you don't have the perfect visual content, the generator is your best ally. Here's a quick guide to help you decide:

| Situation | Recommendation |
|-----------|---------------|
| You don't have product images | Use the generator |
| You want to try different styles | Use the generator |
| You need quick content | Use the generator |
| You have perfect professional photos | Upload manually |
| The strategy requires videos | Not available for videos |

## Where to Find the Generator

You can access the generator directly from a strategy's configuration flow. You don't need to look for it in any hidden menu:

1. Select a strategy that requires images
2. In the creatives section
3. Click **"Generate with AI"**
4. Access the generator directly

---

## Generation Cost

Generation consumes credits based on the quality level chosen. You decide how much to invest in each image based on the quality you need:

| Level | Credits per image |
|-------|---------------------|
| **Pro** | 23 credits |
| **Ultra** | 106 credits |

You have full control: you can mix levels depending on the importance of each image. Use Pro for quick tests and Ultra when you need the highest quality.

## Generation Flow

The entire process takes between 30 seconds and 2 minutes. That's how quickly you go from an idea to images ready for your campaign:

```
1. Provide information about what you want to create
       ↓
2. Optionally upload a photo of your product
       ↓
3. Select quality level (Pro or Ultra)
       ↓
4. Generate images
       ↓
5. Receive the generated images
       ↓
6. Select the best ones to use in your strategy
```

Just provide simple information and in seconds you'll have the images ready; we take care of creating the perfect creative.

## Limitations

So you know exactly what to expect, here's what the generator can and cannot do. Full transparency:

The generator **can** create:
- Product images in attractive scenes
- Lifestyle scenes with professional settings
- Creative and artistic backgrounds
- Different artistic styles (minimalist, vibrant, elegant, etc.)

The generator **cannot** create:
- Videos (images only)
- Legible text in images (it may come out distorted)
- Faces of specific people
- Exact brand logos

Knowing these limitations helps you get the most out of it. If you need text on your image, add it afterward with any basic editor.
