Creating effective advertising images is easier than you think. You don't need to describe complex scenes or use technical language. Just follow a simple 4-part format.

**Important:** This is not ChatGPT or any other text tool. Here we create real advertising images for your business. Don't describe the environment or say "put this product in this place." Simply provide your essential business information.

---

## The Correct Format

Your prompt should follow this exact structure:

```
[Business name] | [Business description] | [4 benefits] | [Discount or "no discount"]
```

### The 4 Components:

| Component | Description | Examples |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **Business name** | Your company name (real or fictional) | "The Burger Joint", "Valley Flowers", "flower shop in miami" |
| **Business description** | What your business does, what you sell | "Artisan burgers with 100% beef", "Floral arrangements for any occasion" |
| **4 benefits** | 4 specific advantages you offer | "Free delivery, Organic ingredients, 24h service, Express shipping" |
| **Discount** | Specific offer or "no discount" | "20% off", "buy one get one free", "no discount" |

## What NOT to Do (Incorrect Examples)

❌ **Don't describe the environment or scene:**
> ~~"A burger on a rustic wooden table with natural light and golden fries on the side"~~

❌ **Don't use ChatGPT-style image creation language:**
> ~~"Create an image of a flower shop with red roses in an elegant setting"~~

❌ **Don't technically describe lighting or angle:**
> ~~"Photo of chocolate cake with soft lighting and professional-style blurred background"~~

✅ **Instead, use the direct format:**
> "Sweet Temptation | Artisan chocolate cakes | Imported Belgian chocolate, Homemade ganache topping, Free design customization, Delivery in under 4 hours | 10% off large cakes"

## Tips for Each Component

### 1. Business Name
- Can be real or fictional
- Can be generic: "restaurant in miami", "flower shop los angeles"
- Can include location: "La Pampa Miami", "Burger Joint LA"

### 2. Business Description
- Brief and clear (maximum 10-12 words)
- Focus on what you sell or what you do
- Examples: "Artisan burgers", "Custom floral arrangements", "Casual women's clothing"

### 3. The 4 Benefits
- List exactly 4 advantages separated by commas
- Be specific and measurable when possible
- Avoid generic words like "good" or "nice"
- Good examples: "Free 30-minute delivery", "1-year warranty", "24-hour service"

### 4. The Discount
- **With discount:** Specify the exact percentage or promotion
  - "20% off everything"
  - "Buy one get one free on burgers"
  - "Buy 3 pay for 2"
- **No discount:** Write explicitly "no discount"

## Format Variations

### If you don't have a current discount:
> "Your Business | What you offer | Benefit 1, Benefit 2, Benefit 3, Benefit 4 | no discount"

### If you want to use a generic name:
> "restaurant in miami | Artisan fast food | Fresh daily ingredients, Free delivery, 15-minute preparation, Varied menu | 15% off"

### If you have a special promotion:
> "La Pampa Fitness | 24-hour gym | Certified trainers, Group classes included, Locker room with lockers, Free parking | First week free"

## Important Limitations

So you have clear expectations from the start:

### What we CANNOT do:
- **Specific text in the image** — We cannot generate legible text (exact prices, brand names). You add text afterward with Canva or similar.
- **Specific people** — We cannot recreate faces of known or real individuals.
- **Exact logos** — We cannot reproduce brand logos with precision.

### What we CAN do:
- Create attractive images showing your product/service
- Include visual elements related to your benefits
- Generate images focused on promotions and discounts
- Show your business professionally for social media
