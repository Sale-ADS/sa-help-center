You don't need to write technical prompts or be a graphic designer. Just provide information about what you want to create and we'll take care of the rest. Speak naturally, as if you were explaining to a colleague what image you need, and I'll transform that description into a professional creative ready for your ads.

Many users think they need design knowledge or special phrases to get good results. The reality is completely different: you provide simple information about your product and the style you're looking for, and we take all the cognitive load of turning it into something visual and impactful.

---

## What Information to Provide?

You only need to indicate three basic things about what you want to create:

- **What you want to show** -- Your product, service, or main concept
- **The context or setting** -- Where or how you want it to look (on a table, in use, in a modern environment)
- **The objective** -- What you want to convey with the image (elegance, energy, trust, urgency)

**You provide the information, we create the creative.** Simple and hassle-free. The more relevant information you give us, the more precise the result, but even with a brief description we can generate something excellent.

---

## What Information You Can Provide

You can indicate information as simple as you want. There's no required format or magic keywords:

- **Your product or service** -- What you want to show
- **The setting** -- Where you want it to appear (e.g., "on a table", "in use", "in a modern environment")
- **The objective** -- What you want to convey (e.g., "elegant", "modern", "professional")

### Simple Example

**Information you provide:**
> "I want to show my smartphone in an elegant and modern setting"

**We create:** A professional image of your smartphone in a perfect scenario for your brand, with carefully crafted lighting and advertising composition.

**You just provide the idea, we do the rest.**

## Useful Information You Can Provide

You can mention simple concepts that help us understand exactly the style you're looking for:

### Mood or Style

- **"Modern"** -- For a current and technological look
- **"Elegant"** -- To convey sophistication
- **"Professional"** -- For a corporate atmosphere
- **"Cozy"** -- To convey warmth
- **"Minimalist"** -- For a clean and simple look
- **"Rustic"** -- For artisanal or natural products
- **"Vibrant"** -- For young and energetic brands
- **"Luxury"** -- For high-end products

### Image Objective

- **"Show quality"** -- To highlight product excellence
- **"Generate desire"** -- To create aspirations in the viewer
- **"Convey trust"** -- To build credibility and authority
- **"Show in use"** -- To see the product in action, in a real context

**You don't need to be technical.** Speak naturally, describe what you imagine, and we'll create the perfect creative.

## Limitations to Keep in Mind

So you have clear expectations from the start, it's important to know what we can and cannot do with image generation:

### What We Cannot Do

- **Specific text** -- We cannot generate legible text in images (prices, offers, brand names). If you need text, add it afterward with an editing tool like Canva.
- **Specific people** -- We cannot recreate faces of known or real individuals
- **Exact logos** -- We cannot reproduce brand logos with pixel-perfect precision

### What We Can Do

- Create product images in different scenarios and settings
- Generate professional settings and visual concepts
- Show concepts and services in an attractive, commercial way
- Adapt the visual style to your brand and audience

## If You Need Adjustments

If the first result isn't exactly what you need, don't worry. The refinement process is normal and designed to be fast:

1. **Indicate what to change** -- Mention which aspect you want to adjust (brighter, darker, different angle, different setting)
2. **Provide more information** -- Add details about what you're looking for
3. **Regenerate** -- We create a new version incorporating your feedback

**Refinement example:**
- First image: "I want to show my coffee"
- If you need an adjustment: "I like it but I want it to look cozier and warmer, with sunset light"
- We create a new version with that adjustment

**You just indicate what you want to change, we do it.** Each iteration gets you closer to the perfect creative.
