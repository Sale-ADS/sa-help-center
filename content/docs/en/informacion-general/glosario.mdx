Throughout the platform and this documentation, you will find technical terms related to digital advertising, marketing, and technology. Do not worry if some of them are unfamiliar. This glossary gathers all the definitions you need to understand every feature of SaleAds.ai without having to search elsewhere.

**You don't need to be an expert.** If you encounter a word you do not know while using the platform, come back here to look it up. We have written each definition with clarity in mind, not jargon.

---

## A

### Ad Account

Account within Meta (or Google, or TikTok) where your ads are billed. Each advertising platform requires an account with an active payment method to be able to launch campaigns. In SaleAds.ai, you connect your Ad Account during the initial setup and I take care of publishing the ads through it.

### AHA Moment

The moment when you experience the real value of SaleAds.ai. For most users, this happens when they generate their first image with artificial intelligence and see how a simple text becomes a professional creative in seconds.

**You have the power to experience** this moment from day one. When you see your first generated creative, you will understand the true value of the platform.

### Audience

The group of people you want your ads to reach. It is defined by geographic location, interests, demographics, and behaviors. In SaleAds.ai, I automatically generate audience suggestions based on your Brief and your location.

## B

### Brief

Document that contains all of your business information: target audience, value proposition, objectives, communication tone, competitors, and differentiators. In SaleAds.ai, the Brief is automatically generated with AI from an audio recording that you make, describing your business in your own words.

You record an audio speaking naturally about your business, and I generate the complete Brief. You do not need to fill out long forms or write documents.

### Business Manager

Meta tool that allows you to manage your advertising assets (pages, ad accounts, pixels, catalogs) in one place. It is an administrative level above the individual ad account.

**You have full control** over your assets from a single place. SaleAds.ai connects to your Business Manager to access the assets it needs.

## C

### Campaign

Top-level advertising structure that groups ad sets. In Meta Ads, a campaign defines the objective (sales, traffic, awareness) and contains one or more ad sets with their respective creatives.

### Copy

The text of your ad. It includes the primary text (what appears above the image), the headline (below the image), and the description (additional text below the headline). In SaleAds.ai, copies are automatically generated with AI based on your Brief, your strategy, and your target audience.

I generate copies optimized for each platform and objective. You just review and approve them.

### CPC (Cost per Click)

The amount you pay each time someone clicks on your ad. It is one of the most important metrics for evaluating the efficiency of your advertising investment.

### CPM (Cost per Thousand Impressions)

The amount you pay for every thousand times your ad is shown. It is useful for brand awareness campaigns where the goal is visibility.

### Creatives

The images or videos shown in your ads. They are the visual component of advertising. They can be:

* **AI-generated**: Images automatically created by me from your description
* **Manually uploaded**: Files that you upload from your device

**You have the power to choose** between generating with AI or uploading your own creatives. Both options produce professional ads.

### Credits

Virtual currency of SaleAds.ai for generating images with AI. Each quality level consumes a different amount of credits: Pro uses 50 credits per image and Ultra uses 100 credits per image. Credits come included in your monthly plan.

### CTR (Click-Through Rate)

The percentage of people who click on your ad out of the total who saw it. A high CTR indicates that your ad is relevant and attractive to your audience.

## D

### Dashboard

The platform's main panel where you can access all features: active campaigns, business configuration, performance metrics, and generation tools.

**You have full control** from your Dashboard. It is your operations center.

### Draft

Intermediate state of a strategy after configuring it and before launching it on Meta. In this state you can review and adjust all elements (copies, creatives, targeting, budget) before it is published.

**You have the power to review** your strategy before launching it. Nothing is published without your approval.

## E

### Engagement

The interaction generated by your posts or ads: likes, comments, shares, saves, and clicks. High engagement indicates that your content resonates with your audience.

### Strategy

In SaleAds.ai, a strategy is a proven and optimized advertising campaign template that you can customize for your business. It includes objective configuration, targeting, ad structure, copies, and creatives. Each strategy is designed to achieve a specific result.

**You don't need to be an expert, you just need SaleAds.** Strategies are designed for easy use, without requiring prior knowledge of digital advertising.

## F

### Funnel

The journey a user takes from discovering your brand to becoming a customer. In advertising, it is usually divided into: awareness, consideration, and conversion. Different strategies in SaleAds.ai target different stages of the funnel.

## G

### AI Generation

The process of automatically creating advertising images using artificial intelligence. You only need to provide information about what you want to create (your product, the style, the objective) and we generate the perfect creative. You do not need design skills or technical prompts.

You provide information and we generate. Speak naturally, no jargon needed.

## I

### Impressions

The number of times your ad is shown on screen. An impression does not mean the person clicked or interacted; only that the ad appeared in their feed or screen.

### Interests

Categories of topics, activities, or behaviors that Meta uses to segment audiences. For example: "fitness", "gourmet cooking", "technology". In SaleAds.ai, I automatically generate relevant interests based on your Brief and your location.

## L

### Launch

The moment when your strategy is published on Meta Ads (or the corresponding platform) and starts being shown to your audience. It is the final step in the campaign creation process.

**From idea to campaign in minutes.** This is the moment where everything comes together and your advertising starts working for you.

## M

### Meta Ads

Meta's advertising platform (formerly Facebook) that includes ads on Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and the Audience Network. It is the most widely used advertising platform by SaleAds.ai users due to its massive reach and detailed targeting options.

## N

### Business

Entity within SaleAds.ai that represents a brand or company. Each business has its own configuration, Brief, connected accounts, and strategies. Depending on your plan, you can have one or several businesses (PRO: 1, BUSINESS: 3, AGENCY: 10).

**You have the power to manage** multiple businesses from a single place, each with its own identity and configuration.

## O

### Optimization

Automatic or manual process of adjusting your campaigns to improve their performance. In SaleAds.ai, the Optimization AI continuously analyzes your campaign data and makes adjustments to maximize your results within your budget.

## P

### Facebook Pixel

Tracking code installed on your website to measure the conversions (purchases, signups, visits) generated by your ads. It significantly improves the optimization of your campaigns because it allows Meta to understand what type of people take valuable actions on your site.

### Plan

Subscription level in SaleAds.ai that determines your usage capacity:

* **PRO**: 1 business, 8 strategies/month, 400 credits
* **BUSINESS**: 3 businesses, 20 strategies/month, 3,180 credits
* **AGENCY**: 10 businesses, unlimited strategies, credits upon request

### Budget

The amount of money you allocate to an advertising campaign. This is the amount that Meta, Google, or TikTok will charge directly to your ad account. It is independent of your SaleAds.ai subscription.

### Information for Generating Creatives

Information you provide about what you want to create. You do not need to write technical prompts, just indicate what you want to show and how you want it to look. Speak naturally and we interpret your intention.

**We take care** of creating the perfect creative based on your information.

## R

### ROAS (Return On Ad Spend)

The return on advertising investment. It is calculated by dividing the revenue generated by campaigns by the total cost invested in advertising. A ROAS of 3x means that for every dollar invested, you generated 3 dollars in sales.

## S

### Targeting

Definition of the audience to whom your ads will be shown. It includes geographic location, interests, demographics (age, gender), behaviors, and connections. Good targeting is the difference between ads that convert and ads that waste budget.

**You have the power to choose** exactly who sees your ads, and I help you with smart suggestions based on your Brief.

## U

### Audience Location

Countries, cities, or regions where you want your ads to be shown. It is configured during your business setup and determines the geographic targeting of all your campaigns.

**You have full control** over where your ads are shown.
