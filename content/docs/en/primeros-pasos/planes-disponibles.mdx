We offer three plans designed for different needs. All include access to artificial intelligence for generating creatives and copies, proven advertising strategies, and automatic campaign optimization.

**You don't need to be an expert, you just need SaleAds.** Choose the plan that best fits your business and start launching professional campaigns from day one.

If you are not sure which one to choose, don't worry. Below you will find a selection guide that will help you decide based on your current situation.

## PRO Plan

**Price:** $59 USD/month

**Ideal for:** Entrepreneurs who want stable daily sales without relying on agencies.

The PRO Plan gives you everything essential to launch professional campaigns for a fraction of what a traditional agency would charge.

### Includes:

| Feature                                   | Quantity                   |
| ----------------------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Credits for AI generation                 | 400 (15 Standard Images)   |
| Strategies per month                    | 8                          |
| Linked businesses                         | 1                          |
| Available professional strategies         | +20                        |

### Features:

* AI Copywriter 24/7
* AI Strategist 24/7
* Automatic AI Optimizer
* Basic Dashboard
* Standard support
* Processing priority

This plan is perfect if you manage a single business and want to launch between 1 and 2 campaigns per week. With 8 monthly strategies you have enough capacity to test different approaches and find what works best for your audience.

## Guide to Choosing Your Plan

Not all businesses need the same thing. Here I help you decide based on your situation:

**Choose PRO if:**
- You have a single business or brand
- You are just starting with digital advertising and want to try without committing to a high expense
- You launch between 1 and 2 campaigns per week
- Your monthly ad budget is under $500 USD

**Choose BUSINESS if:**
- You manage 2 or 3 different businesses or brands
- You need to launch campaigns more frequently (almost daily)
- You want Ultra HD quality creatives for important campaigns
- You need comparative data to optimize your ad spend

## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>

<summary>What does "strategies/month" mean?</summary>

It is the number of advertising strategies you can launch each month. Each strategy is a complete advertising template that generates a campaign on Meta Ads with multiple ads and different creatives. For example, with the PRO Plan you can launch up to 8 different strategies per month, and each one can include multiple ads with different images and copies.

</details>

<details>

<summary>What if I need more than what my plan includes?</summary>

You can:

1. Upgrade to a higher plan with more included credits, more businesses, and more monthly strategies
2. Contact [support](/en/docs/solucion-problemas/contactar-soporte) to evaluate personalized options for your case

To upgrade, go to **Billing** and click **"Upgrade Plan"**: you will be redirected to WhatsApp and an advisor will process the change. The cost difference is prorated on your current billing when the upgrade is processed.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Do unused businesses accumulate?</summary>

No. Business slots are the maximum you can have active at the same time. They do not accumulate month to month. If your BUSINESS Plan allows 3 businesses and you only use 1, the other 2 slots simply remain available for when you need them, but they do not convert into credits or transfer.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Can I change plans at any time?</summary>

Yes. Whenever you need, go to **Settings** → **Billing** and use **"Upgrade Plan"** or **"Change Plan"**: you will be redirected to WhatsApp and an advisor will assist you. For an upgrade, billing is adjusted on a prorated basis as applicable; for a downgrade, the change may take effect at the end of the period ([more information](/en/docs/planes-creditos/facturacion)).

</details>
