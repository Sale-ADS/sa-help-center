In SaleAds.ai **you can only log in with Google or Microsoft**. There is no email and password registration: you use your Google or Microsoft account to access. It is fast and secure. **From idea to campaign in minutes.**

## How to log in or sign up

<Steps>
<Step>
### Go to SaleAds.ai

1. Go to [saleads.ai](https://saleads.ai)
2. Click **"Start Free"**, **"Sign Up"**, or **"Log In"**
</Step>
<Step>
### Choose Google or Microsoft

On the login screen you will see two options:

| Option | Description |
| ------ | ----------- |
| **Sign in with Google** | Log in with your Gmail (Google) account |
| **Sign in with Microsoft** | Log in with your Microsoft account (Outlook, Hotmail, work account) |

- **If you already have a SaleAds.ai account:** Click the same provider (Google or Microsoft) you used the first time. You will be logged in directly.
- **If it is your first time:** Click Google or Microsoft. A window from your provider will open for you to authorize access. You do not create an email or password in SaleAds: everything is done through Google or Microsoft.
</Step>
<Step>
### Authorize with Google or Microsoft

1. A Google or Microsoft window will open
2. Choose the account you want to log in with (if you have multiple)
3. Accept the permissions requested by the provider
4. You will return to SaleAds.ai already connected

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  We do not fill out registration forms. Your email and identity come from your Google or Microsoft account.
</Callout>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>

<summary>Can I use both Google and Microsoft?</summary>

Every time you log in you must use the **same provider** (Google or Microsoft) you used when you first signed up. If you log in with Google one day and Microsoft another, they will be treated as separate accounts.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Can I change the email I log in with?</summary>

The email you log in with is the one from your Google or Microsoft account. To use a different email in SaleAds, log in with a **different** Google or Microsoft account (the one that has the email you want to use).

</details>

<details>

<summary>I cannot log in with Google or Microsoft</summary>

1. Make sure you are using the correct account in the Google or Microsoft window
2. Ensure you accept all permissions requested
3. If you use a pop-up blocker, allow pop-ups for saleads.ai
4. If it still fails, [contact support](/en/docs/solucion-problemas/contactar-soporte)

</details>
