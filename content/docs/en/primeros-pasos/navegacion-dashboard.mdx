Once you have selected your plan, you will have access to the full SaleAds.ai Dashboard. This is your operations center: the place from which you will manage businesses, launch campaigns, review metrics, and access all the artificial intelligence tools I have for you.

Don't worry if the interface seems to have many options at first. I will explain each area of the dashboard so you can take advantage of all the features from day one and know exactly where to find what you need.

---

## Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard is your control center. From here you can access all the platform's features at a glance. The interface is designed so you can find everything intuitively, without needing to memorize routes or complicated menus.

**You have the power to manage** everything from a single place.

### Screen Layout

| Element                 | Location         | Function                              |
| ----------------------- | ---------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| **Side menu**           | Left             | Main navigation                       |
| **Business selector**   | Top left         | Switch between businesses             |
| **Top bar**             | Top              | Search, notifications, profile        |
| **Main area**           | Center           | Content for each section              |

Each element is designed so you can move quickly between sections without losing context of what you are doing.

---

## Navigation Menu

The side menu is your main guide within the platform. It contains the following sections organized by function:

### Brand Center

This section groups everything related to the identity and configuration of your business. It is where you define who you are and what you want to communicate.

| Section                  | Description                                |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------ |
| **Dashboard**            | Overview of your account                   |
| **Set Up Your Business** | Information and Brief for your business    |
| **Connected Accounts**   | Manage Meta Ads and other integrations     |

The **Dashboard** shows you a quick summary of your account status: active campaigns, available credits, and pending notifications. **Set Up Your Business** is where you record your audio to generate the Brief with AI, define your audience, and describe your value proposition. **Connected Accounts** is where you link your advertising platforms (Meta, Google, TikTok) to be able to launch campaigns.

### Strategy Center

This is where the advertising magic happens. From this section you select, configure, and launch your campaigns.

| Section                 | Description                                    |
| ----------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **Copy Strategy**       | Explore and select available strategies        |
| **Active Strategies**   | View your launched strategies                  |

In **Copy Strategy** you will find a catalog of proven and optimized advertising strategies that you can customize for your business. Each strategy includes objectives, ad structure, and targeting configuration. In **Active Strategies** you can monitor the performance of campaigns that are already running, see key metrics, and make adjustments when needed.

### Settings

| Section       | Description                        |
| ------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **Settings**  | Account settings and preferences   |

From here you can manage your profile, change your plan, review billing, and modify general platform preferences.

## Business Selector

If your plan includes multiple businesses (BUSINESS allows up to 3, AGENCY up to 10), you can switch between them instantly:

1. Click the current business name (top left)
2. Select the business you want to switch to
3. The Dashboard will update with that business's information

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  Each business has its own Meta configuration, Brief, and strategies. What you configure in one business does not affect the others, so you can manage completely different brands from the same account.
</Callout>


**You have full control** over all your businesses from a single place. You don't need to log out or switch accounts to manage different brands or clients.

## Top Bar

The top bar is always visible and gives you quick access to three essential tools:

### Search

Use the search field to quickly find what you need without navigating through menus:

* Strategies (by name or type)
* Settings (specific sections)
* Help documents (support center articles)

**You have the power to find** what you need quickly. Just type a few keywords and I will show you the most relevant results.

### Notifications

The bell icon keeps you up to date with everything happening in your account:

* Updates on your strategies (approvals, status changes)
* System alerts (connections, errors, renewals)
* Important messages (new features, scheduled maintenance)

I keep you informed about everything important so you never miss a critical change in your campaigns.

### User Profile

Click your avatar to access personal options:

* **Profile**: Edit your personal information (name, email, password)
* **Settings**: Account settings (plan, billing, preferences)
* **Log out**: Exit the platform securely

## Your Recommended First Tour

After selecting your plan, I recommend following this order to set everything up correctly and launch your first campaign as soon as possible:

### Step 1: Connect Meta Ads

Go to **Connected Accounts** and click **Connect with Meta**.

> This is the most important step. Without connecting Meta, you will not be able to launch strategies. The connection takes less than 2 minutes and you only need to do it once.

### Step 2: Set Up Your Business

Go to **Set Up Your Business** to create your Brief. You will record an audio describing your business and I will automatically generate all the necessary information for your campaigns: target audience, value proposition, communication tone, and more.

### Step 3: Create Your First Strategy

Go to **Copy Strategy** and select one of the available strategies. Each one is designed for a specific objective (sales, traffic, awareness) and comes with an already optimized structure.

### Step 4: Launch

Follow the flow to upload creatives (or generate them with AI) and launch on Meta. **From idea to campaign in minutes.**

## Important Indicators

### Meta Connection Status

In **Connected Accounts** you will see the current status of your integration with Meta. This indicator tells you exactly what you need to do to be ready:

| Status                                    | Meaning                                                    |
| ----------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Not connected**                         | You need to connect your Meta account                      |
| **Connected - Configuration pending**     | You need to select Business Manager and Ad Account         |
| **Configured**                            | Ready to launch strategies                                 |

If you see "Configuration pending", you just need to complete the selection of your advertising assets. It is not an error, simply a missing step.

### Available Credits

In the upper right corner you can see your current credit balance. This number tells you how many images you can generate with AI before they renew with your next billing cycle. If you need more details about how credits work, check the [Credits Included](/en/docs/planes-creditos/creditos-incluidos) guide.
