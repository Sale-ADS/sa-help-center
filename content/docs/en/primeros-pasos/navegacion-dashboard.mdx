SaleAds has been redesigned to give you a cleaner, more intuitive experience. The new dashboard is built to be simple and easy to use from day one.

In this guide, you will learn how the interface is organized, what each area does, and the best path to get your campaigns running faster.

---

## Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is the control center of your account. From here, you can:

- Manage your businesses
- Connect and configure ad channels
- Create and monitor strategies
- Track campaign updates and alerts
- Handle profile, plan, billing, and credits

Everything is centralized in one place so you can move quickly without getting lost.

---

## Side Bar Navigation

In the side bar, right below the logo, you will find your businesses.

### Business Selector

- **PRO plan**: up to 1 business
- **BUSINESS plan**: up to 3 businesses

Click the dropdown arrow to view all businesses. From there, you can also:

- Add a new business
- Upgrade your plan

<Callout type="info" title="Important">
  Each business has its own setup, integrations, and strategies. Changes in one business do not impact the others.
</Callout>

### First Steps

The **First Steps** button takes you to the core onboarding flow. These are the four essential steps to make your ads work properly:

<Steps>
<Step>
### Connect your ad channels

Link your platforms so campaigns can be launched and tracked correctly.
</Step>
<Step>
### Configure your assets

Set up the required assets so everything is ready to publish.
</Step>
<Step>
### Define your brand

Configure identity and tone so ad outputs stay consistent.
</Step>
<Step>
### Create your first strategy

Choose a strategy, complete its setup, and prepare it for launch.
</Step>
</Steps>

These four steps are the foundation for getting better performance from the start.

### My Strategies

Below First Steps, you will find **My Strategies**. This section lists strategies that are:

- Active
- Draft
- Already launched

You can quickly check:

- Which channel they belong to (TikTok, Instagram, Google, etc.)
- Their current status
- Their performance trend

---

## Explore Integrations

In **Explore Integrations**, you connect the channels your operation depends on. Make sure your key platforms are connected:

- Meta
- Instagram
- TikTok
- Facebook
- Google

The more complete your integrations are, the faster you can launch and optimize campaigns.

---

## Resource Manager and Brand Setup

In **Resource Manager**, you build your brand DNA so every ad follows a consistent identity.

In **Your Brand**, define:

- How your brand should speak to customers
- Your color palette
- Brand personality
- Communication tone
- Brand-specific writing style

This setup helps improve ad quality and keeps your messaging aligned.

---

## Bottom Controls

At the bottom of the side bar, you have three key controls:

### 1) Name Button

Click your name to access:

- **My Profile**
- **Log Out**

### 2) Notification Bell

The bell keeps you informed about important events, such as:

- Campaign status updates
- Brief completion alerts
- Errors and system warnings

### 3) Settings Menu

From **Settings**, you can manage:

- Profile
- Business
- Notifications
- Plan
- Billing
- Credits
- Help center

---

## Recommended Getting Started Flow

If you are just getting started, follow this order:

<Steps>
<Step>
### Complete First Steps

Finish the 4 core setup steps to activate your account flow.
</Step>
<Step>
### Connect your integrations

Enable your key channels in **Explore Integrations**.
</Step>
<Step>
### Define your brand

Build a strong brand DNA in **Your Brand**.
</Step>
<Step>
### Create and launch your strategy

Publish your first campaign from **My Strategies**.
</Step>
</Steps>

This flow gets your account ready to run campaigns with better speed and stronger outcomes.
