Choosing the right plan is the gateway to everything SaleAds.ai can do for your business. It is not about spending more, but about choosing the level that fits your current reality. An entrepreneur just starting out needs something different from an agency managing multiple brands, and that is why we offer clear options for every situation.

Choose the plan that best fits your business and start creating professional campaigns in minutes.

## The Plan Selection Screen

When you log in for the first time, a pop-up appears with the available plan options:

* **PRO Plan** - Ideal for entrepreneurs and small businesses who want to launch their first campaigns with professional guidance
* **BUSINESS Plan** - For growing businesses that need more strategies, more credits, and the ability to manage up to 3 brands

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  Take the time you need to review each plan. Compare the features and choose the one that best fits your needs.
</Callout>

## How to select your plan

### 1. Review the Options

On the screen you will see a comparison table with the key aspects. Here I help you understand what each one means so you can make the best decision:

| Feature                | PRO    | BUSINESS |
| ---------------------- | ------ | -------- |
| Businesses             | 1      | 3        |
| Strategies/month       | 8      | 20       |
| Credits included       | 400    | 3,180    |

**Which plan is right for you?** Ask yourself these questions:

- **How many brands do you manage?** If it is just one, PRO is enough. If it is 2-3, you need BUSINESS.
- **How many campaigns do you plan to launch per month?** With 8 strategies you can cover the basics. If you need to test more variations or launch campaigns frequently, BUSINESS gives you room.
- **Will you generate many images with AI?** Credits make the difference. 400 credits are enough to get started, but if you plan to generate content constantly, the 3,180 in BUSINESS go much further.

### 2. Select Billing Period

You can choose between:

* **Monthly**: Pay each month, you can cancel whenever you want. Ideal if you want to try without a long commitment.
* **Annual**: Save up to 20% by paying in advance. Recommended if you are already convinced of the platform's value.

### 3. Click "Start Now"

1. Click the button for the plan you want
2. You will be redirected to the payment gateway (Hotmart)
3. Complete your card details
4. Confirm the payment

### 4. Immediate Access

Once the payment is processed, everything activates in seconds:

* Your account is activated automatically
* You receive the welcome credits
* You can access all features
* You will be redirected to the Dashboard

## What Happens If I Don't Select a Plan?

If you close the plan selection screen without purchasing, you don't lose anything:

* You remain as an **unpurchased user**
* You cannot access the full Dashboard
* Every time you log in, you will see the plan options again
* You can take your time, there is no pressure

The platform will be ready when you are.

<summary>Can I change plans later?</summary>

Yes, you can change your plan at any time from **Dashboard** → **Billing**. Click **"Upgrade Plan"** or **"Change Plan"**: you will be redirected to WhatsApp and an advisor will assist you. For an upgrade, the charge is adjusted on a prorated basis when the change is processed; for a downgrade it may apply at the end of the period ([details](/en/docs/planes-creditos/facturacion)).

</details>
