title: How to add a new business
description: Step-by-step guide to add a new business in SaleAds.ai
---

Learn how to add a new business to your SaleAds.ai account and start setting up your advertising presence.
<YoutubeEmbed videoId="j5NLSC4eJDQ" title="How to add a new business in SaleADS.ai?" />

<Steps>
<Step>
Log in to your SaleAds.ai account
</Step>
<Step>
In the side menu, click **"Set Up Your Business"**
</Step>
<Step>
Select the option to add a new business
</Step>
</Steps>

![Menu to add new business](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/nuevo-negocio-menu.png)
*Select "New business" from the dropdown menu*

<Steps>
<Step>
A pop up will appear
</Step>
<Step>
You will need to add the name of your business
</Step>
</Steps>

![Create business modal](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/crear-negocio-modal.png)
*Enter your business name in the form*

<Steps>
<Step>
Click **"Create"**
</Step>
<Step>
Note: in Pro plan you can have 1 business and in Max plan up to 3 businesses
</Step>
</Steps>
