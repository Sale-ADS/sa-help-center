## Account and Registration

<details>

<summary>How do I create an account or log in?</summary>

Access to SaleAds.ai is only with **Google** or **Microsoft**:

1. Go to [saleads.ai](https://saleads.ai)
2. Click "Start Free", "Sign Up", or "Log In"
3. Choose **Sign in with Google** or **Sign in with Microsoft**
4. Authorize in the Google or Microsoft window
5. The first time, select your plan

There is no email or password form: everything is done with your Google or Microsoft account. **From idea to campaign in minutes.**

</details>

<details>

<summary>Can I try for free before paying?</summary>

To start using the platform you must select a plan (PRO or BUSINESS). Choose the one that best fits your business.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Can I change the email I log in with?</summary>

The email is determined by your Google or Microsoft account. To use a different email in SaleAds, log in with another Google or Microsoft account that has that email.

</details>

<details>

<summary>I don't have a SaleAds password, how do I log in?</summary>

In SaleAds there is no separate password. You always log in with **Google** or **Microsoft**. Use the same option (Google or Microsoft) you used the first time.

</details>

## Plans and Billing

<details>

<summary>Which plan is right for me?</summary>

* **PRO:** For entrepreneurs with 1 business
* **BUSINESS:** For growing businesses (up to 3 brands)
* **AGENCY:** For agencies (up to 10 brands, unlimited strategies)

**You don't need to be an expert, you just need SaleAds.** Choose the plan that best fits your business.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Can I change plans?</summary>

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from Billing.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Are there refunds?</summary>

Check the terms of service for the refund policy.

</details>

<details>

<summary>What payment methods do you accept?</summary>

We accept credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) through Stripe.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Can I cancel whenever I want?</summary>

Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time and you will keep access until the end of the paid period.

</details>

## Connecting with Meta

<details>

<summary>Do I need a Facebook account?</summary>

Yes. You need a personal Facebook account to manage your advertising assets.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Do I need Business Manager?</summary>

Yes. You need access to a Business Manager to connect with SaleAds.ai.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Will SaleAds publish on my behalf without authorization?</summary>

No. I only create ads when you click "Launch". I never publish content without your explicit authorization. **You have full control.**

</details>

<details>

<summary>Can you see my messages or personal data?</summary>

No. We only access information related to advertising (pages, ad accounts, campaign metrics). We do not have access to personal messages or private data.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Why don't my pages appear?</summary>

Make sure that:

1. The page is added to your Business Manager
2. You have an administrator role on the page
3. You granted access to all pages during the connection

</details>

## Strategies and Campaigns

<details>

<summary>What is a strategy?</summary>

A strategy is a proven ad campaign template that you customize with your creatives and I optimize with personalized copies. **You don't need to be an expert, you just need SaleAds.**

</details>

<details>

<summary>How many strategies can I create?</summary>

It depends on your plan:

* PRO: 8/month
* BUSINESS: 20/month
* AGENCY: Unlimited

</details>

<details>

<summary>Why is my campaign created paused?</summary>

For safety. This way you can review everything before spending. Activate it when you are ready. **You decide when to start.**

</details>

<details>

<summary>How much should I spend per day?</summary>

Minimum $3 USD. We recommend $8-10 USD for better results. Adjust based on your budget. **You control your investment.**

</details>

## Technical Issues

<details>

<summary>The page does not load correctly</summary>

1. Reload the page
2. Clear the browser cache
3. Try in incognito mode
4. Use a different browser

</details>

<details>

<summary>I cannot upload files</summary>

Verify:

* File format (MP4, MOV, AVI for videos; JPG, PNG for images)
* Maximum size of 10 MB
* Stable internet connection

</details>

<details>

<summary>The AI generator is not working</summary>

1. Verify that you have available credits
2. Provide clear information about what you want to create
3. Try providing more details or adjusting the information
4. Reload the page if needed

</details>
