This guide covers the errors that can occur when trying to launch your strategy on Meta. Do not worry, each error has an identifiable cause and a clear solution.

---

## Configuration Errors

### "No business selected"

**Cause:** You do not have an active business on the platform.

**Solution:**

1. Use the business selector in the sidebar
2. Select or create a business
3. Complete the Brief configuration

If you are missing this step, the strategy cannot be launched.

---

### "Please select at least one location"

**Cause:** No audience location is configured.

**Solution:**

1. Go to "Configure Your Business"
2. Complete the audience location section
3. Add at least one country, city, or region
4. Return to the strategy

With at least one location, you can proceed.

---

### "No draft to launch"

**Message:** `No draft to launch. Please reconfigure the strategy.`

**Cause:** The strategy draft was not created correctly.

**Solution:**

1. Go back to the configuration step
2. Upload the creatives again
3. Generate the preview
4. Try launching again

---

## Meta Errors

### "Error launching strategy"

**Message:** `Error launching strategy. Please try again.`

**Possible causes:**

* Connection issue with Meta
* Ad account with restrictions
* Invalid payment method
* Unapproved content

**Solution:**

1. Check your internet connection
2. Verify your account status in Meta Business Manager
3. Confirm you have an active payment method
4. Try launching again
5. If it persists, contact support

It is usually due to connection, account restrictions, or payments. Rule them out with these steps.

---

### "Ad account restricted"

**Cause:** Meta restricted your ad account.

**Solution:**

1. Go to [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com)
2. Review your account notifications
3. Follow Meta's instructions to resolve
4. Once resolved, try launching again

This case is resolved directly in Meta.

---

### "Invalid payment method"

**Cause:** There is no payment method or the existing one was declined.

**Solution:**

1. Go to Meta Business Manager
2. Go to Payment settings
3. Add or update your payment method
4. Verify the card has funds
5. Try launching again

---

## Errors During the Process

### "Error saving copies"

**Cause:** Problem saving the edited texts.

**Solution:**

1. Check your connection
2. Click "Retry"
3. If it fails, go back to the preview and save the copies manually

---

### "Error preparing campaign"

**Cause:** Problem configuring the campaign structure.

**Solution:**

1. Wait a few seconds
2. Try again
3. If it continues, verify the configuration in Meta

---

### "Error creating in Meta Ads Manager"

**Cause:** Communication problem with Meta.

**Solution:**

1. Verify that Meta Ads Manager is working: [adsmanager.facebook.com](https://adsmanager.facebook.com)
2. Wait a few minutes
3. Try launching again
4. If the problem is on Meta's side, wait for it to be resolved

---

## Launch Cancellation

### "Launch canceled"

**Message:** `Launch canceled`

**Cause:** You canceled the process before completion.

**Note:** This is not an error. Your strategy remains as a draft and you can launch whenever you want.

---

### "Error canceling the launch"

**Cause:** Problem trying to stop the process.

**Solution:**

1. Wait for the process to finish or fail
2. Check the strategy status afterward
3. If it was left incomplete, you may need to create a new one

---

## After an Error

### Verify Status

After an error, verify:

1. Was the strategy created on Meta? (Check Ads Manager)
2. Is the draft still available in SaleAds?
3. Can you try launching again?

### If the Strategy Was Partially Created

If the campaign appears on Meta but is incomplete:

1. Delete it from Meta Ads Manager
2. Go back to SaleAds
3. Launch again from the draft

---

## Prevention

To avoid launch errors:

* ✅ Verify the connection with Meta before launching
* ✅ Confirm you have an active payment method
* ✅ Check that there are no restrictions on your account
* ✅ Ensure a stable internet connection
* ✅ Complete all configuration before launching
