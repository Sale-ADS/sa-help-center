## Before Contacting

To resolve your issue faster, have this information ready:

### Basic Information

* [ ] Your account email
* [ ] Your plan (PRO, BUSINESS, AGENCY)
* [ ] What you were trying to do

### About the Problem

* [ ] Exact error message (if any)
* [ ] When the problem started
* [ ] What steps you followed before the error
* [ ] Whether the problem is new or has always occurred

### Technical Information

* [ ] Browser you are using (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
* [ ] Device (computer, tablet, mobile)
* [ ] Screenshot of the error (if possible)

**To help you faster:** this information allows us to understand and reproduce the problem.

---

## Support Channels

### In-Platform Chat

1. Click the chat icon in the lower corner
2. Type your question
3. Our team will respond promptly

You can write to us whenever you need.

**Hours:** Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (Colombia time)

### Email

Write to: **support@saleads.ai**&#x20;

Include:

* Descriptive subject line
* Detailed description of the problem
* Screenshots if applicable

**Response time:** 24-48 business hours

## Types of Inquiries

### Technical Inquiries

* Platform errors
* Meta connection problems
* File upload errors
* AI generation issues

### Billing Inquiries

* Questions about charges
* Plan changes
* Invoice requests
* Payment methods

### Usage Inquiries

* How to use a feature
* Best practices
* Strategy recommendations

---

## Issues We Cannot Resolve

Some topics are outside our scope:

### Meta Issues

* Facebook account blocked → Contact Meta
* Ads rejected by policies → Review Meta policies
* Business Manager problems → Contact Meta

### Payment Issues

* Card declined by the bank → Contact your bank
* Charge disputes → Use Stripe or your bank

## Preparing a Good Inquiry

### Example of an Effective Inquiry

> **Subject:** Error launching strategy - "Error creating on Meta"
>
> **Description:** When trying to launch my e-commerce strategy, I receive the error "Error creating on Meta Ads Manager".
>
> **Steps I followed:**
>
> 1. I selected the "Dynamic Catalog" strategy
> 2. I uploaded 3 images successfully
> 3. The preview was generated correctly
> 4. When clicking "Launch", the error appeared
>
> **Additional information:**
>
> * Plan: BUSINESS
> * Browser: Chrome 120
> * The error occurred today for the first time
> * My Meta account has no restrictions (I verified)
>
> **Screenshot:** \[attached]

### Example of an Ineffective Inquiry

> "The platform doesn't work"
