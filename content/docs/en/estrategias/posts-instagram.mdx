Some strategies allow you to use existing posts from your Instagram account instead of uploading new creatives. This is one of the smartest ways to advertise because you leverage content that has already proven to work with your organic audience.

When a post already has likes, comments, and shares, that social proof becomes a huge competitive advantage over a completely new ad starting from scratch.

---

## What Is Promoting a Post?

Instead of creating a new ad from scratch, you can take content you've already published on Instagram and turn it into paid advertising. I handle configuring everything so that post reaches a much wider audience than the one that saw it organically.

Specifically, you can:

* Take an existing Instagram post
* Turn it into a paid ad
* Show it to a wider audience defined by the strategy

**Advantages of using existing posts:**

* You use content that already worked (has likes, comments) -- that generates immediate trust in whoever sees the ad
* You don't need to create new creatives or invest time in production
* Existing engagement acts as social proof and improves conversion rates
* Setup is faster because the content is already ready

You reuse your best Instagram content and turn it into professional ads without creating creatives from scratch. It's the most efficient way to get more value from your organic content.

---

## When to Use Existing Posts vs New Creatives

Before deciding which option to choose, consider this guide. Both options have their ideal moment, and knowing when to use each one will make your campaigns more effective:

| Use Existing Posts when...             | Use New Creatives when...                |
| --------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| You have posts with good engagement     | You want content specific to ads         |
| You want to leverage social proof       | You don't have high-quality posts        |
| The content has already proven to work  | You want to test new creatives           |
| You want to set up quickly              | You have professional product images     |
| Your organic audience responds well     | You need a different message than organic |

**Tip:** If you have a post that got a lot of engagement in the first 24 hours, it's an excellent candidate for promoting as an ad. That initial traction indicates the content resonates with people.

## Requirements

### Instagram Account

To use existing posts as ads, you need to have your Instagram account properly configured:

* You must have [Instagram connected](/en/docs/conectar-plataformas/meta-ads/activos-digitales/instagram) to SaleAds.ai
* The account must be a business or creator account (it doesn't work with personal accounts)
* It must be linked to your Facebook Page

If you haven't connected Instagram yet, you can do so from the Connected Accounts section in your Dashboard. The process takes just a few minutes.

### Eligible Posts

Not all posts can be promoted. Meta has specific policies about what content can become paid advertising:

* The post must comply with Meta advertising policies
* It cannot have copyrighted music (songs from unlicensed artists)
* It cannot have restricted special effects (certain AR filters)
* The content cannot include sensitive or age-restricted material

## How to Use Existing Posts

### Step 1: Select a Compatible Strategy

Not all strategies support Instagram posts. Those that do are specifically designed to leverage existing organic content.

1. Choose a strategy that supports Instagram posts (you'll see it indicated in the strategy description)
2. In the configuration, you'll see the option to use existing posts alongside the option to upload creatives

You choose which posts to promote and which to leave as organic content only.

### Step 2: View Available Posts

Once you choose the Instagram posts option, I load your eligible content for you to review:

1. Click on the **Instagram posts** option
2. Your eligible posts will load automatically
3. For each post you'll see: image/video, caption, likes, and comments

I show you all your available posts with their metrics so you can make the best decision based on real performance data.

### Step 3: Select Posts

Now you can choose which content you want to turn into an ad:

1. Click on the posts you want to promote
2. You can select up to the maximum indicated by the strategy
3. You'll see a counter: "2/3 selected"

I recommend choosing the posts with the highest engagement (most likes and comments), since that social proof will be displayed in the ad and generate more trust with your audience.

### Step 4: Continue

With your posts selected, you're ready to move to the next step:

1. Once you have the posts selected
2. Click continue
3. You'll proceed to the preview and launch

## Ineligible Posts

If you don't see all your posts in the list, don't worry. Some may not meet Meta's requirements for paid advertising:

| Reason                    | Explanation                                 |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| **Restricted music**      | Uses copyrighted audio                      |
| **Unsupported effects**   | Uses filters or special effects             |
| **Policies**              | Content that Meta does not allow to be promoted |
| **Very old post**         | Some very old posts are not eligible        |

If a post you're interested in doesn't appear, check that it doesn't have copyrighted music. This is the most common cause. You can edit the original post on Instagram to change or remove the music, and then try again.

## Selection Limit

Each strategy has a specific limit of posts you can select:

```
Select up to 3 posts to promote
```

If you reach the maximum:

* You won't be able to select more posts
* Deselect one to add a different one

This limit is designed so the strategy works optimally. Each selected post becomes an individual ad within the campaign, and Meta optimizes delivery by showing the best-performing one more often.

## Common Errors

### "No eligible posts found"

**Possible causes:** None of your posts meet Meta's advertising requirements, or your Instagram account isn't properly connected.

**Solution:**

1. Verify that your Instagram account is connected in Connected Accounts
2. Publish new content that complies with Meta advertising policies (no copyrighted music, no restricted effects)
3. If you don't have eligible posts, use the upload creatives or generate with AI option instead

### "Error loading posts"

**Possible causes:** Connection issue with the Instagram API, or the connection session has expired.

**Solution:**

1. Click **"Retry"** to try loading the posts again
2. Verify that Instagram is still connected in Connected Accounts
3. If the problem persists, reconnect your Instagram account from Connected Accounts

Don't worry, reconnecting doesn't affect your existing campaigns or your configuration.
