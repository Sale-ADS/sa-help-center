Imagine being able to launch advertising campaigns with the same quality as a professional agency, but without needing to understand algorithms, complex targeting, or technical metrics. That's exactly what a **strategy** does for you in SaleAds.ai: it transforms your business idea into a campaign ready to publish, with a proven structure, AI-generated copies, and intelligent targeting based on your location.

Strategies are designed so you can launch professional campaigns without technical knowledge, in 1-3 minutes.

---

## Strategy Concept

A strategy is not simply a "campaign". It's a complete, proven advertising communication template that I've tested and optimized to work from day one. Each strategy includes everything you need for your ad to make a real impact:

| Component        | Description                                  |
| ---------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| **Objective**    | What you want to achieve (sales, leads, traffic) |
| **Structure**    | How the ads are organized                    |
| **Targeting**    | Who sees them (based on your location)       |
| **Creatives**    | Images or videos you need                    |
| **Copies**       | Texts that I generate with AI                |

> **Difference from "Campaign":** In SaleAds.ai we use "strategy" because it's not just a campaign, but a complete proven communication strategy.

The advantage of working with strategies is that you focus on what you know about your business, and I handle the technical part. You provide the vision; I provide the execution.

## What Each Strategy Includes

When you select a strategy, you'll see all the information you need to make an informed decision. There's no fine print or surprises: everything is transparent from the start.

### Information Visible in the Catalog

Each strategy shows essential information:

- **Name** - Indicates platform, advantage type, and format (e.g., "SALES Instagram | +Advantage 10 | IMG")
- **Category** - Strategy objective (currently "Conversions")
- **Minimum budget** - Minimum daily amount required (e.g., $4 USD)
- **Content quantity** - Number of creatives needed (e.g., "5 images", "10 videos")

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  Strategies are presented with all key information at a glance. Once selected, you'll be able to see more details and fully configure it before committing a single cent.
</Callout>

## Strategy Status

Once created, your strategy can be in these states. This way you'll always know where each one stands:

| Status       | Meaning                           |
| ------------ | --------------------------------- |

| **Error**    | There was a problem launching     |
