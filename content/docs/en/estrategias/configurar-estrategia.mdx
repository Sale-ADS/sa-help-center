## Configuration Screen

You'll see a two-step wizard:

1. **Configuration** - Budget, location, creatives
2. **Preview & Editing** - Review copies and launch

Each step is designed so you progress logically, without confusing jumps. I tell you what you need at every moment.

## Pre-Launch Checks

Before starting, the system automatically verifies that everything is ready so you don't encounter surprises halfway through:

| Check                               | Status |
| ----------------------------------- | ------ |
| **Meta account connected**          | Pass or Fail |
| **Ad account configured**           | Pass or Fail |

If any check fails, don't worry. I tell you exactly what's missing and how to fix it:

1. You'll see a message indicating what's missing
2. Click **"Configure Now"**
3. Complete the Meta configuration
4. Return to the strategy

## Step 1: Strategy Information

### Selected Strategy

You'll see a summary of the chosen strategy to confirm it's the right one:

* Strategy name
* Category
* Creative requirements

### Daily Budget

The budget is one of the most important decisions. It defines how much you want to invest each day on this campaign. There's no trick: the amount you set is exactly what Meta will spend as a maximum per day.

1. Enter the daily budget in USD
2. Respect the indicated minimum

I recommend values, but you decide.

| Field           | Description                                  |
| --------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| **Platform minimum** | The minimum Meta requires ($3 USD typical). Each SaleAds strategy may require a higher minimum (e.g., $4 USD). |
| **Recommended** | 2-3x the minimum for better results          |
| **Maximum**     | No limit                                     |

**Example:**

> Minimum: $3 | Recommended: $8

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  Starting with low budgets ($5-10) allows you to test without much risk. When you see the campaign is working, you can scale the budget directly from Meta Ads Manager.
</Callout>

## Step 2: Audience Location

The location defines who will see your ads. I use this information along with your Brief to create intelligent targeting that connects your message with the right people.

You'll see the location configured in your Brief:

* Selected countries, cities, or regions
* You can see all added locations

### Change Location

If you need to modify the location, think about where your actual customers are. There's no point showing ads across all of Latin America if your business only delivers in Bogota:

1. You can edit directly if available
2. Or go to **Set Up Your Business** to change it

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  The location directly affects the targeting interests I use to optimize your ads. The more precise it is, the better results you'll get.
</Callout>

## Step 3: Upload Creatives

This is the most important part and where your campaign comes to life. You have two options depending on your needs:

### Option A: Upload Manually

If you already have visual material ready, this is the fastest option. It works for any type of strategy (images or videos):

1. Click **"Upload manually"**
2. Drag files or click to select
3. Upload the indicated amount

**Video Formats:**

* MP4, MOV, AVI
* Maximum 10 MB per file

**Image Formats:**

* JPG, PNG
* Maximum 10 MB per file

### Option B: Generate with AI (Images Only)

If the strategy requires images and you don't have them, this option is your ally. In seconds you can have professional images without needing a designer:

1. Click **"Generate with AI"**
2. The AI will create images for you
3. Select the ones you want to use

> **This is the AHA moment:** When you see the quality of images you can generate, you'll understand the true value of having your AI agent working 24/7.

## Create the Ad

Now that you have all creatives ready, it's time to take the step that activates the AI magic:

1. Click **"Create ad"**
2. The creatives are uploaded to Meta
3. The AI generates the personalized copies based on your Brief
4. You'll proceed to the Preview screen

### Upload Process

You'll see a modal with real-time progress:

1. **Uploading creatives to Meta** - Uploading files
2. **Processing** - Meta processes the files
3. **Generating copies with AI** - Creating texts optimized for your audience

> **Estimated time:** 30 seconds to 2 minutes. Don't close the window during this process.

## Common Errors

Don't worry if an error appears. Here are the most common ones with their direct solution:

### "Please select at least one location"

**Cause:** No location is configured in your Brief.

**Solution:**

1. Go to **Set Up Your Business**
2. Add audience locations
3. Return to the strategy

### "No business selected"

**Cause:** You don't have an active business in the session.

**Solution:**

1. Use the business selector in the sidebar
2. Select or create a business
3. Configure the Brief

### "Error uploading videos to Meta"

**Possible causes:** Issue with the file format, excessive size, or unstable connection.

**Solution:**

1. Verify that the file meets the requirements (MP4, MOV, AVI / max. 10 MB)
2. Try with a different file
3. Check your internet connection

## Missing Assets

If the strategy requires assets you haven't configured, I'll tell you clearly before you waste time:

**Message:** _"To use this strategy, you need to configure some assets"_

For example, WhatsApp strategies require:

* WhatsApp Business configured

**Solution:**

1. Click **"Configure Now"**
2. Complete the Meta configuration
3. Return to the strategy

The next step is to review the copies I generated for you and customize your ad before launching it.
