## Access the Catalog

1. In the side menu, click **"Copy Strategy"**
2. You'll see the full catalog of available strategies

You have full control to explore all the options before committing to one. There's no pressure: browse, compare, and decide when you're ready.

## Catalog View

Strategies are presented on cards with essential information so you can choose quickly. Each card works as a summary sheet that tells you everything you need at a glance.

### Information Shown on Each Card

Each strategy shows:

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Name** | Title indicating platform (Instagram/WhatsApp), advantage type (+Advantage), and format (VID/IMG) |
| **Category** | Label indicating the objective (currently "Conversions") |
| **Minimum budget** | Minimum daily amount required (e.g., $4 USD) |
| **Content quantity** | Number of creatives needed (e.g., "10 videos", "5 images") |
| **Action button** | "Use this strategy" button to select it |

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  Strategies are shown with concise information. Once you select a strategy, you'll be able to see more details and configure it according to your needs.
</Callout>


Now that you know what information each card contains, let's see how to interpret the strategy names so you can navigate the catalog with confidence.

## Understanding Available Strategies

Strategies are organized by different characteristics that you can identify in the name. Learning to read these names will allow you to mentally filter and find what you need much faster:

### By Platform
- **SALES Instagram** - Strategies optimized for the Instagram ecosystem, ideal if your audience is visual and active on this network
- **SALES WhatsApp** - Strategies designed to generate direct conversations with your potential customers

### By Advantage Type (+Advantage)
- **Flexible** - Flexible advantage configuration, perfect if you want Meta to automatically optimize delivery
- **+Advantage 5, 10, 20** - Different optimization levels; the number indicates how many variations Meta will test to find the winning combination

### By Content Format
- **VID** - Requires videos
- **IMG** - Requires images

**Name example:** "SaleADS.AI | SALES Instagram | Flexible +Advantage | VID"
- Platform: Instagram
- Type: Flexible +Advantage
- Format: Videos

The next step is to understand what you need to have ready before selecting your strategy.

## Understanding the Requirements

Before choosing, it's important to know exactly what each strategy will ask of you. This way you avoid surprises and can prepare everything in advance.

### Creative Type

Strategies require a specific type of creative that is clearly indicated in the name:

- **IMG** - Requires images only
- **VID** - Requires videos only

Each strategy is designed for a specific format; there are no mixed strategies.

### Required Quantity

Each card clearly shows the amount of content needed:
- "10 videos" - You need 10 video files
- "5 images" - You need 5 image files
- "20 images" - You need 20 image files

Make sure you have the exact number of creatives before selecting the strategy. If you don't have them, remember that for images you can generate them with AI directly from the platform.

### Minimum Budget

Each strategy shows its required minimum budget (e.g., "$4 USD").

This is the daily minimum that Meta requires for the strategy to work properly. You can set a higher budget in the next step, and in fact I recommend it to get faster results.

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  The recommended budget is usually 2-3x the minimum for better results. Starting with $8-12 USD daily will give you significant data in less time.
</Callout>


Now that you understand the requirements, let's see how to choose the perfect strategy for your situation.

## Selecting a Strategy

### How to Choose

When viewing the strategy cards, ask yourself these questions in order:

1. **Platform** - Where is your audience, on Instagram or WhatsApp?
2. **Available format** - Do you have videos or images ready?
3. **Content quantity** - Can you provide the required amount?
4. **Budget** - Can you cover the daily minimum?

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  If you're not sure which to choose, select any strategy and explore the configuration screen. You're not committed until you click "Launch", so you can explore without risk.
</Callout>


### Use the Strategy

1. Review the available strategy cards
2. Identify the one that best fits your platform and format needs
3. Click **"Use this strategy"**
4. You'll be redirected to the configuration where you can see more details and customize everything

## Choosing Between Image or Video Strategies

This is one of the most important decisions you'll make. The key is simple: choose based on the material you already have available or can easily create.

### Image Strategies (IMG)

Identified by **"IMG"** in the strategy name.

**Characteristics:**
- Require images (quantity varies: 5, 10, 20, etc.)
- Faster to set up if you already have images
- Ideal if you don't have produced videos
- You can generate images with AI if you don't have them

**Example:** "SaleADS.AI | SALES Instagram | +Advantage 5 | IMG" requires 5 images

### Video Strategies (VID)

Identified by **"VID"** in the strategy name.

**Characteristics:**
- Require videos (quantity varies depending on the strategy)
- Higher engagement and potential conversion
- Ideal for showcasing products in use or demos
- Videos must be uploaded manually

**Example:** "SaleADS.AI | SALES Instagram | Flexible +Advantage | VID" requires 10 videos

<Callout type="warn" title="Important">
  Choose based on the type of content you have available. If you have images, look for strategies with "IMG". If you have videos, look for strategies with "VID".
</Callout>
