This section combines three key decisions for launching strategies that actually perform: **location**, **daily budget**, and **copy language**.

When these three settings are clear, targeting becomes more accurate, the algorithm learns faster, and your ads are more likely to produce real results.

---

## 1) Location: where your ad will run

Location defines the exact place where you want your ads to appear. You can select:

- **City**
- **Country**
- **Region/State**

You can choose **multiple locations at the same time** depending on your strategy. For example, you can run ads in Medellin + Bogota + Mexico City if your operation supports it.

<Callout type="info" title="Recommendation">
  Start with focused locations and expand later. This helps you identify winning markets faster.
</Callout>

---

## 2) Budget: your daily campaign investment

Daily budget is how much money you want to invest each day so the strategy can run properly.

In SaleAds.ai, the recommended minimum depends on the strategy type:

- Some strategies work from **$4 USD/day**
- Others require at least **$20 USD/day**
- More demanding strategies may need **$40 USD/day or more**

If your budget is below the recommended minimum, the campaign may take longer to learn or fail to deliver stable performance.

<Callout type="warn" title="Important">
  Not all strategies have the same daily minimum. Always check the recommendation before launch.
</Callout>

---

## 3) Language: which language your copies use

Copy language should match the market where you are advertising.

It depends on the target country or region. Common examples:

- **United States**: English or Spanish (depending on audience)
- **Brazil**: Portuguese
- **Latin American countries**: Spanish

You can choose between **Portuguese, English, or Spanish** based on the audience you want to reach.

---

## Recommended quick setup

Before launching, validate this checklist:

1. You selected one or more correct locations (city, country, or region)
2. You set a daily budget that matches the strategy minimum
3. You chose the right copy language for that market

This helps your strategies start with a stronger setup from day one.
