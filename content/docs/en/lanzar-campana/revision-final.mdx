This is the moment of truth. Before launching your strategy on Meta, you perform a final review to confirm that everything is exactly as you want it. Think of this step as a pilot checking instruments before takeoff: everything important is verified here, and you have the final say.

**From idea to campaign in minutes.** This is the last stop before your ad reaches the world.

---

## Accessing the Review

1. After reviewing the preview and being satisfied with the copies
2. Click **"Review and Publish"**
3. The final review modal will appear with a complete summary

---

## Information Displayed

The review modal presents a consolidated summary of everything you configured. It is your opportunity to verify every detail before committing your investment.

### Strategy Details

| Field                | Description                          |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| **Strategy**         | Name of the selected strategy        |
| **Campaign name**    | Identifier in Meta Ads Manager       |
| **Daily budget**     | How much it will spend per day       |
| **Uploaded creatives** | Number of images/videos            |

### Example

```
Strategy: E-commerce - Dynamic Catalog
Campaign name: Campaign 2026-01-25
Daily budget: $8.00 USD
Uploaded creatives: 3 images
```

---

## Important Warning

You will see a warning message that is actually good news:

> ⚠️ The strategy will be created in a **PAUSED** state. You can activate it from Meta Ads Manager when you are ready.

### What Does This Mean?

This is a safety measure designed to protect your investment:

* Your campaign is created on Meta but **is not activated immediately**
* It will not start spending money until you decide to activate it
* You can review everything in Meta Ads Manager before activating
* If you discover something to change, you can do so without having spent anything

You have full control. Nothing is spent without your explicit authorization.

---

## Verification Mental Model

Before clicking "Launch", mentally walk through these three verification levels. Each one covers a different aspect, and together they ensure your campaign is ready:

### Level 1: Content (What your audience sees)

* [ ] The copies are correct, error-free, and with a clear message
* [ ] The images/videos are the right ones and look good
* [ ] The main message is within the first 125 characters of the text

### Level 2: Configuration (How it runs)

* [ ] The daily budget is the amount you decided
* [ ] The audience location is correct (country/city)
* [ ] The strategy is the right one for your objective

### Level 3: Infrastructure (The technical side on Meta)

* [ ] The ad account has an active payment method
* [ ] There are no restrictions on your Meta account
* [ ] The pixel is configured (if the strategy requires it)

If all three levels check out, you are ready to launch with confidence.

---

## Review Options

### Return to Preview

If during the review you notice something you want to change:

1. Click **"Return to Preview"**
2. Edit what is needed
3. Go back to the review

You can go back and forth between preview and review as many times as you need. There is no limit.

### Launch Strategy

If everything is correct and you passed your mental verification:

1. Click **"Launch strategy on Meta"**
2. You will see a confirmation message
3. Confirm to proceed

---

## Launch Confirmation

A final security dialog will appear:

> **Launch strategy?**
>
> Are you sure you want to launch this strategy?

Options:

* **"Cancel"** - Go back without launching
* **"Yes, launch"** - Proceed with the launch

---

## Launch Process

After confirming, you will see the progress in real time. Each step completes in seconds:

### Stages

1. **Saving copies** - Saving the final texts
2. **Preparing campaign** - Configuring the structure on Meta
3. **Launching on Meta** - Creating everything in Meta Ads Manager

### Estimated Time

* 30 seconds to 2 minutes
* Do not close the window during the process

### Cancel Launch

If you change your mind during the process:

1. Click **"Cancel launch"**
2. The process will stop
3. Your strategy will remain as a draft with no cost

---

## Possible Outcomes

### Success

If everything goes well, you will see the confirmation message:

* **"Strategy created successfully"**
* Status: The strategy is on Meta, paused and ready to activate
* Next step: Activate in Meta Ads Manager when you are ready

### Error

If something fails, do not worry:

* You will see an error message with details of what happened
* The system will try to provide the cause and the solution
* You can retry or contact support for immediate help

## After the Launch

Once launched successfully, your strategy goes into action:

1. The strategy appears in **"Active Strategies"** on your dashboard
2. The initial status is **"Paused"** (remember, you decide when to activate)
3. You must activate it in Meta Ads Manager for it to start spending
4. Once active, you will be able to see performance metrics

**From idea to campaign in minutes.** You did it. The next step is to monitor and optimize.
