This is the final step: your strategy is created in Meta Ads Manager and is ready to be activated.

---

## The Launch Moment

When you click **"Yes, launch"**:

1. I communicate with Meta
2. I create the campaign with all the configuration
3. I upload the creatives
4. I configure the copies
5. I set up the targeting
6. I leave the campaign paused

I do all the technical work; you just give the green light.

---

## Success Screen

If everything goes well, you will see:

> ✅ **Strategy created successfully**
>
> Your strategy is ready and in a **PAUSED** state. You can activate it whenever you want.

### Available Options

| Button                    | Action                   |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| **"Close"**               | Return to Dashboard      |
| **"View my strategies"**  | Go to Active Strategies  |
| **"Activate strategy"**   | Activate immediately     |

---


## Why Is It Created Paused?

The strategy is created paused for several reasons:

1. **Safety:** You can review everything before spending
2. **Control:** You decide when to start
3. **Verification:** Meta can review the content
4. **Flexibility:** You can make final adjustments

---

## Activate the Strategy

You have two options to activate:

### Option 1: From SaleAds.ai

1. Go to **"Active Strategies"**
2. Find your strategy
3. Click **"Activate"**
4. Confirm the activation

### Option 2: From Meta Ads Manager

1. Go to [adsmanager.facebook.com](https://adsmanager.facebook.com)
2. Find the campaign created by SaleAds
3. Change the status from "Paused" to "Active"

---

## What Happens When You Activate

Once active:

1. Meta reviews the content (can take minutes or hours)
2. If approved, it starts being shown
3. Your daily budget starts being consumed
4. You can see metrics in real time

### Possible States

| State           | Meaning                            |
| --------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **Paused**      | Not active, not spending           |
| **In review**   | Meta is verifying the content      |
| **Active**      | Being shown to users               |
| **Rejected**    | Meta did not approve the content   |
| **Limited**     | Running with restrictions          |

---

## Meta Review Time

Meta reviews all new ads:

* **Typically:** 15 minutes to 24 hours
* **Occasionally:** May take longer
* **During holidays:** May be delayed

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  Launch your campaigns in advance if you have important dates.
</Callout>


---

## If Meta Rejects Your Ad

Possible reasons:

* Prohibited content (see Meta policies)
* Excessive text in images
* Unverifiable claims
* Industry-restricted content

**What to do:**

1. Review the rejection reason in Ads Manager
2. Modify the problematic content
3. Create a new strategy with the corrected content

* Clicks
* CTR
* Spend

---

## Pause or Stop

If you need to pause the strategy:

1. Go to **"Active Strategies"**
2. Click **"Pause"**
3. The strategy will stop being shown
4. No more costs will be generated

To reactivate, simply click **"Activate"** again.

---

## Next Steps

After launching:

1. Wait for Meta's approval
2. Activate the strategy when you are ready
3. Monitor results in the first few days
4. Adjust budget based on performance
5. Create more strategies to test
