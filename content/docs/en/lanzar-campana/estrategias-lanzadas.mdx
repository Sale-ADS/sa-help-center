Launching a strategy is just the beginning. The real value appears when you start seeing results and learning what works for your business. From the Active Strategies section, you can monitor, pause, activate, and manage each of your campaigns in one place.

## Access Active Strategies

1. In the side menu, click **"Active Strategies"**
2. You will see the list of all your launched strategies, organized so you can find what you need quickly

---

## List View

Each strategy shows the essential information at a glance:

| Column       | Information                        |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------- |
| **Name**     | Strategy name                      |
| **Status**   | Paused, Active, In review, etc.    |
| **Date**     | When it was created                |
| **Budget**   | Configured daily budget            |
| **Actions**  | Management buttons                 |

---

## Strategy States

Understanding the states helps you know exactly what is happening with each campaign and what action to take:

### Paused

* The strategy is created on Meta but not active
* It does not generate costs while paused
* You can activate it whenever you decide

This is the initial state after launching. It is your opportunity to review everything in Meta Ads Manager before you start investing.

### Active

* The strategy is running on Meta, reaching your audience
* Generating impressions, potentially clicks and conversions
* Consuming daily budget

When you see the first interactions, you will understand the real value of having configured everything correctly.

### In Review

* Meta is verifying that your content complies with their advertising policies
* Temporary state that usually resolves in minutes to a few hours
* Your ad cannot be shown until it is approved

### Rejected

* Meta did not approve the content due to a policy violation
* Review the rejection reason in Meta Ads Manager
* You may need to adjust the creative or copies and create a new strategy

### Error

* There was a technical problem creating the strategy on Meta
* Review the error details to understand what happened
* You can try to repair or create again

---

## Available Actions

### Activate/Pause

* **If paused:** Button to activate so it starts being shown
* **If active:** Button to pause without deleting it

### View Details

* Click on the strategy to see the full information
* Creatives used
* Copies configured
* Complete audience and budget configuration

### Go to Meta Ads Manager

* Direct link to the campaign on Meta for advanced management
* See complete and detailed metrics
* Make adjustments that go beyond what SaleAds offers

---

## What the Metrics Mean

For active strategies, you will see basic metrics that tell the story of your campaign:

| Metric          | What it means                    | Why it matters |
| --------------- | -------------------------------- | -------------- |
| **Impressions** | Times the ad was shown           | Indicates your campaign's reach. More impressions = more people saw your ad |
| **Clicks**      | Interactions with the ad         | Shows how many people were interested enough to take action |
| **Spend**       | Money spent so far               | Lets you control that your investment stays within plan |

**How to interpret the first days:** Do not be alarmed if the first 2-3 days the metrics are low. Meta needs time to optimize delivery and find the right people. Give it at least 3-5 days before making decisions about pausing or scaling.

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  For detailed metrics such as cost per click, conversion rate, and ROAS, use Meta Ads Manager. SaleAds shows you the essentials for a quick overview.
</Callout>

> "You don't have any launched strategies yet"

## Filter Strategies

If you have many strategies and need to find a specific one:

* Search by name to find it quickly
* Filter by status (Active, Paused, etc.)
* Sort by date to see the most recent ones first

---

## Managing Multiple Businesses

If you have several businesses on your plan:

1. Use the business selector in the sidebar
2. Each business has its own independent strategies
3. Metrics and configurations are completely separate

---

## Strategies and Plan Limits

Keep your plan limits in mind when planning your launches:

| Plan     | Strategies/month |
| -------- | ---------------- |
| PRO      | 8                |
| BUSINESS | 20               |
| AGENCY   | Unlimited        |

The counter resets every month. If you need more capacity, you can upgrade your plan at any time.

---

## Next Steps

Now that you have launched strategies, your job is to optimize and scale what works:

1. **Monitor** performance in the first 3-5 days without making changes
2. **Compare** different strategies to see which one connects better with your audience
3. **Adjust** budgets: increase on the ones that work, reduce on the ones that do not
4. **Scale** the winners by gradually increasing budget
5. **Pause** underperforming ones and redirect that budget
