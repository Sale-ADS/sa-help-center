Every time you generate an image with artificial intelligence, I use a portion of the credits that come included in your monthly subscription. Do not worry: there are no separate credit packages or surprise charges. Everything you need is already part of your plan, and from day one you can generate professional creatives at no additional cost.

Understanding how your credits are distributed and consumed will help you plan your campaigns better and make the most of every generated image.

## Credits per Plan

**Each plan includes a monthly amount of credits** that renew automatically with your billing cycle:

* **PRO Plan:** 400 credits (equivalent to 15 Standard Images)
* **BUSINESS Plan:** 3,180 credits (30 ULTRA HD Images or 138 basic)
* **AGENCY Plan:** Contact support

Credits not used during the month do not carry over to the next cycle, so I recommend making the most of them within each billing period.

---

## How Credits Are Consumed

Not all images cost the same. Consumption depends on the quality level you choose when generating:

| Quality Level | Credits per image | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Pro** | 50 credits | Good quality, ideal for most daily campaigns |
| **Ultra** | 100 credits | Maximum quality, perfect for important campaigns or premium products |

**Practical example with PRO Plan (400 credits):**
- If you generate only in Pro quality: up to **8 images** per month
- If you generate only in Ultra quality: up to **4 images** per month
- If you combine both levels: for example, 4 Pro images + 2 Ultra images = 400 credits

**Practical example with BUSINESS Plan (3,180 credits):**
- If you generate only in Pro quality: up to **63 images** per month
- If you generate only in Ultra quality: up to **31 images** per month
- Combining: you have the flexibility to alternate based on the importance of each campaign

## Real-World Usage Scenarios

To help you better gauge your capacity, here are typical scenarios:

| Scenario | Estimated credits | Recommended plan |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Entrepreneur with 2 weekly campaigns (Pro) | ~400/month | PRO |
| Business with varied daily campaigns | ~1,500-2,500/month | BUSINESS |
| Agency with multiple clients and simultaneous campaigns | 3,000+/month | AGENCY |

**You have full control** over how to distribute your credits. You can invest more in an important campaign with Ultra quality and use Pro for the rest of the month.

## Need More Generation Capacity?

If your campaigns are growing and you need to generate more creatives than your current plan allows, you have two options:

1. **Upgrade to a higher plan** — more credits included each month, plus additional features like extra businesses, advanced strategies, and priority support
2. **Contact [support](/en/docs/solucion-problemas/contactar-soporte)** — we will help you find the option that best fits your particular case
