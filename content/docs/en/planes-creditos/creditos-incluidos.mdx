Every time you generate an image with artificial intelligence, a portion of the credits included in your monthly subscription is deducted. Do not worry: there are no separate credit packages or surprise charges. Everything you need is already part of your plan, and from day one you can generate professional creatives at no additional cost.

Understanding how your credits are distributed and consumed will help you plan your campaigns better and make the most of every generated image.

In the product you can check your balance and account-related benefits. You may sometimes see the word **bonuses**; they are part of the same **credits** system included in your plan.

That is separate from **education bonuses** (programs, group mentorship, masterclasses, etc.) that may be included with **annual Pro and Business** plans. See [Education bonuses](/en/docs/planes-creditos/bonos-educacion).

## Credits per Plan

**Each plan includes a monthly amount of credits** that renew automatically with your billing cycle:

* **PRO Plan:** 400 credits per month
* **BUSINESS Plan:** 3,180 credits per month

With the per-quality costs below, that is at most **17 Pro images** or **3 Ultra** (PRO), and **138 Pro images** or **30 Ultra** (BUSINESS).

Credits not used during the month do not carry over to the next cycle, so I recommend making the most of them within each billing period.

---

## How Credits Are Consumed

Not all images cost the same. Consumption depends on the quality level you choose when generating (these are the only two levels in the product):

| Quality level | Credits per image | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Pro** | 23 credits | Good quality, ideal for most daily campaigns |
| **Ultra** | 106 credits | Maximum quality, perfect for important campaigns or premium products |

**Practical example with PRO Plan (400 credits):**
- If you generate only in Pro quality: up to **17 images** per month (400 ÷ 23)
- If you generate only in Ultra quality: up to **3 images** per month (400 ÷ 106)
- If you combine both levels: for example, 4 Pro images + 2 Ultra images = 400 credits

**Practical example with BUSINESS Plan (3,180 credits):**
- If you generate only in Pro quality: up to **138 images** per month (3,180 ÷ 23)
- If you generate only in Ultra quality: up to **30 images** per month (3,180 ÷ 106; the last generation may leave a remainder below 106 credits)
- Combining: you can alternate based on the importance of each campaign

## Using your credits step by step

1. **Check your balance** on the creative generation screen or wherever the product shows it.
2. **Choose the quality** (Pro or Ultra) based on campaign priority; the credit cost is shown before you confirm.
3. **Generate** your images; when you confirm, the corresponding credits are deducted.
4. **Check your balance again** and adjust how many more images you can create during the month.

## Real-World Usage Scenarios

To help you better gauge your capacity, here are typical scenarios:

| Scenario | Estimated credits | Recommended plan |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Entrepreneur with 2 weekly campaigns (Pro) | ~400/month | PRO |
| Business with varied daily campaigns | ~1,500-2,500/month | BUSINESS |

**You have full control** over how to distribute your credits. You can invest more in an important campaign with Ultra quality and use Pro for the rest of the month.

## Need More Generation Capacity?

If your campaigns are growing and you need to generate more creatives than your current plan allows, you have two options:

1. **Upgrade to a higher plan** — more credits included each month, plus additional features like extra businesses, advanced strategies, and priority support
2. **Contact [support](/en/docs/solucion-problemas/contactar-soporte)** — we will help you find the option that best fits your particular case
