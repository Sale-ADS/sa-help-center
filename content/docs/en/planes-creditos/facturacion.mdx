## Accessing Billing

1. In the Dashboard, look for the billing section
2. Or go to **Settings** → **Billing**
3. Click **"Manage"** to access the full portal

## Change Plan

### Upgrade

If your business has grown and you need more strategies, more credits, or to manage more brands, to upgrade your plan you need to contact us from the app:

1. Click **"Upgrade Plan"**. You will be redirected to WhatsApp, where an advisor will assist you and process the change.
2. Confirm the new plan with the advisor.
3. When the advisor processes the upgrade, the prorated difference is charged (you only pay what applies for the remainder of the current period).

### Downgrade

If you need a lower plan, the process protects your current investment:

1. Click **"Change Plan"**. You will be redirected to WhatsApp, where an advisor will assist you and coordinate the change.
2. With the advisor, choose the lower plan and confirm the change.
3. The change takes effect at the end of the current period.
4. You keep all the features of your current plan until that date.

---

## Scheduled Plan Change

If you schedule a downgrade:

* You will see a message: _"Scheduled Plan Change"_
* It indicates: _"Your plan will change from X to Y on \[date]"_
* You can cancel the scheduled change and keep the current plan if you change your mind

## Hotmart Portal

By clicking **"Manage"**, you access the Hotmart portal, the world's most secure payment platform. From there you can manage everything related to your billing:

### Payment Information

* View your current payment method
* Update your credit or debit card
* Add an alternative payment method as a backup

### Invoice History

* View all previous invoices with detail
* Download receipts in PDF for your accounting
* View details of each charge (date, amount, description)

### Billing Details

* Update the name or company on your invoices
* Change billing address
* Add a tax identification number (EIN, VAT, etc.)

## Cancel Subscription

If you need to cancel, the process is simple and friction-free:

1. Go to **"Manage"**
2. Look for the cancel option
3. Confirm the cancellation
4. You will keep access until the end of the paid period

No commitment required. No penalties.

### After Canceling

* Your account remains active until the end date of the paid period
* Your credits remain intact
* You can reactivate at any time without losing data
* Your business data, Brief, and configurations are preserved

## Monthly vs Annual Billing

| Aspect       | Monthly                 | Annual                   |
| ------------ | ----------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Charge       | Every month             | Once a year              |
| Flexibility  | Can cancel each month   | 1-year commitment        |
| Price        | Regular price           | Up to 20% discount       |
| Renewal      | Automatic every month   | Automatic every year     |

If you already know the platform and plan to use it long-term, the annual plan saves you up to 20%. If you prefer total flexibility, the monthly plan lets you adjust without commitment.

## Payment Errors

If there is a problem with the charge, we act quickly so you do not lose access:

1. You will receive an email notifying you of the problem with details
2. Hotmart will automatically retry the charge
3. If it fails, you must update the payment method
4. If not resolved within the grace period, access may be temporarily suspended

### Solution

1. Go to the Hotmart portal from **Manage**
2. Update your card or add a new payment method
3. The charge will be automatically retried within the next few hours

## Common Errors

### "Error loading subscription"

**Cause:** Temporary connection issue with the billing server.

**Solution:** Reload the page. If it persists after a couple of attempts, contact support.

### "Error creating checkout"

**Cause:** Communication issue with the payment gateway.

**Solution:** Check your internet connection. Try again. If it fails, try a different browser or clear the cache.

### "Error opening billing portal"

**Cause:** The Hotmart portal may take a few seconds to load.

**Solution:** Wait a few seconds and try again. If it does not open, try from a private browsing window.
