Credits are the virtual currency of SaleAds.ai that lets you generate images with artificial intelligence. Think of them as creative fuel for your account: every time you generate an image, you spend credits based on the quality level you choose. For **how many credits your plan includes each month**, renewal rules, and image equivalents, see [Credits Included in Your Plan](creditos-incluidos).

In the product you can see your balance and, in some flows, benefits labeled as **bonuses**; for day-to-day AI generation, everything is handled as **credits**. **Education bonuses** (programs, mentoring, etc. on annual plans) are separate: [Education bonuses](bonos-educacion).

You have full control over how and when you use your credits; you choose the quality on each generation.

---

## What Are Credits?

Credits are your access to AI-powered visual content generation. They allow you to:

* Generate professional images for your ads
* Choose between two quality levels (**Pro** and **Ultra**)
* Use the platform flexibly, generating whenever you need to

---

## How You Get Credits

### Credits included in your plan

When you subscribe to a plan, you receive included credits that renew with your billing cycle. Summary amounts:

| Plan     | Credits included (per cycle) |
| -------- | ------------------------------ |
| PRO      | 400                            |
| BUSINESS | 3,180                          |

Details on image equivalents (Pro/Ultra) and the rule that **unused monthly credits do not roll over** are in [Credits Included in Your Plan](creditos-incluidos).

---

## How They Are Consumed

Each image generation consumes credits based on the quality level. The exact cost is always shown in the generation view before you confirm. In the help center we use these reference values (aligned with the included-credits guide):

| Quality level | Credits per image |
| ------------- | ----------------- |
| **Pro**       | 23                |
| **Ultra**     | 106               |

If the app shows a different number for your account, **what you see on screen always wins**.

See also the [Introduction to the Generator](../generar-creativos/introduccion-al-generador).

### Plan Your Consumption

To make the most of your credits, think about how many campaigns you plan to launch and how many images each needs:

**With 400 credits (PRO plan):**
- Up to **17** images in Pro quality (400 ÷ 23), or **3** in Ultra (400 ÷ 106)
- Ideal for 1-2 campaigns with generated images

**With 3,180 credits (BUSINESS plan):**
- Up to **138** images in Pro quality (3,180 ÷ 23), or **30** in Ultra (3,180 ÷ 106; a remainder below 106 may remain)
- Enough for multiple campaigns and testing different styles

**Quick calculation:** divide your credits by the per-image cost for the level you choose (the current amount is shown in the app).

## Consumption Flow

The process is transparent: when you generate an image, credits for the chosen quality level (Pro or Ultra) are deducted. You will see your updated balance before and after each generation.

If you run out of credits, you do not lose access to the platform. You will not be able to generate more AI images until you have enough balance again (for example, **upgrading your plan** or at the **start of the next billing cycle**, depending on your case). Meanwhile, you can always upload images manually. Concrete options: [Credits Included in Your Plan](creditos-incluidos) and [Manage billing](facturacion).

## When Credits Are Consumed

Full transparency on when credits are deducted and when they are not:

Credits are consumed:

* ✅ When generating images with AI
* ✅ When confirming the generation (before seeing the results)

Credits are **NOT** consumed:

* ❌ When uploading images manually
* ❌ When uploading videos
* ❌ When editing copies
* ❌ When launching strategies

Everything else on the platform (creating strategies, launching campaigns, editing copies) is unlimited in that sense within your plan. Credits apply to **AI image generation**.

## Insufficient Credits

If you try to generate without enough credits, you will see a clear message:

> "Insufficient credits - Missing X credits"

**Do not worry.** You can:

1. **Upgrade to a plan with more included credits** — the most practical option if you generate often ([more detail](creditos-incluidos))
2. **Use a lower quality level** — Pro uses fewer credits than Ultra and still delivers professional results
3. **Upload images manually** — if you already have product photos, you do not need to generate
4. **Contact [support](/en/docs/solucion-problemas/contactar-soporte)** — we will guide you based on your account

---

## Need more capacity

If you need to generate more than your current plan allows, see the options in [Credits Included in Your Plan](creditos-incluidos) (upgrade or support). That page includes the key details to find balance and billing screens in the app.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>

<summary>Do monthly plan credits roll over to the next month?</summary>

No. Included subscription credits renew each billing cycle, and per product guidance **unused credits in that period do not carry over to the next**. Details: [Credits Included in Your Plan](creditos-incluidos).

</details>

<details>

<summary>Can I transfer credits to another user?</summary>

No. Credits are personal and non-transferable. Each account manages its own credits independently.

</details>

<details>

<summary>What happens if I cancel my subscription?</summary>

The exact policy may depend on your account type and when you cancel. If you reactivate, balance and conditions will be whatever the application shows at that time. If in doubt, [contact support](/en/docs/solucion-problemas/contactar-soporte).

</details>
