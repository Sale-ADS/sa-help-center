Credits are the virtual currency of SaleAds.ai that allows you to generate images with artificial intelligence. Think of them as the creative fuel for your account: every time you generate an image, you consume credits based on the quality level you choose. Understanding how they work will help you plan your campaigns better and make the most of your plan.

You have full control over how and when you use your credits. I give you the tools; you decide the quality.

---

## What Are Credits?

Credits are your access to AI-powered visual content generation. They allow you to:

* Generate professional images for your ads
* Choose between different quality levels (Pro and Ultra)
* Use the platform flexibly, generating whenever you need to

---

## How to Get Credits

### Welcome Credits

When you subscribe to any plan, you automatically receive included credits:

| Plan     | Credits included |
| -------- | ---------------- |
| PRO      | 400              |
| BUSINESS | 3,180            |
| AGENCY   | Contact us       |

These credits are credited at the moment of your subscription and are available immediately.

---

## How They Are Consumed

Each image generation consumes credits based on the quality level. The exact cost is always shown in the generation view before you confirm, so you know exactly how much you will spend. Check the details in the [Introduction to the Generator](../generar-creativos/introduccion-al-generador).



### Plan Your Consumption

To make the most of your credits, think about how many campaigns you plan to launch and how many images each one needs:

**With 400 credits (PRO Plan):**
- You can generate approximately 8 images in Pro quality, or 4 in Ultra
- Ideal for 1-2 campaigns with generated images

**With 3,180 credits (BUSINESS Plan):**
- You can generate approximately 63 images in Pro quality, or 31 in Ultra
- Enough for multiple campaigns and testing different styles

**For a quick calculation:** Divide your credits by the cost per image of your preferred level and you will know how many images you can generate.

---

## Consumption Flow

The process is transparent: when generating an image, the credits corresponding to the chosen quality level (Pro or Ultra) are deducted. You will always see your updated balance before and after each generation.

If your credits run out, you do not lose access to the platform. You simply will not be able to generate more AI images until you get more credits by upgrading your plan. In the meantime, you can always upload images manually.

---

## View Your Balance

Your credit balance is visible at several points on the platform so you always know how many you have:

### Location

* In the image generation section (before generating)
* On the account settings page
* At the moment of confirming a generation

---

## When Credits Are Consumed

Full transparency on when credits are deducted and when they are not:

Credits are consumed:

* ✅ When generating images with AI
* ✅ When confirming the generation (before seeing the results)

Credits are **NOT** consumed:

* ❌ When uploading images manually
* ❌ When uploading videos
* ❌ When editing copies
* ❌ When launching strategies

This means that everything else on the platform (creating strategies, launching campaigns, editing copies) is unlimited within your plan. Credits only apply to AI image generation.

---

## Insufficient Credits

If you try to generate without enough credits, you will see a clear message:

> "Insufficient credits - Missing X credits"

**Do not worry.** You have several options to continue:

1. **Upgrade to a plan with more included credits** - The most practical option if you generate images frequently
2. **Use a lower quality level** - Pro consumes fewer credits than Ultra and still delivers professional results
3. **Upload images manually** - If you already have product photos, you do not need to generate them

## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>

<summary>Do credits expire?</summary>

No. Purchased or welcome credits do not expire as long as your account is active. You can use them whenever you want, with no time pressure.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Can I transfer credits to another user?</summary>

No. Credits are personal and non-transferable. Each account manages its own credits independently.

</details>

<details>

<summary>What happens if I cancel my subscription?</summary>

Your credits are retained. If you reactivate your account, they will still be available exactly as you left them. You do not lose anything.

</details>
