After connecting your TikTok account, you must select which Business Center and specific ad account you'll use for your campaigns. This step is crucial because it determines where your ads will be published and from where they will be billed.

**You have total control** over which account to use for each business in SaleAds.ai.

---

## Why is Selection Necessary?

### Scenario 1: Single Business Center
If you only have one Business Center with a single ad account, you'll simply confirm that account.

### Scenario 2: Multiple Business Centers or Accounts
If you have access to multiple Business Centers (common in agencies) or multiple ad accounts within one, you must choose which to use for this specific business.

<Callout type="warn" title="Important">
  Each business in SaleAds.ai links to a single TikTok Ads account. This decision is important because it determines where your campaigns will be billed.
</Callout>

---

## Access the Configuration

After connecting to TikTok, you'll automatically see the configuration screen. If you don't see it:

1. Go to **Connected Accounts**
2. In the TikTok section, click **"Complete Configuration"**

![TikTok configuration panel](/images/screenshots/02-conectar-plataformas/tiktok-configuration-panel.png)
*Figure 4.3: TikTok configuration panel showing ad account, profile, and pixel options*

## Step 1: Select Business Center

If you have access to multiple Business Centers:

1. You'll see a list of all available Business Centers
2. Each shows:
   * **Name** of the Business Center
   * **ID** of the Business Center
   * **Country/Region**

### Choose the Correct One

1. Identify the Business Center containing the ad account you want to use
2. Click to select it
3. The Business Center will be highlighted

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  If you have multiple Business Centers, choose the one containing the ad account with the payment method and configuration you need for this business.
</Callout>

---

## Step 2: Select Ad Account

After selecting the Business Center, you'll see the available ad accounts within it:

| Information | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Name** | The name you gave the ad account |
| **ID** | Account number (numeric format) |
| **Status** | Active/Inactive/Suspended |
| **Currency** | The currency configured for the account |

### Choose the Correct One

1. Identify your business or client's account
2. Verify the name and currency are correct
3. Make sure the status is "Active"
4. Click to select it

---

## Account Verification

Before confirming, verify:

### ✅ Account Status
- The account must be **Active**
- It must not have billing restrictions
- It must not be suspended for TikTok policy violations

### ✅ Admin or Operator Access
- You must have admin or operator permissions
- Verify you can create campaigns in TikTok Ads Manager directly

<Callout type="info" title="Role Hierarchy">
TikTok has **two levels** of permissions:

**Business Center Level:**
- **Admin**: Full control over the Business Center
- **Standard**: Access to assigned assets only
- **Finance Manager/Analyst**: Billing management

**Ad Account Level:**
- **Admin**: Full control of the account
- **Operator**: Can create and manage campaigns
- **Analyst**: Read-only access to reports

To use SaleAds.ai you need **Admin** or **Operator** permissions at the **Ad Account** level.
</Callout>

### ✅ Payment Method
- The account must have a valid payment method
- Verify there are no pending or rejected payments

## Can't Find My Account

Don't worry. There are several common causes and all have solutions:

### Possible Cause 1: You didn't give access during connection

When you connect TikTok with SaleAds, you must authorize access to the Business Center. If you didn't, it won't appear in the list.

**Solution:**

1. Go back to the Connected Accounts screen
2. Disconnect the TikTok account
3. Reconnect
4. During authorization, make sure to select **ALL** Business Centers

### Possible Cause 2: The account is in another Business Center

If you don't see the account you're looking for, it might be in a different Business Center.

**Solution:**

1. Go to [business.tiktok.com](https://business.tiktok.com)
2. Verify which Business Center the account is in
3. Make sure you have access to that Business Center
4. Reconnect TikTok and select the correct Business Center

### Possible Cause 3: The account is suspended

Suspended accounts don't appear in the list.

**Solution:**

1. Go to [ads.tiktok.com](https://ads.tiktok.com) and verify the status
2. If it's suspended, resolve the policy issues
3. Wait for reactivation by TikTok

## Selecting Between Multiple Accounts

If you have several accounts available (common in agencies):

### Use the Search

If you have many accounts:

1. Use the **"Search accounts..."** field
2. Type part of the name or ID
3. The list will filter automatically

### For Agencies with Multiple Clients

1. Make sure you select the correct client's account
2. Verify the account has a payment method configured
3. Confirm you have appropriate permissions

---

## Confirm Selection

Once you've selected the correct account, review and proceed:

1. Review that the name, ID, and currency are correct
2. Verify the status is "Active"
3. Click **"Next"**
4. You'll proceed to billing configuration

---

## Change Account Later

If you need to change your TikTok Ads account later, you can, but keep in mind the implications:

1. Go to **Connected Accounts**
2. Click **"Edit Configuration"**
3. You can select a different Business Center and account

> **Warning:** Changing the TikTok Ads account affects existing campaigns. Campaigns in progress will be paused in the previous account and you'll need to recreate them in the new account.

## Common Errors

### "No Business Centers found"

**Message:** `We couldn't find any Business Center in your TikTok account.`

**Possible causes:**

1. You don't have a Business Center created
2. You didn't give access during authorization
3. Temporary connection problem with TikTok

**Solution:**

1. Verify you have a Business Center at [business.tiktok.com](https://business.tiktok.com)
2. If you don't, create one
3. Disconnect and reconnect TikTok in SaleAds.ai

### "No ad accounts found"

**Message:** `No ad accounts found for the selected Business Center.`

**Causes:**

1. The Business Center doesn't have ad accounts created
2. Accounts are in another Business Center
3. Accounts are suspended or pending review

**Solution:**

1. Go to [business.tiktok.com](https://business.tiktok.com)
2. Create a new ad account
3. Wait for TikTok's approval
4. Return to SaleAds.ai and refresh

### "Account not eligible"

**Cause:** The account has restrictions or doesn't meet requirements.

**Solutions:**

- Verify the account isn't suspended
- Confirm it has a payment method configured
- Make sure you have admin or operator permissions
