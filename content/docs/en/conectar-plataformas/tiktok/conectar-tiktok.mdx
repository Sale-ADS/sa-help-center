Connecting your TikTok Ads account opens the door to one of the fastest-growing platforms in digital advertising. TikTok is especially powerful for reaching young audiences and for video campaigns that generate engagement quickly. Once connected, I take care of publishing and managing your video campaigns directly from SaleAds.ai, without you having to navigate through the TikTok Ads Manager.

The connection process is quick and secure. In just a few minutes you will have your account linked and ready to launch your first video campaigns on TikTok.

## Prerequisites

Before connecting TikTok, make sure you have everything ready so the process is smooth:

- **An active TikTok Business account** -- A personal TikTok account is not enough. You need a Business type account, which you can create for free from the TikTok app or from [business.tiktok.com](https://business.tiktok.com). The Business account gives you access to advertising and analytics tools.
- **Access to the TikTok Ads Manager** -- You must have set up your TikTok Ads Manager at [ads.tiktok.com](https://ads.tiktok.com). This includes having completed registration, selected your country and currency, and accepted the advertising terms of service.
- **An active SaleAds.ai account** -- You need an active plan (PRO, BUSINESS, or AGENCY) to connect advertising platforms.

<Callout type="warn" title="Important">
  TikTok requires your Business account to have at least one advertising account created within the Ads Manager. If it is your first time, TikTok will guide you to create one during registration at ads.tiktok.com.
</Callout>

## Steps to Connect

The connection process uses TikTok's secure authorization. At no point do we share or store your TikTok password.

1. **Go to Settings** in the SaleAds.ai dashboard. You will find this option in the left side menu, in the Settings section.
2. **Select Connect Platforms** and then click **TikTok**. You will see the connection button next to the TikTok logo.
3. **Click Authorize with TikTok**. A TikTok authentication system window will open. If your browser blocks pop-ups, make sure to allow them for SaleAds.ai.
4. **Sign in with your TikTok Business account**. Use the credentials associated with your Business account, not a personal account. If you have multiple accounts, make sure to choose the correct one.
5. **Accept the requested permissions**. TikTok will show you the permissions SaleAds.ai needs to manage your campaigns. All permissions are necessary for correct operation.
6. **Select your TikTok Ads Manager account**. If you have multiple advertising accounts, choose the one you want to link. You can connect additional accounts later.

Once the authorization is completed, you will see a confirmation in your SaleAds.ai dashboard indicating that TikTok is connected. **From idea to campaign in minutes.**

## Common Errors

### No ad accounts found

**Possible causes:**
- Your TikTok account is personal, not Business
- You haven't created an advertising account within the TikTok Ads Manager
- The advertising account is suspended or pending review

**Solution:** Make sure you have an active TikTok Ads Manager account. If you don't have one, you can create it at [ads.tiktok.com](https://ads.tiktok.com). The creation process takes a few minutes, and TikTok may take up to 24 hours to activate new accounts.

### Permission denied

**Possible causes:**
- Your TikTok Business account doesn't have administrator permissions in the Ads Manager
- Another user is the primary administrator and hasn't granted you sufficient access

**Solution:** Verify that your TikTok Business account has administrator access to the Ads Manager. If you are part of a team, ask the primary administrator to assign you administrator permissions.

### The authorization fails or the window closes

**Possible causes:**
- Unstable internet connection
- The browser blocked the pop-up window
- Third-party cookies disabled in your browser

**Solution:** Don't worry. Try again from step 1, making sure your browser allows pop-ups and third-party cookies for TikTok. If the problem persists, try in incognito mode or in another browser.
