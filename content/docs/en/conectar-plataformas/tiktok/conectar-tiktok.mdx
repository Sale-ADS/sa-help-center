Connecting your TikTok Ads account opens the door to one of the fastest-growing platforms in digital advertising. TikTok is especially powerful for reaching young audiences and for video campaigns that generate engagement quickly.

Once connected, I take care of publishing and managing your video campaigns directly from SaleAds.ai, without you having to navigate through the TikTok Ads Manager. **From idea to campaign in minutes.**

<Callout type="warn" title="Important">
  This is the most critical point of the process. Take your time and follow each step carefully.
</Callout>

---

## Prerequisites

Before starting, make sure you have:

- An active TikTok Business account
- Access to TikTok Business Center
- A TikTok Ads Manager account created

> If you don't have these requirements yet, check [Prerequisites](requisitos-previos-tiktok) first.

## Current Connection Status

On the Connected Accounts screen you'll see one of these statuses:

| Status | Meaning | Action Required |
|--------|---------|-----------------|
| **Not connected** | You haven't linked your TikTok account | Click "Connect Account" |
| **Connected - Configuration pending** | Connected TikTok but need to select account | Complete configuration |
| **Configured** | Ready to launch strategies | None |

## Connection Process

<YoutubeEmbed videoId="RRdHZMsVEzY" title="How to connect my TikTok account?" />

<Steps>
<Step>
### Access Settings

1. In the Dashboard, go to the side menu
2. Click **"Connected Accounts"**
3. You'll see the **TikTok Ads** section with the connect button
</Step>

<Step>
### Start Connection

1. Click the **"Connect with TikTok"** button
2. A TikTok authentication pop-up window will open

![TikTok Ads login page](/images/screenshots/02-conectar-plataformas/tiktok-login-page.png)
*Figure 4.1: TikTok for Business login page*

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  If you use a pop-up blocker, allow pop-ups from SaleAds.ai
</Callout>
</Step>

<Step>
### Sign in to TikTok

In the pop-up window:

1. Sign in with your TikTok Business account
2. If you have multiple accounts, select the correct one

![TikTok for Business login](/images/screenshots/02-conectar-plataformas/tiktok-login.png)
*Figure 4.2: TikTok for Business login view showing email and phone options*

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  Use the TikTok Business account that has access to Business Center and Ads Manager.
</Callout>
</Step>

<Step>
### Authorize Permissions

TikTok will ask you to authorize several permissions. Here's what I need them for:

| Permission | What I use it for |
|------------|-------------------|
| **View your Business Center information** | Access your ad accounts |
| **Manage campaigns** | Create, edit, and pause video campaigns on your behalf |
| **Read metrics** | Show ad performance in your dashboard |
| **Manage creatives** | Upload videos and configure ad formats |

1. Review the requested permissions
2. Click **"Authorize"** or **"Accept"**
3. **Important:** Make sure the correct Business Center is selected

> **Warning:** If you don't authorize all permissions, I won't be able to manage your campaigns correctly.
</Step>

<Step>
### Confirm Connection

Once authorized:

1. The pop-up window will close automatically
2. You'll return to SaleAds.ai
3. You'll see the message: **"Successfully Connected!"**
</Step>
</Steps>

## TikTok Account Hierarchy

It's important to understand how TikTok organizes accounts:

### TikTok Business Center
- This is the highest organizational level
- Here you manage users, assets, and billing
- One Business Center can have multiple ad accounts

### TikTok Ads Manager
- This is where campaigns are created and managed
- Each account has its own budget and configuration
- Inherits users and permissions from Business Center

<Callout type="info" title="Key difference">
  When you connect TikTok with SaleAds.ai, you select the Business Center first, and then choose which specific ad account to use.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

### "No ad accounts found"

**Possible causes:**
- Your TikTok account is personal, not Business
- You haven't created an advertising account within TikTok Ads Manager
- The advertising account is suspended or pending review

**Solution:** Make sure you have an active TikTok Ads Manager account. If you don't have one, you can create it at [ads.tiktok.com](https://ads.tiktok.com). The creation process takes a few minutes, and TikTok may take up to 24 hours to activate new accounts.

### "Permission denied"

**Possible causes:**
- Your TikTok Business account doesn't have administrator permissions in Ads Manager
- Another user is the primary administrator and hasn't granted you sufficient access

**Solution:** Verify that your TikTok Business account has administrator or operator access to Ads Manager. If you're part of a team, ask the primary administrator to assign you appropriate permissions.

### The authorization fails or window closes

**Possible causes:**
- Unstable internet connection
- Browser blocked the pop-up window
- Third-party cookies disabled in your browser

**Solution:** Don't worry. Try again from step 1, making sure your browser allows pop-ups and third-party cookies for TikTok. If the problem persists, try in incognito mode or another browser.
