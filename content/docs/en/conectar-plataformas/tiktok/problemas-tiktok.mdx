This guide covers the most frequent errors when connecting and configuring TikTok Ads in SaleAds.ai.

Don't worry, most are resolved in minutes. I'll explain how to fix them step by step.

---

## Connection Problems

### "Authorization Error"

**Message:** `We couldn't authorize access to your TikTok Ads account. Please try again.`

**Possible causes:**

1. **Permissions not granted:** You didn't accept all requested permissions during the OAuth process
2. **Session expired:** Your TikTok session expired during the process
3. **Pop-up blocker:** The authorization window was blocked
4. **Wrong account:** You signed in with a TikTok account that doesn't have Business Center

**Solutions:**

**Solution 1: Retry with all permissions**
1. Click **"Retry"**
2. Make sure to accept ALL permissions TikTok requests
3. Don't close the pop-up window until you complete the process

**Solution 2: Check pop-ups**
1. Check if your browser blocked a pop-up window
2. Allow pop-ups for saleads.ai
3. Try again

**Solution 3: Verify TikTok account**
1. Make sure you're signing in with the correct Business account
2. If you have multiple accounts, sign out of all and use only the correct one
3. Verify this account has access to Business Center

---

### "No ad accounts found"

**Message:** `We couldn't find any TikTok Ads accounts associated with this account.`

**Causes:**

1. The TikTok account doesn't have a Business Center created
2. You haven't created an advertising account within TikTok Ads Manager
3. The advertising account is suspended or pending review

**Solutions:**

**Solution 1: Create Business Center and ad account**
1. Go to [business.tiktok.com](https://business.tiktok.com)
2. Create a Business Center
3. Within Business Center, create a new ad account
4. Wait for approval (may take up to 24-48 hours)
5. Return to SaleAds.ai and try connecting again

**Solution 2: Verify account status**
1. Go to [ads.tiktok.com](https://ads.tiktok.com)
2. Verify the account is active (not suspended)
3. If under review, wait for TikTok's approval

---

### "Permission denied"

**Message:** `You don't have sufficient permissions to access this account.`

**Causes:**

1. Your role in Business Center is "Analyst" or "Finance" (you need "Operator" or "Admin")
2. You don't have access assigned to the specific ad account

**Solution:**

1. Contact the Business Center administrator
2. Request they assign you the **"Operator"** or **"Admin"** role
3. Make sure you have access to the specific ad account you want to connect

---

### TikTok window doesn't open

**Causes:**
- Pop-up blocker active
- Browser extensions interfering
- Incompatible browser
- Third-party cookies disabled

**Solution:**
1. Disable pop-up blocker for saleads.ai
2. Enable third-party cookies in your browser
3. Try in incognito/private mode
4. Use updated Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
5. Disable extensions temporarily

## Business Center Issues

### "Business Center not found"

**Causes:**

1. You haven't created a Business Center
2. You didn't give access to Business Center during authorization
3. The Business Center is in another country/region

**Solutions:**

**Solution 1: Create Business Center**
1. Go to [business.tiktok.com](https://business.tiktok.com)
2. Click "Create Business Center"
3. Complete the required information
4. Wait for confirmation

**Solution 2: Reconnect with all permissions**
1. Disconnect TikTok in SaleAds.ai
2. Reconnect
3. During authorization, make sure to select **ALL** Business Centers

---

### "I don't have administrator role"

**Cause:** Your user has a limited role in Business Center.

**Solution:**

1. Go to [business.tiktok.com](https://business.tiktok.com)
2. Go to **"Users"**
3. Verify your current role
4. If you're not an admin, contact the Business Center owner

## Billing Problems

### "Payment method declined"

**Causes:**

* Card not enabled for international purchases
* Insufficient funds
* Bank blocked the transaction ("bank block")
* Billing address doesn't match card

**Solution:**

1. **Contact your bank:** Explain that you'll be making recurring payments to "TikTok". Many banks mark TikTok charges as suspicious due to the nature of multiple small rapid charges.

2. **Verify address:** Make sure the billing address in TikTok matches exactly with your card.

3. **Try another card:** If possible, use a business credit card instead of personal debit.

<Callout type="warn" title="TikTok's 'Bank Block'">
  Many banks automatically mark payments to TikTok as "high risk" or "suspicious" because the spending pattern (multiple small charges in rapid succession when thresholds are hit) resembles card testing fraud.

  **You must call your bank BEFORE scaling and:**
  1. Inform them you'll make recurring payments to "TikTok"
  2. Request they explicitly authorize transactions to TikTok
  3. Confirm your card is enabled for international purchases
</Callout>

---

### "Campaigns paused due to payment issues"

**Cause:** A payment failed (threshold reached but card was rejected).

**Consequences:**
* Campaigns pause immediately
* Algorithm learning phase resets
* CPM may be higher when resuming

**Solution:**

1. Go to [ads.tiktok.com](https://ads.tiktok.com)
2. Go to **"Payments"**
3. Resolve the outstanding balance
4. Update or add a valid payment method
5. Wait for reactivation (usually immediate)

**Prevention:**
* Set up a backup payment method
* Maintain sufficient balance/credit on the card
* Communicate with your bank before scaling spend

---

### "Cannot change currency"

**Cause:** Currency is selected when creating the account and cannot be changed.

**Solution:**

You must create a new TikTok Ads account with the correct currency:
1. Create a new Business Center
2. Create a new ad account
3. Select the correct currency during creation
4. Set up the new account in SaleAds.ai

<Callout type="warn" title="Important">
  You cannot transfer campaigns or history between TikTok Ads accounts. You'll need to start fresh in the new account.
</Callout>

## TikTok Pixel Issues

### "Pixel not firing"

**Causes:**

* Code not installed correctly
* Event not configured
* Conflicts with ad blockers

**Solutions:**

**Verify installation:**
1. Install **TikTok Pixel Helper** Chrome extension
2. Visit your website
3. Verify the pixel is active
4. Complete a test conversion
5. Confirm the event fires

**Verify event:**
1. In TikTok Ads Manager, go to **"Assets"** → **"Events"**
2. Verify the event is configured
3. Confirm the event name matches

---

### "Events not appearing in dashboard"

**Causes:**

* Normal reporting delay (may take up to 24 hours)
* Low Event Match Quality
* Users with ad blockers

**Solution:**

1. **Wait for reporting time:** First events may take time to appear
2. **Implement Events API:** Use server-side tracking for better coverage
3. **Verify with TikTok Pixel Helper**

---

### "Low Event Match Quality"

**Cause:** TikTok cannot effectively match your events with users.

**Solutions:**

1. **Send user data:** Include hashed email and phone in events
2. **Use Events API:** Server-side tracking significantly improves matching
3. **Configure Advanced Matching:** Enable advanced matching in pixel settings

## Disconnect and Reconnect

If no solution works, you can completely disconnect and start over.

### Steps to Disconnect

1. Go to **Connected Accounts**
2. In TikTok, click **"Edit Configuration"**
3. Click **"Disconnect TikTok account"**
4. Confirm: **"Are you sure?"** → **"Disconnect"**
5. You'll see: **"TikTok account disconnected successfully"**

### After Disconnecting

- Your account returns to "Not connected" status
- You must reconnect and configure everything again
- Existing strategies may be affected

---

## Revoke Permissions from TikTok

If you want to completely remove SaleAds.ai access:

1. Go to [business.tiktok.com](https://business.tiktok.com)
2. Go to **"Settings"** → **"Security"**
3. Look for connected third-party applications
4. Find **SaleAds** and remove access
5. Confirm the removal

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  After revoking permissions, you'll need to reconnect from scratch if you want to use SaleAds.ai again.
</Callout>

## General Verifications

If you have persistent problems with TikTok Ads:

1. **Verify in TikTok:** Go to [business.tiktok.com](https://business.tiktok.com) and confirm you can access
2. **Try another browser:** Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge
3. **Clear cache:** Clear browser cookies and cache
4. **Incognito mode:** Try in private window
