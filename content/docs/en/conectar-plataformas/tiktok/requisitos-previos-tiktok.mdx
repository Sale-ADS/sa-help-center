## 1. TikTok Account

You need a TikTok account to start. If you already have a personal account, you can use it to create your Business account. If you don't have one, create it first.

**If you already have an account:** Make sure you can sign in correctly in the TikTok app.

**If you don't have an account:**

1. Download the TikTok app from App Store or Google Play
2. Create an account using your email, phone, or Google/Facebook account
3. Complete your basic profile

---

## 2. TikTok Business Account

TikTok Business is the entry point to access advertising tools. You need to convert your personal account to Business or create a new Business account.

### Convert Personal Account to Business

1. Open the TikTok app
2. Go to your profile → Menu (☰) → **Settings and privacy**
3. Select **"Account"** → **"Switch to Business Account"**
4. Select the category that best describes your business
5. Complete your business profile information

### Create Business Account from Scratch

1. Go to [business.tiktok.com](https://business.tiktok.com)
2. Click **"Create account"**
3. Enter your business email and create a password
4. Verify your email
5. Complete your business information:
   * Business name
   * Country/region
   * Time zone
   * Currency (important: cannot be changed later)

<Callout type="warn" title="Critical">
  The time zone and currency **cannot be changed** after creating the account. Choose carefully based on where your business operates.
</Callout>

## 3. TikTok Business Center

Business Center is TikTok's organizational control hub. It's similar to Meta's Business Manager: here you manage ad accounts, team members, pixels, and billing.

### Create Business Center

1. Go to [business.tiktok.com](https://business.tiktok.com)
2. Sign in with your TikTok Business account
3. Select **"Create Business Center"**
4. Complete the information:
   * Business Center name (usually your company name)
   * Industry
   * Contact information

### Understand TikTok's Hierarchy

TikTok organizes accounts in levels:

| Level | Description | Function |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Business Center** | Organizational control hub | Manage users, assets, billing |
| **Ad Accounts** | Accounts where campaigns run | Each has its own budget and billing |
| **Campaigns** | Ad groups with an objective | Set optimization objective |

<Callout type="info" title="Key difference">
  Business Center is for organizational management (users, assets, billing). Ads Manager is for daily operations (create campaigns, review metrics).
</Callout>

## 4. TikTok Ads Manager

You need to have at least one TikTok Ads Manager account created within your Business Center.

### Create Ad Account

1. In Business Center, go to **"Assets"** → **"Ad Accounts"**
2. Click **"Add ad account"**
3. Select **"Create new ad account"**
4. Complete:
   * Account name (e.g., "My Company - TikTok Ads")
   * Time zone
   * Currency
   * Account type (Business)
5. Accept the terms of service

<Callout type="info" title="Approval time">
  New ad accounts are generally approved in less than 24 hours, though TikTok may take up to 48 hours in some cases.
</Callout>

## 5. Payment Method

TikTok requires setting up a payment method before launching campaigns. Without this, your ads won't be published.

### Available Payment Options

Options vary by country:

| Method | Payment Type | Activation Time |
|--------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Credit/debit card** | Automatic or Prepay | Immediate |
| **PayPal** | Automatic | Immediate (where available) |
| **Bank transfer** | Prepay | 1-3 business days |
| **Monthly invoicing** | Monthly | Requires approval |

### Set Up Automatic Payments (Recommended)

1. In Ads Manager, click your profile picture (top right)
2. Go to **"Payments"**
3. Select **"Add payment method"**
4. Enter your card details
5. Accept the terms

### Understanding the Payment Threshold

TikTok uses a **payment threshold** system that grows automatically:

- You start with a low threshold (e.g., $20-50 USD)
- When your spending reaches the threshold, your card is charged
- As you pay on time, the threshold automatically increases
- Typical progression: $20 → $50 → $200 → $350 → $500 → $750 → $1,000+

<Callout type="info" title="Example">
  If your threshold is $50 and you spend $60, $50 is charged immediately and the remaining $10 accumulates. If you spend less than $50 in the month, everything is charged on the 1st of the following month.
</Callout>

## 6. Roles and Permissions

To connect TikTok with SaleAds.ai, you need appropriate permissions in the Business Center.

### Access Levels

| Role | Capabilities | Appropriate for |
|------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Admin** | Full control: assets, team, billing | Owners, senior managers |
| **Operator** | Manage assets and campaigns | Account managers, media buyers |
| **Analyst** | View only: reports and campaigns | Stakeholders, executives |
| **Finance** | Access to billing | Accounting team |

### Verify Your Role

1. In Business Center, go to **"Users"**
2. Look for your email in the list
3. Confirm you have **"Admin"** or **"Operator"** role

## Common Issues

<details>

<summary>I can't create a TikTok Business account</summary>

Make sure:
- You have a valid TikTok account
- You haven't violated TikTok's policies previously
- You're using a valid business email (not temporary)

</details>

<details>

<summary>My account has been under review for a long time</summary>

New account verifications can take up to 48 hours. If after that time it's still under review, contact TikTok support.

</details>

<details>

<summary>I don't have administrator access</summary>

Contact the Business Center owner and request they assign you the administrator role in **"Users"**.

</details>

<details>

<summary>My card is rejected</summary>

Possible causes:
- Card not enabled for international purchases
- Insufficient funds
- Bank blocked the transaction (TikTok is sometimes marked as "high risk" by banks)
- Billing address doesn't match

Solution: Contact your bank and authorize transactions to "TikTok", or try another card.

</details>
