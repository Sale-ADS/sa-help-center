---

## Step-by-step to link WhatsApp

1. Log in to [ads.tiktok.com](https://ads.tiktok.com) with your TikTok Ads account.
2. Open your ad account settings.
3. Find the option to connect contact or messaging channels.
4. Select **WhatsApp** as the channel.
5. Enter your number including country code.
6. Complete number verification when TikTok prompts you.
7. Save changes and confirm the status shows as connected.

---

## Important recommendations

- Use an active WhatsApp Business number.
- Make sure the number can receive messages or verification codes.
- If you manage multiple ad accounts, double-check you linked the number in the correct one.

---

## Common issues

### "Verification code does not arrive"

- Confirm the number and country code are correct.
- Wait a few minutes and request a new code.
- Check carrier restrictions or anti-spam filtering.

### "Number is already in use"

- The number may already be linked to another account or previous setup.
- Unlink it from the old account first, or use another number.
