Billing configuration is essential for your campaigns to be published. TikTok Ads requires a valid payment method before launching any ads. In this section, I explain how to verify and manage your billing, including TikTok-specific aspects like the growing threshold system.

**You have total control** of your payment methods and billing configuration.

---

## Understanding TikTok Ads Billing

TikTok Ads primarily uses **automatic payments** in most countries:

### How Automatic Payments Work

1. Your ads start showing immediately
2. Costs accumulate daily
3. TikTok automatically charges when:
   - You reach your **payment threshold** (starts at $20-50), or
   - The **billing date** arrives (weekly or monthly)

<Callout type="info" title="Example">
  If your threshold is $50 and you spend $60, $50 is charged immediately and the remaining $10 accumulates. If you spend less than $50 in the period, everything is charged on the billing date.
</Callout>

### TikTok's Payment Threshold

The payment threshold is the accumulated amount that triggers an automatic charge. TikTok has a unique system where the threshold **automatically grows** as you demonstrate payment history:

| Payment History | Typical Threshold |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| New account | $20-50 USD |
| After on-time payments | $200 USD |
| Established account | $350-500 USD |
| High-volume account | $750-1,000+ USD |

**Typical progression:** $20 → $50 → $200 → $350 → $500 → $750 → $1,000+

TikTok automatically increases your threshold based on:
- Consistent spending
- Successful payments without declines
- Stable account behavior

## Verify Billing Configuration

### Step 1: Access Billing

1. Go to [ads.tiktok.com](https://ads.tiktok.com)
2. Click your profile picture (top right)
3. Select **"Payments"**

### Step 2: Verify Payment Method

In the **"Payment methods"** section verify:

| Element | Correct Status |
|---------|----------------|
| Primary method | ✅ Active and valid |
| Expiration date | ✅ Current |
| Billing address | ✅ Matches card |

### Step 3: Verify Configuration

Confirm:

- Billing country: Correct (affects available methods)
- Currency: Correct (cannot be changed)
- Time zone: Correct (cannot be changed)

## Available Payment Options

Options vary by country:

| Method | Availability | Activation Time |
|--------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Credit/debit card** | Almost all countries | Immediate |
| **PayPal** | Select countries | Immediate |
| **Bank transfer** | Some countries | 1-3 business days |
| **Prepay/Manual** | Where available | Immediate after deposit |
| **Monthly invoicing** | Qualified accounts | Requires approval |

### Credit/Debit Card (Most Common)

**Accepted:** Visa, Mastercard, American Express (by country)

**Set up:**
1. In Ads Manager, click your profile → **"Payments"**
2. Click **"Add payment method"**
3. Select **"Credit card"**
4. Enter your card details
5. Accept the terms

<Callout type="warn" title="Critical: TikTok's 'Bank Block'">
  Many banks automatically mark payments to TikTok as "high risk" or "suspicious" because the spending pattern (multiple small rapid charges when thresholds are hit) resembles card testing fraud.

  **You must call your bank BEFORE scaling and:**
  1. Inform them you'll make recurring payments to "TikTok"
  2. Request they explicitly authorize transactions to TikTok
  3. Confirm your card is enabled for international purchases
</Callout>

### Prepay/Manual (Where Available)

In some countries you can use manual payments:

1. Deposit funds in advance to your account
2. Ads show until balance runs out
3. Ideal for strict budget control

**Note:** Not available in all countries.

## Add Backup Payment Method

I recommend setting up a secondary payment method to prevent campaigns from pausing:

1. Go to **"Payments"** → **"Payment methods"**
2. Add a second method (different card)
3. TikTok will automatically use it if the primary fails

**Benefits:**
- Prevents campaigns from pausing due to payment failures
- Protection against expired cards
- Continuity of your campaigns 24/7

## Monthly Invoicing (For Qualified Accounts)

Some accounts may qualify for monthly invoicing:

### Typical Requirements

- Account active for several months
- Consistent payment history
- Significant monthly spend (generally $10,000+ USD)
- Registered business

### How to Apply

1. Contact your TikTok representative or support
2. Request change to monthly invoicing
3. Complete credit verification if necessary
4. Wait for approval

<Callout type="info" title="Benefit">
  With monthly invoicing you receive a consolidated invoice and have time to pay (typically Net 30 or Net 45).
</Callout>

## Resolve Payment Issues

### "Payment method declined"

**Common causes and solutions:**

| Cause | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Insufficient funds | Check balance or use another card |
| Expired card | Update expiration date |
| Incorrect address | Correct billing address |
| Bank block | **Call your bank** to authorize "TikTok" |
| International purchases | Enable international purchases |
| Daily limit reached | Request limit increase from bank |

### "Account suspended for billing"

If your account was suspended for payment issues:

1. Go to [ads.tiktok.com](https://ads.tiktok.com)
2. Resolve the outstanding balance
3. Update or add a valid payment method
4. Wait for reactivation (usually immediate)

**Impact of suspension:**
- Campaigns pause immediately
- Algorithm learning phase may reset
- CPM may be temporarily higher when resuming

## View Invoices and Payments

### Transaction History

1. Go to **"Payments"** → **"Transactions"**
2. You'll see all charges made
3. You can download invoices in PDF

### Set Up Notifications

To receive billing alerts:

1. Go to **"Settings"** → **"Notifications"**
2. Configure alerts for:
   - Payment threshold reached
   - Payment method issues
   - Invoices generated

## Change Billing Configuration

### What you CAN change:

- ✅ Primary payment method
- ✅ Add backup methods
- ✅ Configure notifications

### What you CANNOT change:

- ❌ Currency (selected when creating account)
- ❌ Time zone (selected when creating account)
- ❌ Primary billing country

<Callout type="warn" title="Critical">
  If you need to change currency or time zone, you must create a new TikTok Ads account. These values are not modifiable after creation.
</Callout>
