This guide covers the most frequent errors when connecting and configuring Meta Ads in SaleAds.ai.

Don't worry, most of them are resolved in minutes. I will explain how to fix them step by step.

---

## Connection Problems

### "Connection Failed"

**Message:** `We couldn't connect your Meta account. Please try again.`

**Possible causes:**

1. **Permissions not granted:** You didn't accept all the requested permissions
2. **Session expired:** Your Facebook session expired during the process
3. **Pop-up blocker:** The authorization window was blocked
4. **Temporary Meta issue:** Meta servers are not responding

**Solutions:**

**Solution 1: Retry**
1. Click **"Retry"**
2. Make sure to accept ALL permissions
3. Don't close the pop-up window until you're done

**Solution 2: Check pop-ups**
1. Check if your browser blocked a pop-up window
2. Allow pop-ups for saleads.ai
3. Try again

**Solution 3: Clear session**
1. Log out of Facebook in another tab
2. Log out of SaleAds.ai
3. Log back in to both
4. Try connecting again

Following these steps will resolve the issue quickly.

---

### "Unexpected callback state"

**Message:** `Unexpected callback state. Please try again.`

**Cause:** Error during the OAuth authorization process.

**Solution:**
1. Close the current window/tab
2. Return to the SaleAds.ai Dashboard
3. Go to Connected Accounts
4. Try connecting again

This problem is resolved in a couple of clicks.

---

### The Facebook window doesn't open

**Causes:**
- Active pop-up blocker
- Browser extensions interfering
- Incompatible browser

**Solution:**
1. Disable the pop-up blocker
2. Try in incognito/private mode
3. Use an updated version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
4. Temporarily disable extensions

## Business Manager Problems

### "No Business Managers found"

**Message:** `We couldn't find any Business Manager in your Meta account.`

**Causes:**

1. You don't have a Business Manager created
2. You didn't select the Business Managers during authorization
3. Your Facebook account is not associated with any Business Manager

**Solutions:**

**Solution 1: Create a Business Manager**
1. Go to [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com)
2. Create a new Business Manager
3. Return to SaleAds.ai
4. Disconnect and reconnect Meta

**Solution 2: Reconnect with all permissions**
1. Disconnect the Meta account
2. Reconnect
3. During authorization, select ALL Business Managers

---

### "Error loading Business Managers"

**Cause:** Communication issue with the Meta API.

**Solution:**
1. Click **"Retry"**
2. Wait 30 seconds and try again
3. If it persists, try in 5-10 minutes
4. Check Meta's service status at [metastatus.com](https://metastatus.com)

---

## Ad Account Problems

### "No ad accounts found"

**Message:** `No ad accounts were found for the selected Business Manager.`

**Causes:**
- The Business Manager has no ad accounts
- You don't have permissions on existing accounts

**Solutions:**

**Solution 1: Create a new account**
1. Click **"Create New Ad Account"**
2. Fill in the details
3. The account will be created in your Business Manager

**Solution 2: Request access**
1. Contact the Business Manager administrator
2. Request an admin role on the ad account
3. Return to SaleAds.ai and refresh

---

### "Error creating ad account"

**Causes:**
- Account limit reached
- Restrictions on your Facebook account
- Temporary Meta error

**Solution:**
1. Check if your Facebook account has restrictions
2. Check the account limit in your Business Manager
3. Create the account manually in Business Manager
4. Return to SaleAds.ai to select it

---

## Problems with Optional Assets

### "No pages found"

**Cause:** The page is not added to the Business Manager.

**Solution:**
1. Go to [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com)
2. Settings → Accounts → Pages
3. Add your page
4. Return to SaleAds.ai and refresh

---

### "No pixels found"

**Cause:** No pixels have been created in the ad account.

**Solution:**
1. Click **"Create New Pixel"** in SaleAds.ai
2. Or create the pixel manually in Events Manager

---

### "No Instagram accounts linked"

**Cause:** Your Instagram account is not connected to the Facebook Page.

**Solution:**
1. Open Instagram → Settings → Account
2. Linked Accounts → Facebook
3. Select your Facebook Page
4. Return to SaleAds.ai and refresh

<Callout type="warn" title="Requirement">
  The Instagram account must be a business or creator account.
</Callout>

## Disconnect and Reconnect

If no solution works, you can disconnect completely and start over.

### Steps to Disconnect

1. Go to **Connected Accounts**
2. In Meta Ads, click **"Edit Configuration"**
3. Click **"Disconnect Meta account"**
4. Confirm: **"Are you sure?"** → **"Disconnect"**
5. You will see: **"Meta account disconnected successfully"**

### After Disconnecting

- Your account returns to the "Not connected" status
- You must reconnect and configure everything again
- Existing strategies may be affected

---

## Revoke Permissions from Facebook

If you want to completely remove SaleAds.ai access:

1. Go to [facebook.com/settings](https://facebook.com/settings)
2. Security and Login → Apps and Websites
3. Search for SaleAds
4. Click "Remove"
5. Confirm the removal

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  After revoking permissions, you will need to reconnect from scratch if you want to use SaleAds.ai again.
</Callout>

## Save Errors

### "Error saving configuration"

**Causes:**
- Internet connection issue
- Session expired
- Server error

**Solution:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try saving again
3. If it persists, reload the page and try again

---

### "Validation failed"

**Message:** `Please select a Business Manager and an Ad Account to continue.`

**Cause:** Required fields not completed.

**Solution:**
1. Make sure you have selected a Business Manager
2. Make sure you have selected an Ad Account
3. These two fields are required

---

## Error When Disconnecting

### "Error disconnecting Meta account"

**Solution:**

1. Wait a few seconds and try disconnecting again
2. Reload the page and try again
3. If it fails, [contact support](/en/docs/solucion-problemas/contactar-soporte)

---

## General Checks

If you have persistent problems with Meta Ads:

1. **Check Meta:** Go to [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com) and confirm you can access it
2. **Try another browser:** Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge
3. **Clear cache:** Clear your browser cache
4. **Incognito mode:** Try in a private window

---

## Does the Problem Persist?

If after trying all the solutions the problem continues:

1. Take a screenshot of the error
2. Write down the steps you took
3. [Contact support](/en/docs/solucion-problemas/contactar-soporte) with this information

### Useful Information for Support

- The exact error message
- When the problem started
- Browser and version you are using
- Whether you can access [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com) normally
