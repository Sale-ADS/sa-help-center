After setting up the Business Manager and the Ad Account (required), you can configure additional assets that enhance your strategies.

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  All fields in this section are optional. You can configure them now or later.
</Callout>


**You have full control** over which assets to set up based on your needs.

---

## Assets Overview

| Asset                  | What is it for?                  | When to set it up?          |
| ---------------------- | -------------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| **Facebook Page**      | Identity of your ads             | Required                    |
| **Facebook Pixel**     | Measure conversions on your site | If you have a website       |
| **WhatsApp Business**  | Ads directing to WhatsApp        | If you use business WhatsApp|
| **Instagram Account**  | Show ads on Instagram            | Required                    |
| **Website URL**        | Destination for your ads         | If you have a website       |

---

## 1. Facebook Page

### Why is it important?

The Facebook Page is the identity from which your ads are published. Users will see the page's name and photo.

> **Although it is "optional" in the setup, it is required to publish any ad.**

### Select a Page

1. In the **"Facebook Page"** field, click to open the list
2. You will see the pages linked to your Business Manager
3. Select your business page

Choose the page that best represents your business.

### I Don't See My Page

**Possible causes:**

* The page is not added to the Business Manager
* You don't have an admin role on the page

**Solution:**

1. Go to [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com)
2. Settings → Accounts → Pages → Add
3. Add your page to the Business Manager
4. Return to SaleAds.ai and refresh

---

## 2. Facebook Pixel

### What is the Pixel?

It is a tracking code installed on your website to:

* Measure conversions (purchases, sign-ups, etc.)
* Optimize ad delivery
* Create remarketing audiences

### Select an Existing Pixel

1. In the **"Facebook Pixel"** field, click to open the list
2. You will see the pixels available in your ad account
3. Select the pixel installed on your website

### Create a New Pixel

If you don't have a pixel:

1. Click **"Create New Pixel"**
2. Enter a name (e.g., "My Store Pixel")
3. Click **"Create Pixel"**
4. The pixel is created and automatically selected

If you don't have a pixel, I can create one for you in seconds.

<Callout type="warn" title="Important">
  After creating the pixel, you must install it on your website. Check Meta's documentation for installation instructions.
</Callout>


### I Don't Have a Website

If you don't have a website, you can skip this field. Your ads will work normally, but you won't be able to measure web conversions.

## 3. WhatsApp Business

<YoutubeEmbed videoId="wzQdu16Sey0" title="How to set up WhatsApp Business?" />

### When Do I Need WhatsApp Business?

Set up WhatsApp if you want to create strategies where users:

* Click to send a message to WhatsApp
* Receive automatic replies
* Start conversations with your business

### Select a WhatsApp Account

1. In the **"WhatsApp Business"** field, click to open the list
2. You will see the linked WhatsApp Business accounts
3. Select the account you want to use

### I Don't See Any Account

**Message:** `No WhatsApp Business accounts were found.`

**This means that:**

* You don't have WhatsApp Business set up in your Business Manager
* Or you didn't link it during the Meta connection

**Solution:**

1. Go to [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com)
2. Settings → Accounts → WhatsApp Accounts
3. Set up your WhatsApp Business account
4. Return to SaleAds.ai

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  Setting up WhatsApp Business requires a verified phone number and may take several days.
</Callout>

## 4. Instagram Account

<YoutubeEmbed videoId="Gfadl4_1GT0" title="How to link Instagram to your ad account?" />

### Why Connect Instagram?

If you have a business Instagram account, connecting it allows you to:

* Show ads in the Instagram feed and stories
* Use the name and photo of your Instagram account
* Access Instagram analytics

### Select an Instagram Account

1. In the **"Instagram Account"** field, click to open the list
2. You will see the Instagram accounts linked to your Facebook Pages
3. Select your Instagram account

### I Don't See My Instagram Account

**Message:** `No Instagram accounts are linked to your Facebook Pages.`

**To link Instagram to your Facebook Page:**

1. Open the Instagram app
2. Go to Settings → Account → Linked Accounts
3. Select Facebook
4. Choose the correct Facebook Page
5. Return to SaleAds.ai and refresh

<Callout type="warn" title="Requirement">
  Your Instagram account must be a "Business account" or "Creator account", not a personal account.
</Callout>

## 5. Website URL

### What is it for?

Your website URL is used as:

* Default destination for your ads
* Domain for Pixel verification
* Suggested landing page

### Enter URL

1. In the **"Website URL"** field, type the address
2. You must include `https://` or `http://`
3. Example: `https://www.mystore.com`

### Validation Errors

| Error              | Cause               | Solution                  |
| ------------------ | ------------------- | ------------------------- |
| "Invalid URL"      | Incorrect format    | Include https://          |
| "Invalid protocol" | Doesn't start with http | Use https://example.com |

## Complete Setup

Once you have configured the assets you need:

1. Review the configuration summary
2. Click **"Next"** to go to the final review
3. On the review screen, verify everything is correct
4. Click **"Complete Setup"**

### Success Message

You will see: **"Meta Ads setup completed successfully!"**

Your status will change to **"Configured"** and you will be ready to create strategies.

3. Modify the necessary assets
4. Save the changes
