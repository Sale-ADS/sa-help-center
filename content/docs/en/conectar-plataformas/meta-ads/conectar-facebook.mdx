This is the first step to launch advertising strategies. Connecting your Meta account allows me to create and manage ads on your behalf. **You have full control** over the permissions you grant.

<Callout type="warn" title="Important">
  This is the most critical point of the process. Take your time and follow each step carefully.
</Callout>


---

## Access the Settings

1. In the Dashboard, go to the side menu
2. Click **"Connected Accounts"**
3. You will see the **Meta Ads** section with the connect button

---

## Current Connection Status

On the Connected Accounts screen you will see one of these statuses:

| Status                                  | Meaning                                        | Action Required               |
| --------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| **Not connected**                       | You haven't linked your Meta account           | Click "Connect Account"       |
| **Connected - Setup pending**           | You connected Meta but need to select assets   | Complete the setup            |
| **Configured**                          | Everything is ready to launch strategies       | None                          |

## Connection Process

<GoogleDriveEmbed fileId="1xpbzV8Lt9eiF4tg6PCwpbdh3Fjv3UICQ" title="How to connect your Meta account?" />



<Steps>
<Step>
### Start Connection

1. Click the **"Connect with Meta"** button
2. A Facebook pop-up window will open

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  If you use a pop-up blocker, allow pop-ups from SaleAds.ai
</Callout>
</Step>
<Step>
### Log In to Facebook

In the pop-up window:

1. Enter your Facebook email and password
2. Click **"Log in"**

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  Use the Facebook account that has access to your business's Business Manager.
</Callout>
</Step>
<Step>
### Authorize Permissions

Facebook will ask you to authorize several permissions. Here is what I need them for:

| Permission                | What I use it for                 |
| ------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Manage pages              | Publish ads from your page        |
| Manage ads                | Create and manage campaigns       |
| Read analytics            | Show metrics for your ads         |
| Business Manager access   | Use your ad accounts              |

1. Review the requested permissions
2. Click **"Continue"** or **"Accept"** on each screen
3. **Important:** Select **ALL** the pages and accounts you want to grant access to

> **Warning:** If you don't select all assets, you won't be able to use them in SaleAds.ai later.
</Step>
<Step>
### Confirm Connection

Once authorized:

1. The pop-up window will close automatically
2. You will return to SaleAds.ai
3. You will see the message: **"Successfully Connected!"**
</Step>
</Steps>

## Account Creation

Use this section if you are missing a component to proceed with Meta Ads.

### Create a Business Manager (Meta Business)

If no Business Manager appears when setting up Meta, it is usually because one doesn't exist yet.

1. Go to [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com)
2. Click **"Create account"**
3. Fill in your company details
4. Verify your email (if Meta requests it)
5. Wait 2-5 minutes
6. Return to SaleAds.ai
7. **Disconnect and reconnect Meta** so it appears in the list

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  If a Business Manager already exists but you don't see it, reconnect Meta and select **ALL** Business Managers in the permissions.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

### The pop-up window doesn't open

**Possible causes:**

* Active pop-up blocker
* Incompatible browser

**Solution:**

1. Disable the pop-up blocker for saleads.ai
2. Use an updated version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
3. Try in an incognito window

### Error: "Connection Failed"

**Possible causes:**

* Facebook session expired
* Permissions not granted correctly
* Temporary Meta issue

**Solution:**

1. Click **"Retry"**
2. Make sure to accept ALL permissions
3. Try again in 5 minutes if the problem persists
4. If it continues, [contact support](/en/docs/solucion-problemas/contactar-soporte)

### I don't see my pages/accounts

**Possible causes:**

* You didn't select all assets during authorization
* You don't have the correct permissions in Business Manager

**Solution:**

1. Disconnect the account (see [Edit/Disconnect](problemas-meta))
2. Reconnect
3. During authorization, select **ALL** assets

### Message: "Unexpected callback state"

**Cause:** Error in the Facebook authorization process.

**Solution:**

1. Close the current window
2. Return to Connected Accounts
3. Try connecting again

## Permissions We Request

These are all the permissions I need and why:

| Permission                  | Usage                              |
| --------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| `pages_manage_ads`          | Create ads from your page          |
| `pages_read_engagement`     | Read interactions on your ads      |
| `ads_management`            | Create and manage campaigns        |
| `ads_read`                  | Read performance metrics           |
| `business_management`       | Access Business Manager            |
| `instagram_basic`           | Connect Instagram account          |
| `instagram_content_publish` | Publish ads on Instagram           |

> **Privacy:** I will never publish content without your explicit authorization. I only create ads when you click "Launch". You have full control.
