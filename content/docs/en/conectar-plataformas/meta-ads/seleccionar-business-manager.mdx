Business Manager is the foundation on which all your advertising campaigns on Meta are built. Selecting the right one is fundamental because it links your business in SaleAds with the pages, ad accounts, and pixels you will use for your ads. Think of it as the central office from which your entire advertising ecosystem is managed.

I will explain how to choose the right Business Manager for your ads, step by step and without complications.

---

## What is Business Manager?

Business Manager is Meta's tool where all of a company's advertising assets are managed:

* Facebook Pages
* Ad accounts
* Pixels
* Product catalogs
* Users and permissions

<Callout type="warn" title="Important">
  Each business in SaleAds.ai is linked to a single Business Manager. This decision is important because it determines which advertising assets will be available for your campaigns.
</Callout>


To make the right decision, it is helpful to understand the roles within a Business Manager. If you are an **administrator**, you have full access to all assets. If you have an **employee** or **analyst** role, you may not see all ad accounts. If you work with a team, make sure your Facebook account has administrator permissions in the Business Manager you plan to use.

---

## Access the Settings

After connecting with Facebook, you will automatically see the Meta configuration screen. If you don't see it:

1. Go to **Connected Accounts**
2. In the Meta Ads section, click **"Complete Setup"**

---

## Step 1: Select Business Manager

### View Available Business Managers

You will see a list of all the Business Managers you have access to with your Facebook account. It is normal to have more than one if you work with several companies or if a client has given you access to theirs.

Each Business Manager shows:

* **Name** of the Business Manager
* Unique **ID**
* **Status** (active/inactive)

### Choose the Right One

1. Identify your business's Business Manager
2. Click to select it
3. The Business Manager will be highlighted

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  If you have multiple Business Managers, choose the one that contains the ad account and the Facebook Page you will use for your ads. If you are not sure, it is the one with your company or main brand name.
</Callout>


### Use the Search

If you have many Business Managers:

1. Use the **"Search Business Managers..."** field
2. Type part of the name
3. The list will be filtered automatically

## I Can't Find My Business Manager

Don't worry. There are three common causes and all have a solution:

### Possible Cause 1: You didn't grant access during connection

When you connect Facebook with SaleAds, an authorization screen appears where you must select which Business Managers you grant access to. If you skipped one, it won't appear in the list.

**Solution:**

1. Return to the Connected Accounts screen
2. Disconnect the Meta account
3. Reconnect
4. During the Facebook authorization, make sure to select **ALL** Business Managers

### Possible Cause 2: You don't have an admin role

For SaleAds to access the assets of a Business Manager, you need a role with sufficient permissions (ideally administrator).

**Solution:**

1. Go to [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com)
2. Verify that you can see the Business Manager
3. If you can't, contact the administrator to have them add you with an admin role

### Possible Cause 3: The account is restricted

Meta can restrict Business Managers for violations of their advertising policies.

**Solution:**

1. Check your account status in Meta Business Suite
2. If there are restrictions, follow Meta's instructions to resolve them

---

## I Don't Have a Business Manager

If no Business Manager appears in the list, it means that:

1. You don't have a Business Manager created, or
2. You didn't grant access during the connection

### What to Do

Creating a Business Manager is free and takes less than 5 minutes. Once created, reconnect Meta so it appears in SaleAds.ai:

> [Account Creation (Business Manager and Instagram) →](conectar-facebook#creacion-de-cuentas)

---

## Confirm Selection

Once you have selected the correct Business Manager, review and proceed:

1. Verify that the name and ID are correct
2. Click **"Next"**
3. You will proceed to the Ad Account selection

---

## Change Business Manager Later

If you need to change the Business Manager later, you can do so, but keep in mind the implications:

1. Go to **Connected Accounts**
2. Click **"Edit Configuration"**
3. You can select a different Business Manager

> **Warning:** Changing the Business Manager may affect the strategies you already have configured, since the associated ad accounts and pages may be different.

---

## Common Errors

### "No Business Managers found"

**Message:** `We couldn't find any Business Manager in your Meta account.`

**Possible causes:**

1. You don't have a Business Manager created
2. You didn't grant access during authorization
3. Temporary connection issue with Meta

**Solution:**

1. Verify that you have a Business Manager at [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com)
2. If you don't have one, create it (it's free)
3. Disconnect and reconnect Meta in SaleAds.ai

### "Error loading Business Managers"

**Cause:** Temporary communication issue with the Meta API. These issues usually resolve on their own within a few minutes.

**Solution:**

1. Click **"Retry"**
2. Wait a few seconds and try again
3. If it persists, try in 5-10 minutes
