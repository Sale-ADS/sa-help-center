Before connecting your Meta Ads account with SaleAds.ai, make sure you have the following elements set up. This preparation will save you time and prevent errors.


<Callout type="warn" title="Important">
  This section describes actions you must perform **outside of SaleAds.ai**, directly on Meta's platforms.
</Callout>


---

## Checklist

Before continuing, confirm that you have:

* [ ] An active personal Facebook account
* [ ] A Facebook Page for your business
* [ ] Access to a Business Manager
* [ ] An Ad Account with an active payment method

> **Don't have one of these?** Don't worry, below I explain how to create each one.

**You have full control** of the preparation. I will guide you step by step.



---

## 1. Facebook Account

You need a personal Facebook account to manage your advertising assets.

**If you already have an account:** Make sure you can log in correctly.

**If you don't have an account:**

1. Go to [facebook.com](https://facebook.com)
2. Click "Create new account"
3. Complete the registration with your email and personal information
4. Verify your email

---

## 2. Facebook Page

The Facebook Page is your business's identity on the platform. Your ads will be displayed as posts from this page.

**If you already have a page:** Make sure you have an **Admin** role on the page.

**If you don't have a page:**

1. Log in to Facebook
2. Click the menu (☰) → **Pages**
3. Click **"Create new page"**
4. Fill in the details:
   * Page name (your business name)
   * Category (e.g., Store, Restaurant, Services)
   * Short description
5. Add a profile photo and cover image
6. Click **"Create page"**

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  Use your business's real name. This will be the identity users see in your ads.
</Callout>


With your Page ready, your ads will have the perfect identity for your business.

---

## 3. Business Manager

Business Manager is the central tool where you manage all your advertising assets: pages, ad accounts, pixels, etc.

**If you already have a Business Manager:** Go to [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com) and verify that you can access it.

**If you don't have a Business Manager:**

1. Go to [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com)
2. Click **"Create account"**
3. Fill in the details:
   * Company name
   * Your name
   * Company email
4. Click **"Submit"**
5. Verify your email

### Add Your Page to Business Manager

If your Page exists but is not in the Business Manager:

1. In Business Manager, go to **Settings** → **Accounts** → **Pages**
2. Click **"Add"**
3. Select **"Add a page"**
4. Search for your page and add it

When you see your Business Manager ready, you will understand the true value of having everything organized before connecting.

---

## 4. Ad Account

The Ad Account is where your ads are billed. You need to have an active payment method.

**If you already have an ad account:** Verify that you have a valid payment method configured.

**If you don't have an ad account:**

<Callout type="info" title="Good news">
  I can create an ad account for you during the setup process. You just need to have your Business Manager ready.
</Callout>


**If you prefer to create it manually:**

1. In Business Manager, go to **Settings** → **Accounts** → **Ad accounts**
2. Click **"Add"** → **"Create a new ad account"**
3. Fill in:
   * Account name
   * Time zone
   * Currency (important: it cannot be changed later)
4. Add a payment method:
   * Credit/debit card
   * PayPal (in some countries)



---

## 5. Payment Method (Required)

Without an active payment method, you won't be able to launch ads, even if I set up everything correctly.



### Add a Payment Method

1. In Business Manager, go to **Payment settings**
2. Click **"Add payment method"**
3. Enter your card details
4. Meta may make a small verification charge (it will be reversed)

<Callout type="warn" title="Important">
  Make sure your card has sufficient funds and is enabled for international purchases.
</Callout>


**You have full control** of your payment method and budget.

## Verify Everything is Ready

Before continuing to SaleAds.ai, verify:

1. **Log in** to [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com)
2. Go to **Settings** → **Accounts**
3. Confirm that you see:
   * At least 1 Facebook Page
   * At least 1 Ad Account (or I will create one for you)
4. If you have an Ad Account, verify you have a payment method in **Payment settings**

Verifying these points will save you time when connecting.

## Common Problems

<details>

<summary>I don't have access to my company's Business Manager</summary>

Contact the current administrator of your Business Manager and ask them to add you as an administrator. In Business Manager → Settings → People → Add people.

</details>

<details>

<summary>My Page is in another Business Manager</summary>

A page can only be in one Business Manager at a time. Options:

1. Request access in the current Business Manager
2. Request that the page be transferred to your Business Manager
3. Create a new page

Choose the option that best fits your situation.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Meta rejects my payment method</summary>

Possible causes:

* The card is not enabled for international purchases
* Insufficient funds
* The bank blocked the transaction due to suspected fraud

Solution: Contact your bank and authorize transactions to "Meta Platforms" or "Facebook".

I will explain what might be causing the problem and how to resolve it.

</details>
