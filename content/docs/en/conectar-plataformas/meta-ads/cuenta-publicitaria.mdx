The Ad Account is where Meta bills your ads and where all your campaign history is stored. Without a properly configured ad account, you won't be able to launch any strategy. This step is required, but it is simple and quick: in 1-3 minutes you will have everything ready.


<YoutubeEmbed videoId="NmEmmx25a2w" title="How to configure the payment method and security of your Facebook ad account?" />

Understanding why this step matters will help you make better decisions. The ad account is not just a technical requirement; it is the financial center of your advertising. The currency you choose, the payment method you configure, and the history you accumulate directly affect the trust Meta has in your ads and, therefore, the performance of your campaigns.

---

## What is an Ad Account?

The Ad Account is the container where:

* Your campaigns are created and managed
* Payment methods are configured
* Advertising expenses are billed
* Historical metrics are stored

<Callout type="warn" title="Important">
  Each ad account has a configured currency that **cannot be changed** after creation.
</Callout>


Choose carefully from the start. If you pick the wrong currency, you will have to create a new account. Think about what currency you want to see your reports in and receive billing in.

## Select an Existing Ad Account

After selecting your Business Manager, you will see the available ad accounts. If you already have accounts created, simply choose the right one.

### View Available Accounts

The list shows the following for each account:

| Field        | Description                                 |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------- |
| **Name**     | Name of the ad account                      |
| **ID**       | Unique identifier (act\_XXXXXXXXX)          |
| **Currency** | Billing currency (USD, COP, MXN, etc.)      |
| **Status**   | Active, Disabled, etc.                      |

### Choose the Right Account

1. Identify the account you want to use
2. Click to select it
3. The account will be highlighted

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  Choose an account that has:

* An active payment method (valid credit or debit card)
* The currency you prefer for billing
* A clean history (no restrictions or policy violations)
</Callout>


### Use the Search

If you have many accounts:

1. Use the **"Search Ad Accounts..."** field
2. Type part of the name or ID
3. The list will be filtered

## Create a New Ad Account

If you don't have an ad account or want to create a new one, SaleAds allows you to do it directly without leaving the platform. Here are the steps:



### Steps to Create

1. Click **"Create New Ad Account"**
2. Fill in the details:

| Field                | Description              | Example           |
| -------------------- | ------------------------ | ----------------- |
| **Account Name**     | Identifier for you       | "My Store - Ads"  |
| **Currency**         | Billing currency         | USD, COP, MXN     |
| **Time Zone**        | For reports              | America/Bogota    |

3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. Wait for confirmation: **"Ad account created successfully!"**

<Callout type="warn" title="Important About Currency">
  * Choose carefully, **it cannot be changed later**
* Ad prices will be displayed in this currency
* Billing will be in this currency
</Callout>


If your business operates in Colombian pesos but you prefer to control spending in dollars, choose USD. If you prefer to see everything in your local currency, choose COP or MXN.

### After Creating

The new account:

* Is created within your Business Manager
* Is automatically selected
* Can be used immediately

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  You still need to set up a payment method directly in Meta before you can launch ads. See [Prerequisites](requisitos-previos#4-cuenta-publicitaria).
</Callout>

## I Don't See Any Ad Accounts

Don't worry. These are the most common causes and how to resolve them:

### Possible Cause 1: The Business Manager has no accounts

**Message:** `No ad accounts were found for the selected Business Manager.`

**Solution:**

1. Click **"Create New Ad Account"**
2. SaleAds.ai will create an account in your Business Manager
3. Set up the payment method in Meta Business Suite

### Possible Cause 2: You don't have permissions on existing accounts

Ad accounts have separate permissions from the Business Manager. It is possible to be a BM administrator but not have access to a specific account.

**Solution:**

1. Contact the Business Manager administrator
2. Request admin access to the ad account
3. Return to SaleAds.ai and refresh the page

### Possible Cause 3: The accounts are disabled

Meta can disable accounts for advertising policy violations.

**Solution:**

1. Go to [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com)
2. Check the status of your ad accounts
3. If they are disabled, follow Meta's instructions to reactivate them or create a new one

## Verify Payment Method

Before continuing, make sure the selected account has an active payment method. This is a step you must do directly in Meta:

1. Go to [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com)
2. Go to **Payment settings**
3. Select the ad account
4. Verify there is an active payment method

> **No payment method = You won't be able to launch ads**
>
> SaleAds.ai will create the strategy correctly, but Meta won't publish it if there's no way to charge. It is like having the car ready but without fuel.

## Common Errors

### "No ad accounts found"

**Possible causes:**

* The Business Manager has no ad accounts
* You don't have admin permissions

**Solution:**

* Create a new account using the button in SaleAds.ai
* Or request access to an existing account

### "Error creating ad account"

**Possible causes:**

* Account limit reached in the Business Manager
* Restrictions on the Facebook account
* Temporary Meta error

**Solution:**

1. Try again in a few minutes
2. Verify that your Facebook account has no restrictions
3. Create the account manually at [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com) and return to select it

### The account shows "Disabled"

**Cause:** Meta has restricted the account due to advertising policy violations.

**Solution:**

1. You cannot use disabled accounts
2. Create a new account or use another existing one
3. Review Meta's policies to avoid future restrictions
