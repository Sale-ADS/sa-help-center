This guide covers the most frequent errors when connecting and configuring Google Ads in SaleAds.ai.

Don't worry, most are resolved in minutes. I'll explain how to fix them step by step.

---

## Connection Problems

### "Authorization Error"

**Message:** `We couldn't authorize access to your Google Ads account. Please try again.`

**Possible causes:**

1. **Permissions not granted:** You didn't accept all requested permissions during the OAuth process
2. **Session expired:** Your Google session expired during the process
3. **Pop-up blocker:** The authorization window was blocked
4. **Wrong account:** You signed in with a Google account that doesn't have Google Ads access

**Solutions:**

**Solution 1: Retry with all permissions**
1. Click **"Retry"**
2. Make sure to accept ALL permissions Google requests
3. Don't close the pop-up window until you complete the process

**Solution 2: Check pop-ups**
1. Check if your browser blocked a pop-up window
2. Allow pop-ups for saleads.ai
3. Try again

**Solution 3: Verify Google account**
1. Make sure you're signing in with the correct Google account
2. If you have multiple accounts, sign out of all and use only the correct one
3. Verify this account has administrator access to Google Ads

---

### "No Google Ads accounts found"

**Message:** `We couldn't find any Google Ads accounts associated with this Google account.`

**Causes:**

1. The Google account doesn't have a Google Ads account created
2. The Google Ads account is in a different manager (MCC) account
3. You don't have administrator permissions on the Google Ads account

**Solutions:**

**Solution 1: Create Google Ads account**
1. Go to [ads.google.com](https://ads.google.com)
2. Create a new Google Ads account
3. Complete the initial setup (country, currency, time zone)
4. Return to SaleAds.ai and try connecting again

**Solution 2: Verify MCC access**
If you use a manager (MCC) account:
1. Make sure the individual account is linked to the MCC
2. Verify you have administrative access to the specific account
3. Try connecting from the individual account instead of the MCC

---

### "Account already linked to another manager"

**Message:** `This Google Ads account is already being managed by another manager account.`

**Cause:** The Google Ads account is already linked to a different MCC.

**Solution:**

A Google Ads account can only be directly linked to one MCC at a time. Options:

1. **Request access from current MCC:** Contact the administrator of the current MCC and ask them to give you access
2. **Unlink from previous MCC:** The current owner must unlink the account from their MCC
3. **Use a different account:** Create or use another Google Ads account

---

### Google window doesn't open

**Causes:**
- Pop-up blocker active
- Browser extensions interfering
- Incompatible browser

**Solution:**
1. Disable pop-up blocker for saleads.ai
2. Try in incognito/private mode
3. Use updated Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
4. Disable extensions temporarily

## Account Selection Issues

### "My Google Ads account doesn't appear in the list"

**Causes:**

1. You didn't give access to all accounts during OAuth authorization
2. The account is at a different level in the MCC (deep hierarchy)
3. Temporary Google Ads API issues

**Solutions:**

**Solution 1: Reconnect with all permissions**
1. Disconnect the Google Ads account in SaleAds.ai
2. Reconnect
3. During Google authorization, make sure to select **ALL** accounts you want to give access to

**Solution 2: Verify MCC hierarchy**
1. If you use MCC, verify the account isn't at a very deep level (limit: 6 levels)
2. Consider linking the account directly to your main MCC

---

### "Error loading accounts"

**Message:** `Error loading Google Ads accounts. Please try again.`

**Cause:** Communication problem with Google Ads API.

**Solution:**
1. Click **"Retry"**
2. Wait 30 seconds and try again
3. If it persists, try in 5-10 minutes
4. Check Google Ads services status at [status.google.com](https://status.google.com)

## Billing Problems

### "Payment method declined"

**Causes:**

* Card not enabled for international purchases
* Insufficient funds
* Bank blocked transaction for suspected fraud
* Billing address doesn't match card

**Solution:**

1. Contact your bank and authorize transactions to "Google"
2. Verify the billing address in Google Ads matches exactly with your card
3. Try with another card or payment method
4. If using bank account, verify it completed the verification process (3-5 days)

---

### "Cannot change billing country"

**Cause:** Google Ads doesn't allow changing the billing country after initial setup.

**Solution:**

You must create a new Google Ads account with the correct country:
1. Go to [ads.google.com](https://ads.google.com)
2. Create a new account
3. Select the correct billing country
4. Set up the new account in SaleAds.ai

<Callout type="warn" title="Important">
  You cannot transfer campaigns or history between Google Ads accounts. You'll need to start fresh in the new account.
</Callout>

---

### "Monthly invoicing not available"

**Cause:** Monthly invoicing requires meeting certain requirements.

**Typical requirements:**
- Account active for more than 12 months
- Consistent spending history (generally $5,000+ USD monthly)
- Good payment history

**Solution:**

If you don't qualify for monthly invoicing:
1. Continue using automatic card payments
2. Maintain good payment history
3. Contact Google Ads support after 12 months to request monthly invoicing

## Conversion Tracking Issues

### "Conversion tag not firing"

**Causes:**

* Code not installed correctly
* Conversion event not configured
* Conflicts with ad blockers

**Solutions:**

**Verify installation:**
1. Use **Google Tag Assistant** Chrome extension
2. Visit your website
3. Verify the Google Tag (AW-XXXXX) is installed
4. Complete a test conversion
5. Confirm the conversion tag fires

**Verify conversion event:**
1. In Google Ads, go to **Tools** → **Conversions**
2. Verify the conversion action is active
3. Confirm the event name matches what's configured on your site

---

### "Conversions not showing in dashboard"

**Causes:**

* Normal reporting delay (24-48 hours for first conversion)
* Conversion window expired
* User used an ad blocker

**Solution:**

1. **Wait for reporting time:** First conversions may take 24-48 hours to appear
2. **Verify conversion window:** Make sure it's configured correctly (30-90 days)
3. **Enable enhanced conversions:** Improves tracking when there are blockers
4. Use GTM test mode to verify the tag works

---

### "Discrepancy between Google Ads and GA4"

**Cause:** Google Ads and GA4 use different attribution models.

| Factor | Google Ads | GA4 |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| Attribution model | Last click (default) | Data-driven |
| Conversion window | 30 days (configurable) | 30-90 days |
| Conversion counting | One per click | Every event |

**Solution:**

1. Use Google Ads native tracking as source of truth for optimization
2. Use GA4 for multi-channel attribution analysis
3. Don't import GA4 conversions if you already have Google Ads native tracking

## Disconnect and Reconnect

If no solution works, you can completely disconnect and start over.

### Steps to Disconnect

1. Go to **Connected Accounts**
2. In Google Ads, click **"Edit Configuration"**
3. Click **"Disconnect Google Ads account"**
4. Confirm: **"Are you sure?"** → **"Disconnect"**
5. You'll see: **"Google Ads account disconnected successfully"**

### After Disconnecting

- Your account returns to "Not connected" status
- You must reconnect and configure everything again
- Existing strategies may be affected

---

## Revoke Permissions from Google

If you want to completely remove SaleAds.ai access:

1. Go to [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)
2. Look for **SaleAds** in the third-party apps list
3. Click **"Remove access"**
4. Confirm the removal

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  After revoking permissions, you'll need to reconnect from scratch if you want to use SaleAds.ai again.
</Callout>

## General Verifications

If you have persistent problems with Google Ads:

1. **Verify in Google:** Go to [ads.google.com](https://ads.google.com) and confirm you can access normally
2. **Try another browser:** Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge
3. **Clear cache:** Clear browser cookies and cache
4. **Incognito mode:** Try in private window
