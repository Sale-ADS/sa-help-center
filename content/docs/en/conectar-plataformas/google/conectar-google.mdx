Connecting your Google Ads account allows you to launch and manage search and display advertising campaigns directly from SaleAds.ai. This step is essential for reaching people who are already actively searching for products or services like yours on Google. Once connected, I take care of coordinating the publication and optimization of your ads without you having to log into the Google Ads panel.

The connection process is quick and secure. You just need to authorize SaleAds.ai through your Google account, and in less than two minutes you will be ready to launch campaigns.

## Prerequisites

Before connecting your Google Ads account, make sure you have everything ready so the process is smooth and uninterrupted:

- **An active Google Ads account** -- If you don't have one yet, you can create it for free at [ads.google.com](https://ads.google.com). You need to have completed the initial account setup (country, currency, time zone).
- **Administrator access to the account** -- You must be the owner or have administrator permissions on the Google Ads account you want to connect. Read-only or standard user permissions are not sufficient.
- **An active SaleAds.ai account** -- You need an active plan (PRO, BUSINESS, or AGENCY) to connect advertising platforms.

<Callout type="warn" title="Important">
  If you use Google Ads through a manager account (MCC), make sure to sign in with the email address that has direct access to the advertising account you want to connect.
</Callout>

## Steps to Connect

The authorization process uses Google's secure OAuth system. At no point do we share or store your password.

<Steps>
<Step>
Go to **Settings** in the SaleAds.ai dashboard. You will find it in the left side menu, in the Settings section.
</Step>
<Step>
Select **Connect Platforms** and then click **Google Ads**. You will see the connection button next to the Google logo.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **Authorize with Google**. This will open a pop-up window from Google's authentication system. If your browser blocks pop-ups, make sure to allow them for SaleAds.ai.
</Step>
<Step>
Sign in with your Google account. Use the same email address associated with your Google Ads account. If you have multiple Google accounts, select the correct one.
</Step>
<Step>
Accept the requested permissions. Google will show you exactly what permissions SaleAds.ai needs. These include the ability to manage your advertising campaigns. All permissions are necessary for the platform to function correctly.
</Step>
<Step>
Select your Google Ads account from the list. If you have multiple advertising accounts, choose the one you want to use with SaleAds.ai. You can connect additional accounts later.
</Step>
</Steps>

Once these steps are completed, you will see a confirmation message in your dashboard. The account will be linked and ready to launch campaigns. **From idea to campaign in minutes.**

## Permissions SaleAds.ai Requests

So you know exactly what you are authorizing, these are the permissions I need from your Google account:

| Permission | What I use it for |
| --- | --- |
| **Manage campaigns** | Create, edit, and pause campaigns on your behalf |
| **Read metrics** | Show performance and statistics in your dashboard |
| **Manage budgets** | Configure and adjust budgets according to your strategy |

**You have full control.** You can revoke these permissions at any time from your Google account settings.

## Common Errors

### My Google Ads account doesn't appear

**Possible causes:**
- The Google account you signed in with doesn't have administrator access to Google Ads
- Your Google Ads account is not fully set up (missing payment method or initial configuration)

**Solution:** Verify that the Google account you used has administrator access to Google Ads. If you have multiple Google accounts, try with another one.

### Permission error

**Possible causes:**
- You didn't accept all permissions during the authorization process
- Your browser blocked the pop-up window before completing authorization

**Solution:** Make sure to accept all permissions when Google requests them. If the problem persists, try from a different browser or in incognito mode.

### The connection was interrupted

**Possible causes:**
- Unstable internet connection during the process
- The authorization window timed out

**Solution:** Don't worry, simply repeat the process from step 1. Your previous data is not affected.
