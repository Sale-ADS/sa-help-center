Connecting your Google Ads account allows you to launch and manage search and display ad campaigns directly from SaleAds.ai. This step is fundamental to reaching people who are actively searching for products or services like yours on Google.

Once connected, I take care of coordinating the publication and optimization of your ads without you having to enter the Google Ads panel.

## Prerequisites

Before starting, make sure you have:

- An active and configured Google Ads account
- Administrator access to the account
- A valid payment method configured

> If you don't have these requirements yet, check [Prerequisites](requisitos-previos-google) first.

---

## Current Connection Status

On the Connected Accounts screen you'll see one of these statuses:

| Status | Meaning | Action Required |
|--------|---------|-----------------|
| **Not connected** | You haven't linked your Google Ads account | Click "Connect Account" |
| **Connected - Configuration pending** | Connected Google but need to select account | Complete configuration |
| **Configured** | Ready to launch strategies | None |

## Connection Process

<Steps>
<Step>
### Access Settings

1. In the Dashboard, go to the side menu
2. Click **"Connected Accounts"**
3. You'll see the **Google Ads** section with the connect button
</Step>

<Step>
### Start Connection

1. Click the **"Connect with Google"** button
2. A Google authentication pop-up window will open

![Google account selection](/images/screenshots/02-conectar-plataformas/google-account-selection.png)
*Figure 3.1: Google account selection screen to continue to SaleAds.ai*

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  If you use a pop-up blocker, allow pop-ups from SaleAds.ai
</Callout>
</Step>

<Step>
### Sign in to Google

In the pop-up window:

1. Sign in with your Google account associated with Google Ads
2. If you have multiple Google accounts, select the correct one

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  Use the Google account that has administrator access to your Google Ads account.
</Callout>
</Step>

<Step>
### Authorize Permissions

Google will ask you to authorize several permissions. Here's what I need them for:

| Permission | What I use it for |
|------------|-------------------|
| **View your Google Ads account** | Access your campaigns and metrics |
| **Manage campaigns** | Create, edit, and pause campaigns on your behalf |
| **View statistics** | Show performance in your dashboard |
| **Manage budgets** | Set budgets according to your strategy |

1. Review the requested permissions
2. Click **"Continue"** or **"Accept"** on each screen
3. **Important:** Select **ALL** Google Ads accounts you want to give access to

![Google permissions detail](/images/screenshots/02-conectar-plataformas/google-permissions-detail.png)
*Figure 3.2: Permissions required to manage Google Ads campaigns*

> **Warning:** If you don't select all assets, you won't be able to use them in SaleAds.ai later.
</Step>

<Step>
### Confirm Connection

Once authorized:

1. The pop-up window will close automatically
2. You'll return to SaleAds.ai
3. You'll see the message: **"Successfully Connected!"**

![Google OAuth confirmation](/images/screenshots/02-conectar-plataformas/google-oauth-confirm.png)
*Figure 3.3: Final authorization confirmation with Google*
</Step>
</Steps>

## Account Types and Hierarchy

It's important to understand the difference between account types:

### Individual Account
- A single Google Ads account
- Directly linked to your Google account
- Ideal for businesses with a single brand

### MCC Account (Manager)
- "Umbrella" account that manages multiple accounts
- You can view and select child accounts
- Ideal for agencies or companies with multiple brands

<Callout type="info" title="Key difference">
  If you connect an MCC account, you'll see all accounts it manages. If you connect an individual account, you'll only see that account.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

### "My Google Ads account doesn't appear"

**Possible causes:**
- The Google account you signed in with doesn't have administrator access to Google Ads
- Your Google Ads account isn't fully configured (missing payment method)

**Solution:** Verify the Google account you used has administrator access to Google Ads. If you have multiple Google accounts, try with another one.

### "Permission error"

**Possible causes:**
- You didn't accept all permissions during the authorization process
- Your browser blocked the pop-up before completing authorization

**Solution:** Make sure to accept all permissions when Google requests them. If the problem persists, try from a different browser or incognito mode.

### "Account already linked"

**Cause:** The Google Ads account is already being managed by another MCC or is connected to another SaleAds account.

**Solution:** The account must be unlinked from the previous MCC before it can be connected. Contact the previous administrator.

### Connection interrupted

**Possible causes:**
- Unstable internet connection during the process
- Timeout in the authorization window

**Solution:** Don't worry, simply repeat the process from step 1. Your previous data isn't affected.

| Permission | Use |
