## 1. Google Account

You need a Google account to access Google Ads. It can be a personal Gmail account or a Google Workspace account if you use business email.

**If you already have an account:** Make sure you can sign in correctly at [google.com](https://google.com).

**If you don't have an account:**

1. Go to [accounts.google.com/signup](https://accounts.google.com/signup)
2. Complete the registration with your email or create a new Gmail account
3. Verify your email following the instructions you receive

---

## 2. Google Ads Account

Google Ads is the platform where search and display ads are created and managed. You need to have an account created and configured.

### Google Ads Account Types

Google offers two different account types:

| Type | Description | Best for |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Individual Account** | Single account for one business | Business owners, small internal teams |
| **Manager Account (MCC)** | "Umbrella" account to manage multiple accounts | Agencies, consultants, companies with multiple brands |

### Create an Individual Account

1. Go to [ads.google.com](https://ads.google.com)
2. Click **"Start now"**
3. Sign in with your Google account
4. Configure the basic data:
   * **Billing country**: Determines available payment methods
   * **Time zone**: Affects reports and scheduling
   * **Currency**: **Cannot be changed later** - choose carefully
5. Save the configuration

<Callout type="warn" title="Critical">
  The currency and billing country **cannot be changed** after creating the account. If you make a mistake, you'll need to create a new account.
</Callout>

### Create a Manager Account (MCC)

If you're an agency or manage multiple clients, you need an MCC account:

1. Go to [ads.google.com/home/tools/manager-accounts/](https://ads.google.com/home/tools/manager-accounts/)
2. Click **"Create a manager account"**
3. Choose the purpose:
   * *"Manage my clients' accounts"* (for agencies)
   * *"Manage my accounts"* (for companies with multiple brands)
4. Complete your business information
5. Configure country, time zone, and currency

<Callout type="info" title="MCC Advantage">
  With an MCC account you can manage multiple Google Ads accounts from a single panel without having to constantly sign out and back in.
</Callout>

## 3. Administrator Access

To connect Google Ads with SaleAds.ai, you need administrator permissions on the account.

**If you're the account owner:** You already have administrative access.

**If the account belongs to a client or company:**

1. Ask the owner to add you as an administrator
2. Go to [ads.google.com](https://ads.google.com) and verify you can access
3. Make sure you can see all sections, including Billing and Access and Security

### Verify Your Access Level

1. In Google Ads, click the tools icon (⚙️)
2. Go to **Access and Security**
3. Verify your email appears with **"Administrator"** role

## 4. Payment Method

Without a valid payment method, your ads won't be published. Google Ads requires setting up billing before launching campaigns.

### Available Payment Options

Options vary by country, but generally include:

| Method | Payment Type | Activation Time |
|--------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Credit/debit card** | Automatic or Manual | Immediate |
| **Bank account (direct debit)** | Automatic | 3-5 business days |
| **PayPal** | Automatic | Immediate (where available) |
| **Monthly invoicing** | Monthly | Requires Google approval |

### Set Up Automatic Payments (Recommended)

1. In Google Ads, click the tools icon (⚙️)
2. Go to **Billing** → **Settings**
3. Select your country and continue
4. Enter your company billing address
5. Select **"Automatic payments"**
6. Add your payment method (card or bank account)
7. Accept the terms and conditions

### Understanding the Payment Threshold

Google Ads uses a **payment threshold** system:

- You start with a low threshold (e.g., $50 USD)
- When your spending reaches the threshold, your card is charged
- If you don't reach the threshold, you're charged on the 1st of the following month
- As you demonstrate on-time payment history, the threshold automatically increases

<Callout type="info" title="Example">
  If your threshold is $200 and you spend $150 in the month, you're charged on the 1st of the following month. If you spend $250 before month-end, you're charged immediately when reaching $200, and the remaining $50 accumulates for the next cycle.
</Callout>

## 5. Billing Configuration

Google Ads billing configuration includes elements that **cannot be changed** afterward:

| Setting | Can be changed? | Note |

| Currency | ❌ No | Affects all your accounting |
| Time zone | ❌ No | Affects scheduling and reports |
| Payment method | ✅ Yes | Can be changed anytime |

## Common Issues

<details>

<summary>I can't create a Google Ads account</summary>

Make sure:
- You have a valid and verified Google account
- You don't have an existing Google Ads account with the same Google account
- You meet the minimum age requirements for your country

</details>

<details>

<summary>My account is in the wrong currency</summary>

Unfortunately, you **cannot change the currency** of an existing Google Ads account. You must create a new account with the correct currency.

</details>

<details>

<summary>I don't have administrator access</summary>

Contact the owner of the Google Ads account and request they assign you the administrator role in **Access and Security**.

</details>

<details>

<summary>My payment method is rejected</summary>

Possible causes:
- Card not enabled for international purchases
- Insufficient funds
- Bank blocked transaction for suspected fraud
- Billing address doesn't match card

Solution: Contact your bank or try another payment method.

</details>
