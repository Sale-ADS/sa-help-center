Billing configuration is essential for your campaigns to be published. Google Ads requires a valid payment method before launching any ads. In this section, I explain how to verify and manage your billing.

**You have total control** of your payment methods and billing configuration.

---

## Understanding Google Ads Billing

Google Ads primarily uses **automatic payments** in most countries:

### How Automatic Payments Work

1. Your ads start showing immediately
2. Costs accumulate daily
3. Google automatically charges when:
   - You reach your **payment threshold** (e.g., $200 USD), or
   - It's the **1st of the following month**

<Callout type="info" title="Example">
  If your threshold is $50 and you spend $40 in the month, you're charged on the 1st of the following month. If you spend $60 before month-end, you're charged $50 when reaching the threshold and the remaining $10 accumulates for the next cycle.
</Callout>

### The Payment Threshold

The payment threshold is the accumulated amount that triggers an automatic charge:

| Payment History | Typical Threshold |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| New account | $50 USD |
| After on-time payments | $200 USD |
| Established account | $350 USD |
| Mature account | $500 USD |

Google automatically increases your threshold as you demonstrate on-time payment history.

## Verify Billing Configuration

### Step 1: Access Billing

1. Go to [ads.google.com](https://ads.google.com)
2. Click the tools icon (⚙️)
3. Go to **Billing** → **Settings**

### Step 2: Verify Payment Method

In the **"Payment methods"** section verify:

| Element | Correct Status |
|---------|----------------|
| Primary method | ✅ Active and valid |
| Expiration date | ✅ Current |
| Billing address | ✅ Matches card |

### Step 3: Verify Configuration

In **"Billing settings"** confirm:

- Billing country: Correct (cannot be changed)
- Currency: Correct (cannot be changed)
- Time zone: Correct (cannot be changed)

## Available Payment Options

Options vary by country:

| Method | Availability | Activation Time |
|--------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Credit/debit card** | Almost all countries | Immediate |
| **Bank account (direct debit)** | Most countries | 3-5 business days |
| **PayPal** | Select countries | Immediate |
| **Monthly invoicing** | Qualified accounts | Requires approval |
| **Transfer/Deposit** | Some countries | 1-3 days |

### Credit/Debit Card (Most Common)

**Accepted:** Visa, Mastercard, American Express (by country)

**Set up:**
1. In Billing → Payment methods
2. Click **"Add payment method"**
3. Select **"Credit card"**
4. Enter your card details
5. Mark as primary if needed
6. Save

<Callout type="warn" title="Address verification">
  The billing address must match exactly what your bank has on file. If there are discrepancies, payments may be rejected.
</Callout>

### Bank Account (Direct Debit)

**Process:**
1. Add your bank account
2. Google performs small verification deposits (3-5 days)
3. Confirm the deposit amounts in settings
4. Account is activated for automatic payments

## Set Up Manual Payments (Where Available)

In some countries you can use manual payments:

1. Pre-fund your account before ads show
2. Ads pause when balance reaches zero
3. Ideal for strict budget control

<Callout type="info" title="Limitation">
  Manual payments aren't available in all countries. Check availability in your Google Ads account.
</Callout>

## Add Backup Payment Method

I recommend setting up a secondary payment method:

1. Go to **Billing** → **Payment methods**
2. Add a second method (different card or account)
3. This will be used if the primary method fails

**Benefits:**
- Prevents campaigns from pausing due to payment failures
- Protection against expired cards
- Continuity of your campaigns

## Monthly Invoicing (For Qualified Accounts)

Some accounts may qualify for monthly invoicing:

### Typical Requirements

- **Business registered** for more than 1 year
- **Google Ads account** active and in good standing for at least 6-12 months
- **Monthly spend** of at least $5,000 USD for 3 of the last 12 months
- **Consistent and on-time payment history**

### How to Apply

1. Go to **Billing** → **Settings**
2. Look for **"Apply for monthly invoicing"** option
3. Complete the application with your business information
4. Wait for approval (may take several days)

<Callout type="info" title="Benefit">
  With monthly invoicing you receive a consolidated invoice and have time to pay (typically 30 days).
</Callout>

## Resolve Payment Issues

### "Payment method declined"

**Common causes:**

| Cause | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Insufficient funds | Check balance or use another card |
| Expired card | Update expiration date |
| Incorrect address | Correct billing address |
| Bank block | Contact your bank to authorize "Google" |
| International purchases | Enable international purchases on your card |

### "Account suspended for billing"

If your account was suspended for payment issues:

1. Go to [ads.google.com](https://ads.google.com)
2. Resolve the outstanding balance
3. Update or add a valid payment method
4. Wait for reactivation (usually immediate)

## View Invoices and Payments

### Transaction History

1. Go to **Billing** → **Transactions**
2. You'll see all charges made
3. You can download invoices in PDF

### Set Up Notifications

To receive billing alerts:

1. Go to **Billing** → **Settings**
2. Configure alerts for:
   - Payment threshold reached
   - Payment method issues
   - Invoices generated

## Change Billing Configuration

### What you CAN change:

- ✅ Primary payment method
- ✅ Add backup methods
- ✅ Configure notifications
- ✅ Billing address (if it matches country/currency)

### What you CANNOT change:

- ❌ Billing country
- ❌ Currency
- ❌ Time zone

<Callout type="warn" title="Critical">
  If you need to change country or currency, you must create a new Google Ads account. These values are not modifiable after creation.
</Callout>
