Before launching your first campaign, you need to connect the advertising platforms where you want your ads to appear. This step is essential because I need authorized access to your accounts to be able to publish, optimize, and measure the performance of each campaign you launch.

Don't worry: the connection process takes only a few minutes per platform, and once done, you don't need to repeat it. You can disconnect your accounts at any time from your settings panel.

---

## Why Connect Your Platforms?

Connecting your advertising accounts allows you to:

- **Launch campaigns directly** from SaleAds.ai without having to log into each platform separately
- **Manage everything from one place** -- copies, creatives, budgets, and metrics in a single dashboard
- **Leverage AI optimization** -- I analyze the performance of your ads and make automatic adjustments
- **Save time** -- from idea to campaign in minutes, without jumping between platforms

When you see your campaigns running on multiple platforms at the same time, all managed from a single place, you will understand the true value of having your accounts connected.

## Which One to Connect First?

If you are just getting started, I recommend connecting **Meta Ads** first. It is the platform with the greatest reach and the one most of our users activate from day one. Once you have your first campaigns active on Meta, you can expand to Google Ads or TikTok to diversify your channels.
