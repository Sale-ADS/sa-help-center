Once you've completed the initial setup, you can edit or update any part of your business information at any time from the **My Business** section.

---

## How to access it

In the sidebar, click **My Business**. You'll see the main panel with all available configuration tabs.

![My Business screen with Identity, Voice, Audience, Marketing, Products and Services tabs](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/mi-negocio-editar.png)

---

## Configuration tabs

Your business information is organized into **6 tabs**. Click the one you want to edit:

### Identity

![Identity tab with logo, business name, niche, style and brand colors](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/mi-negocio-identidad.png)

The Identity tab is split into two panels. The left panel holds your brand's basic information; the right panel shows the colors used in your ad pieces.

**Logo** — displays your current business image. Use the upload icon to replace it or the trash icon to remove it.

**Business name** — click the pencil icon next to the name to edit it directly.

**Niche** — shows the currently assigned niche as a tag. Click **×** to remove it and **+** to add the one that best represents you.

**Style** — defines your brand's communication tone (e.g. professional, friendly, creative). Click **×** to remove a style and **+** to add another.

**Brand colors** — the right panel shows your active color palette. Click any color to edit it, **+** to add a new one, or **×** to remove it. These are the colors the platform uses when generating your creatives.

### Voice

![Voice tab with tone, personality and prohibited words](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/mi-negocio-voz.png)

The Voice tab defines how your brand sounds in your ad copy. Each field shows the current setting and can be updated at any time.

**Tone** — shows your brand's active tone along with a real example of how an ad would sound with that style. Click **Change** to open the selector.

![Change tone modal with the 8 available options](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/mi-negocio-cambiar-tono.png)

The modal presents all 8 options with a sample text for each: Friendly, Inspiring, Professional, Direct, Enthusiastic, Elegant, Fun, and Bold. Select the one that best represents your brand and click **Save**.

**Personality** — shows the currently selected traits. Click **Change** to open the selector.

![Change personality modal with available traits](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/mi-negocio-cambiar-personalidad.png)

You can choose from 18 traits — select 1 to 5 that describe your brand: Familiar, Empathetic, Honest, Passionate, Adventurous, Brave, Expert, Trustworthy, Leader, Innovative, Original, Fun, Elegant, Exclusive, Sensual, Friendly, Community-driven, or Rebellious. Click **Save** when done.

**Prohibited words** — list of terms you don't want appearing in your ads. Click **+** to add a new one and **×** to remove an existing one.

### Audience

![Audience tab with ideal customer, location and problem you solve](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/mi-negocio-audiencia.png)

The Audience tab defines who your campaigns are targeting. It has three editable fields:

**Ideal customer** — shows a summary of the people your business is aimed at. Click **Edit** to rewrite it so it more accurately reflects your ideal customer.

**Location** — indicates the city, region, or country where your business operates or where your customers are. Click **Edit** to update it.

**Problem you solve** — describes the main need your business addresses. Click **Edit** to adjust it.

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  Be specific about the problem you solve. The more concrete you are, the better your ad targeting will be.
</Callout>

### Marketing

![Marketing tab with engagement, consistency, what makes you different and mini-diagnosis](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/mi-negocio-marketing.png)

The Marketing tab is split into two panels. The left panel shows your business metrics and data; the right panel displays the AI-generated mini-diagnosis.

**Engagement** — shows your Instagram account's interaction level as a progress bar with a label (e.g. "Medium"). This is detected automatically from your recent activity and cannot be edited manually.

**Consistency** — shows how often you post on Instagram along with a rating (e.g. "Irregular — 3 posts/week"). Also detected automatically.

**What makes you different** — this one you can edit: click **+** to add the factors that set your business apart from competitors. These directly shape the message of your campaigns.

**Mini-diagnosis** — the right panel shows an analysis generated automatically from your data. Once enough information has been processed, you'll see observations about your digital presence and areas for improvement.

### Products

The Products tab shows your full catalog with the total number of registered products. You can switch between grid and list view using the icons in the top right corner.

![Products tab in grid view](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/mi-negocio-productos-grid.png)

![Products tab in list view](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/mi-negocio-productos-lista.png)

From either view you can take three actions on each product:

- **Pencil icon** — opens the side panel to edit the product
- **Trash icon** — removes the product from the catalog
- **New product +** — adds a new product to the catalog

#### Add a new product

Clicking **New product +** opens a side panel where you fill in the details:

![New product panel with photo, name, description and price fields](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/mi-negocio-nuevo-producto.png)

Upload the **main photo** and, optionally, additional photos. Then fill in the **name**, **description** (up to 300 characters), and **price**. Click **Create** to save it.

#### Edit an existing product

Clicking the pencil icon on any product opens the same panel with the existing data pre-loaded:

![Edit product panel with photo, name, description, price and margin](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/mi-negocio-editar-producto.png)

You can update the photos, name, description, price, and profit margin. Click **Save** when done.

### Services

The Services tab shows all the services registered in your catalog in list format, with the total count at the top.

![Services tab with service list and New service button](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/mi-negocio-servicios.png)

From the list you can take two actions on each service:

- **Pencil icon** — opens the side panel to edit the service
- **Trash icon** — removes the service from the catalog

#### Add a new service

Clicking **New service +** opens a side panel with an empty form:

![New service panel with name, description, price and delivery fields](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/mi-negocio-nuevo-servicio.png)

Fill in the following fields and click **Create**:

| Field | What to enter |
|-------|--------------|
| **Service name** | What you call the service you offer |
| **What's included?** | A brief description of what the client gets |
| **Price** | Value in USD |
| **Price type** | Fixed, From, Quote, or Hidden |
| **Booking method** | WhatsApp, Website, In person, or Phone |
| **Where it's offered** | At your location, At the client's place, Virtual, or Multiple locations |

#### Edit an existing service

Clicking the pencil icon on any service opens the same panel with the existing data pre-loaded:

![Edit service panel with pre-filled data](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/mi-negocio-editar-servicio.png)

Update the fields you need and click **Save** when done.

## Additional options

In the top right corner of the screen you'll see a three-dot icon **⋮**. Clicking it reveals a menu with additional options:

![Options menu with Redo and Export](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/mi-negocio-menu-opciones.png)

### Redo

The **Redo** option lets you go through the entire setup process from scratch. Use it if you want to update all your business information at once — for example, if you've changed industries, rebranded, or want to replace the full description with new data.

<Callout type="warning" title="Keep in mind">
  Redoing the setup will replace your current business information with the new data you enter.
</Callout>

---

<Callout type="info" title="Changes apply immediately">
  Any edits you save are reflected immediately in your next campaigns and ad pieces.
</Callout>
