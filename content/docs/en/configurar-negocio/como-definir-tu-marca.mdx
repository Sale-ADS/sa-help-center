title: Set Up My Business
description: Overview of the process to configure your business in SaleADS.ai
---

**Set Up My Business** is the process where you give SaleADS.ai all the information it needs to create personalized ad campaigns. You do it once and can edit it at any time.

---

## How to access it

There are two ways to start configuring your business:

### From the Onboarding

If you're completing the initial setup steps, in **Step 3 — Set up your business**, click the **Continue** button.

![Onboarding - Step 3 Set up your business](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/onboarding-configura-negocio.png)

### From the My Business menu

If you've already finished onboarding or prefer to access it directly, go to the sidebar and click **My Business**. Then click the **Start now** button.

![My Business - Start now screen](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/mi-negocio-empezar-ahora.png)

---

## First step: What do you offer?

After clicking **Continue** or **Start now**, the platform asks what type of business you have to build better campaigns for you. Choose the option that best describes what you sell:

![What do you offer? screen with Products, Services and Both options](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/que-ofreces.png)

| Option | When to choose it |
|--------|------------------|
| **Products** | You sell physical or digital items: clothing, food, supplements, accessories… |
| **Services** | You offer your time or expertise: consulting, classes, design, legal advice… |
| **Both** | You sell products AND also offer services. |

After choosing, the platform shows the next screen to narrow down your category.

### What type of products do you sell?

If you selected **Products** or **Both**, the platform will ask you to pick your main category to better personalize your campaigns:

![What type of products do you sell? screen with categories](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/que-tipo-de-productos.png)

Available categories include: Food / Beverages, Clothing / Fashion, Cosmetics / Beauty, Pets, Technology, Home / Decor, Supplements / Health, Babies / Kids, Gaming / Entertainment, Dropshipping, Handmade / Crafts, and Other.

Select the one that best describes your business and click **Continue**.

### What type of services do you offer?

If you selected **Services** or **Both**, you'll see this screen instead to choose your service category:

![What type of services do you offer? screen with categories](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/que-tipo-de-servicios.png)

Available categories include: Medical / Clinic, Legal / Accounting, Aesthetics / Spa, Education / Courses, Repairs / Technical, Coaching / Psychology, Professional Services, Design / Creative, Real Estate, Marketing / Agency, Personal Trainer / Gym, Consulting, and Other.

Select the one that best matches what you do and click **Continue**.

<Callout type="info" title="Can't find your category?">
  Choose the closest one to your type of service, or select **Other**. You'll be able to add more detail in the following steps.
</Callout>

---

## Do you have a website?

After choosing your category, the platform asks whether you have a website. This is where your process splits into two paths:

![Do you have a website? screen with URL field and Continue without website button](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/tienes-sitio-web.png)

- **If you have a website** → Type your URL in the "Website" field and click **Continue**. The platform will analyze your site automatically to understand your business.
- **If you don't have a website** → Click **Continue without a website**. The platform will ask you to tell it about your business — you can choose to **record audio**, **upload an audio file**, or **type it as text**.

Follow the guide for the path that applies to you:
