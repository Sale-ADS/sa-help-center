Selecting where you operate or deliver is one of the most important steps in your business setup.

The more specific your selected area is, the better I can understand your market and suggest relevant targeting.

---

## Why It Matters

Your selected location directly affects:

- **Where ads are shown** (country, city, or region)
- **What targeting interests I suggest**
- **What language and tone your copies use**

<Callout type="warn" title="Important">
  The more accurate your location is, the better the AI can adapt your strategy to local context.
</Callout>

---

## How to Select Locations

### Step 1: Search

1. Type a country, city, or region
2. Enter at least 2 characters
3. Pick one result from the list

### Step 2: Add

1. Click the location you want
2. It appears in **"Selected locations"**
3. Add as many locations as needed

### Step 3: Review

- Review your final list
- Remove entries with the **X**
- Stay focused: more locations = broader audience

---

## Location Types

| Type | Example | Best for |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Country** | Colombia, Mexico, Spain | National e-commerce |
| **State/Region** | Antioquia, California, Catalonia | Regional businesses |
| **City** | Medellin, Mexico City, Barcelona | Local businesses |

---

## Quick Recommendations

- **Local business:** start with your city and nearby areas
- **National e-commerce:** use full country or top cities
- **Digital services:** combine multiple Spanish-speaking countries

---

## Common Errors

### "Location required"
**Message:** `Please select at least one location before continuing.`

**Solution:** Add at least one location from the search field.

### "No results found"
**Solution:**

1. Check spelling
2. Try a broader name
3. Try the English name

---

## Change Location Later

1. Go to **Set Up Your Business**
2. Open the location section
3. Update selected locations

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  Changing location may require regenerating your Brief to refresh suggested interests.
</Callout>
