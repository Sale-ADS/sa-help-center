The Brief is the document that contains all the information about your business. In SaleAds.ai, you create it simply: by recording, uploading audio, or writing text about your business.

> **This is a key moment:** I will use your audio or text to understand your business and generate personalized ad copy. You share, I understand and create.

## What is the Brief?

The Brief contains:

- **Target audience** - Who you sell to
- **Value proposition** - What makes you unique
- **Goals** - What you want to achieve
- **Brand tone** - How you communicate
- **Key messages** - What you want to convey

## What to Say in the Audio

### Example of a Good Audio

> "Hi, I run a sportswear store in Medellin called FitStyle. We sell gym, running, and yoga apparel. My ideal customer is women from 25 to 45 years old who exercise regularly and want comfortable but stylish clothes. What makes us unique is our high-quality Colombian fabrics and exclusive designs. We solve the problem of women not finding sportswear that is both functional and elegant enough to wear outside the gym. Our tone is motivational and approachable."

### Key Points to Mention

- Your business name
- What you sell (products/services)
- Who you sell to (ideal customer)
- What makes you different
- What problem you solve
- How you want to sound (tone)

## Options to Create the Brief

You have three options:

| Option | Description | Ideal for |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| **Record Audio** | Record directly from your browser | When you are in front of the computer |
| **Upload Audio** | Upload an existing audio file | When you already have a recording |
| **Write with Text** | Manually type your business information | When you prefer typing or cannot record audio |

---

## Option 1: Record Audio

### Steps to Record

1. Click the **"Record Audio"** tab
2. Click **"Start Recording"**
3. Allow microphone access if prompted
4. Speak clearly while answering the guide questions
5. Click **"Stop Recording"** when you finish

### Recording Requirements

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| **Minimum duration** | 10 seconds |
| **Maximum duration** | 3 minutes |
| **Format** | Automatic (WebM) |

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  Do not worry about perfection. Speak naturally as if you were explaining your business to a friend.
</Callout>

### After Recording

1. You can **play** the audio and review it
2. If you do not like it, click **"Record Again"**
3. When you are satisfied, continue to the next step

---

## Option 2: Upload Audio

If you already have a recording:

### Supported Formats

- MP3
- WAV
- M4A
- OGG
- WEBM

### File Requirements

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| **Maximum size** | 50 MB |
| **Minimum duration** | 10 seconds |
| **Maximum duration** | 3 minutes |

### Steps to Upload

1. Click the **"Upload Audio"** tab
2. Drag the file or click to select it
3. Wait for upload to finish
4. Verify details (duration, size, format)
5. If needed, click **"Change File"**

---

## Option 3: Write with Text

If you prefer, you can create the Brief by typing manually instead of recording or uploading audio. To know what to include, use the **"What to Say in the Audio"** section as your guide.

### Steps to Write

1. Click the **"Write with Text"** tab
2. Answer the guide questions in writing about your business
3. Review your text to make sure it is clear and complete
4. When you are satisfied, continue to the next step

## Common Errors

### "Audio too short"

**Message:** `Please record at least 10 seconds of audio to provide enough context.`

**Solution:** Record at least 10 seconds and answer the guide questions.

### "Maximum duration reached"

**Message:** `The recording stopped automatically upon reaching 3 minutes.`

**Solution:** This is expected. If needed, record again with a shorter and clearer summary.

### "Could not access microphone"

**Message:** `Please allow microphone access in your browser.`

**Solution:**

1. Click the lock icon in the address bar
2. Find "Microphone" and select "Allow"
3. Reload the page and try again

### "File too large"

**Message:** `The audio file must not exceed 50MB.`

**Solution:** Compress the file or record a shorter one in the platform.

### "Invalid file"

**Message:** `Please select a valid audio file.`

**Solution:** Use one of the supported formats: MP3, WAV, M4A, OGG, WEBM.
