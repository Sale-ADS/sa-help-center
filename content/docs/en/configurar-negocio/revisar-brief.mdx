The Brief is the most important document in your SaleAds account. It is the foundation of everything I generate for you: copies, targeting, communication tone. A well-tuned Brief means ads that connect with your audience. A neglected Brief means missed opportunities. That's why it's worth dedicating a few minutes to reviewing it.

I generate the Brief based on your audio and your social networks; you review, adjust, and approve it. **You have full control.**

---

## View the Generated Brief

After processing, you'll see the Brief organized in clear sections. Each section captures a different aspect of your business. Here I explain what to look for in each one and what to adjust if something isn't right.

### Business Information

| Field                  | Description                      |
| ---------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| **Business Name**      | Identified trade name            |
| **Industry**           | Your business sector             |
| **Description**        | Summary of what your business does |
| **Value Proposition**  | What makes it unique             |

**What to verify:** Make sure the description captures the essence of what you do. If you sell artisan coffee, it should say so clearly, not something generic like "food company". The value proposition is especially important: it's what sets you apart from the competition and what I use to make your ads stand out.

### Target Audience

| Field                   | Description                          |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| **Description**         | Your ideal customer profile          |
| **Pain Points**         | Problems it solves                   |
| **Main Interests**      | Topics relevant to your audience     |

**What to verify:** Pain points are the emotional hook of your ads. If I identify that your audience is looking to "save time", the copies will focus on that. Check that the problems listed are the ones your customers actually face.

### Brand Identity

| Field                      | Description                 |
| -------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| **Brand Voice**            | Communication tone          |
| **Personality Traits**     | Characteristics of your brand |
| **Key Messages**           | What you want to communicate |

**What to verify:** If your brand is fun and approachable, but the Brief says "professional and formal", correct this. The brand voice defines how all your copies sound. Key messages are the central ideas that I will repeat in different formats throughout your ads.

### Visual Identity

| Field                    | Description                   |
| ------------------------ | ----------------------------- |
| **Identity Colors**      | Suggested color palette       |
| **Content Theme**        | General style                 |
| **Visual Style**         | Recommended image types       |

### Marketing Strategy

| Field                       | Description                   |
| --------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| **Main Goals**              | Your advertising goals        |
| **Main Call to Action**     | Action you want them to take  |
| **USPs**                    | Unique selling points         |

**What to verify:** The Call to Action is what you ask your audience to do. If your goal is for them to message you on WhatsApp, make sure this is reflected here. The USPs (unique selling points) are what I highlight when generating persuasive copies.

## Edit the Brief

If something isn't correct or you want to fine-tune the details, the editing process is simple and straightforward.

### Steps to Edit

1. Click the **"Edit"** button
2. The fields will become editable
3. Modify what you need
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

### What You Can Edit

You can modify any section. These are the most common and impactful edits:

* Correct inaccurate information (name, industry, description)
* Add details that were missing from the audio
* Adjust the tone of messages so it sounds more like you
* Change goals if your business strategy has evolved
* Update the audience with more precise data

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  Pay special attention to the **Target Audience** and **Key Messages**, as these directly influence the copies of your ads. A small adjustment here can significantly improve the quality of your campaigns.
</Callout>


---

## Regenerate the Brief

If the Brief doesn't capture your business well and manual edits aren't enough, you can start from scratch:

1. Go back to the Brief creation step
2. Record a new audio or write a new text with more details about your business, audience, and goals
3. The AI will generate a completely updated new Brief

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  Regenerating completely replaces the previous Brief. If you made manual edits you want to keep, note them down before regenerating.
</Callout>

## Audience Location in the Brief

You will also see the **Audience Location** section integrated into the Brief:

* Shows the locations you previously selected
* You can modify them from this view
* Changes affect the targeting of your strategies

Remember that location is one of the most important factors for your ad performance. The more precise it is, the better your budget investment will be.

## Social Media Insights

If you connected social networks, you will see additional information that enriches the Brief:

| Field                           | Description                       |
| ------------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| **High-Performance Content**    | What type of posts work best      |
| **Engagement Level**            | How active your audience is       |
| **Posting Consistency**         | How frequently you post           |

This information helps me generate copies more aligned with your current style and with what has already proven to work on your networks.

---

## View the Brief Later

You can access the Brief at any time from:

1. Go to **Set Up Your Business**
2. You'll see your Brief with the option to edit

The Brief is used automatically every time:

* I generate copies for your ads
* You create new strategies
* The AI needs to understand your business for any task

---

## Multiple Businesses

If your plan includes multiple businesses, each one has its own independent Brief:

* Each business has its own Brief with its own identity
* Use the **business selector** to switch between them
* Configure the Brief for each business separately

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  Make sure you are in the correct business before creating strategies. Copies are generated based on the active business's Brief.
</Callout>
