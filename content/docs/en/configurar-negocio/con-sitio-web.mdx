Follow these steps if you have an active website. The platform will analyze it automatically to enrich your business configuration — **no audio recording needed**.

---

## Step 1: Start the Setup

From the main dashboard, click **Continue** on the **"Set up your business"** step.

![Onboarding - Step 3 Set up your business with Continue button](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/onboarding-configura-negocio.png)

---

## Step 2: What do you offer?

The platform asks about your business type to build better campaigns for you. Choose the option that best describes what you sell:

![What do you offer? screen with Products, Services and Both options](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/que-ofreces.png)

| Option | When to choose it |
|--------|------------------|
| **Products** | You sell physical or digital items: clothing, food, supplements, accessories… |
| **Services** | You offer your time or expertise: consulting, classes, design, legal advice… |
| **Both** | You sell products AND also offer services. |

Select the one that applies. The platform will then ask you to pick your category depending on what you chose:

- **If you chose Products or Both** → you'll see "What type of products do you sell?" with categories like Clothing / Fashion, Supplements / Health, Cosmetics / Beauty, etc.
- **If you chose Services or Both** → you'll see "What type of services do you offer?" with categories like Coaching / Psychology, Design / Creative, Consulting, etc.

Select the category that best describes your business and click **Continue**.

---

## Step 3: Confirm Your Website

The platform automatically detects your website from your Facebook page information. You'll see something like this:

![Website detected screen with URL pre-filled from Facebook](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/sitio-web-detectado.png)

- **If the website shown is yours** → just click **Continue**.
- **If it's not correct or you want to use a different one** → clear the URL and type the new one.
- **If you don't have a website** → click **Continue without a website** to follow the alternative flow.

The platform will automatically analyze your site to understand your business, audience, and value proposition.

<Callout type="info" title="Why enter your website?">
  By analyzing your site, the AI automatically extracts information about your brand, products, and ideal customer. No audio recording is needed.
</Callout>

---

## Step 4: Review What You Offer

The platform automatically detects what you have published on your website and social media. The screen you see depends on what you chose in Step 2:

---

### If you chose Products

Since you entered your website, the platform automatically detects the products you have listed on your site and social media. You don't need to add anything from scratch.

![We found these products screen - grid view](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/productos-detectados-grid.png)

All detected products are ready to use in your campaigns. From this screen you can:

- **Edit** a product (pencil icon) to fix its name, description, price, margin, or photos
- **Delete** products you don't want to include in your campaigns (trash icon)
- **Add a new product** manually using the **+ New product** button

### Two ways to view your catalog

You can switch between grid and list view using the icons in the top right corner:

![We found these products screen - list view](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/productos-detectados-lista.png)

### Editing a product

Clicking the pencil icon on any product opens a panel where you can update all its details:

![Edit product modal with photo, name, description, price and margin](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/editar-producto.png)

You can change the **main photo**, add **additional photos**, and edit the **name**, **description**, **price**, and **margin**. Click **Save** when done.

### Adding a product manually

If a product wasn't detected, click **+ New product** to add it yourself:

![Upload manually modal with photo, name, description, price and margin fields](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/subir-producto-manual.png)

Fill in the fields and click **Save**.

<Callout type="info" title="How many products to review?">
  You don't have to work with all of them. Delete the ones you won't advertise and keep your best sellers. You can edit your catalog at any time.
</Callout>

---

### If you chose Services

The platform automatically detects the services from your website and shows you a form to review and complete each one. You'll see numbered tabs for all the services found at the top of the screen.

![Add services screen with detected service and fields to edit](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/agregar-servicios-detectados.png)

For each service you can fill in or correct:

| Field | What to enter |
|-------|--------------|
| **Service name** | What you call the service you offer |
| **What's included?** | A brief description of what the client gets |
| **Price** | Choose between fixed price, "From $X", quote, or don't show price |
| **How is it booked or hired?** | Via WhatsApp, on your website, in person, or by phone |
| **Where do you serve clients?** | At your location, at their place, online/virtual, or multiple options |

### Adding a new service

If you have a service that wasn't detected, click **+ New service** to add it manually:

![Add services screen with empty form for new service](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/agregar-servicio-nuevo.png)

Fill in all the fields and click **Save**.

<Callout type="info" title="Tip for services">
  Be specific in "What's included?": instead of "consulting", write "60-min session to review your marketing strategy and define the next steps". That makes a big difference in the quality of your ads.
</Callout>

When you're ready, click **Continue** — or **I'll do it later** if you prefer to come back to this step.

---

## Step 5: Your Identity

The platform takes you to the **"Your identity"** screen, where you define the visual and communication elements of your brand. Some fields are pre-filled automatically — review each one and adjust as needed.

![Your identity screen - initial view with empty fields](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/tu-identidad-vacio.png)

### Logo and business name
Click **+ Upload logo** to load your business image (PNG or JPG; transparent background recommended). The **business name** is detected automatically from your information, but you can edit it directly in the field if you need to correct it.

### Niche
The platform suggests a niche based on the type of business you configured. If the one shown isn't right, click **×** to remove it and then **+** to add the one that best represents you.

### Style
Define the communication tone of your brand — for example "professional", "friendly", or "creative". Remove the styles that don't fit and add the ones that do with the **+** button.

### Brand colors
On the right panel you define the colors that will appear in your ad pieces. You can add more than one to better represent your palette.

![Your identity screen - with logo uploaded and colors loaded](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/tu-identidad-completo.png)

- Click **+** to add a new color
- Click the **pencil icon** to edit an existing color — this opens the picker with a color wheel and a hex field where you can enter your brand's exact code
- Click **×** to remove a color

![Color picker with wheel and hex field](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/color-picker-marca.png)

When you've reviewed all the fields, click **Continue**.

---

## Step 6: Your Voice

Here you define how you want your brand to sound in your ad copy. The platform pre-suggests options based on your business — you can accept them or swap them out.

![Your voice screen with Tone, Personality and Prohibited Words fields](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/tu-voz.png)

### Tone
Click the **↺** icon next to the current tone to open the selector. Choose from 8 options — each one shows a real example of how an ad would sound with that style:

| Tone | How it sounds |
|------|--------------|
| **Friendly** | Hey! Have you tried our new sandwich yet? |
| **Inspiring** | Every bite tells a story of fresh ingredients and passion |
| **Professional** | We offer high-quality food solutions for businesses |
| **Direct** | Artisan sandwich. $25. Free delivery. Order now. |
| **Enthusiastic** | You WON'T BELIEVE how good our new combo is! |
| **Elegant** | A gastronomic experience designed for the most discerning palates |
| **Fun** | Your boring lunch just died of envy |
| **Bold** | Fast food? No. REAL food that arrives fast. |

![Change tone modal with the 8 available options](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/cambiar-tono.png)

Select the one that best represents your brand and click **Save**.

### Personality
Click the **↺** icon next to the current personality to open the selector. Choose **1 to 5 traits** that describe your brand: Familiar, Empathetic, Honest, Passionate, Adventurous, Brave, Expert, Trustworthy, Leader, Innovative, Original, Fun, Elegant, Exclusive, Sensual, Friendly, Community-driven, or Rebellious.

![Change personality modal with the available traits to select](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/cambiar-personalidad.png)

Check the traits that best represent your brand and click **Save**.

### Prohibited words
Enter words you **don't** want to appear in your ads — for example, competitor terms or expressions that don't fit your image. Click **+** to add one and **×** to remove it.

<Callout type="info" title="Live preview">
  On the right you'll see an example of how an ad piece might look with your chosen tone and personality. It's just a reference image to help you visualize the result.
</Callout>

When done, click **Continue**.

---

## Step 7: Audience

The platform takes you to the **"Audience"** screen, where you can adjust the profile of the people you want to reach with your campaigns. The AI pre-detects this information from your website — review each field and edit as needed.

![Audience screen with description, business location and problem you solve](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/audiencia.png)

### Audience description
The first field shows a summary of who your business is targeting. Click **Edit** to adjust it so it more accurately reflects your ideal customer.

### Business location
Indicate the city, region, or country where your business operates or where your customers are. Click the **pencil icon** to edit it.

### Problem you solve
Describe the main need your business addresses for its customers. Click the **pencil icon** to edit it.

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  Be specific about the problem: instead of "technological innovation", write something like "lack of digital tools to manage projects efficiently". The more concrete you are, the better your ad targeting will be.
</Callout>

When done, click **Continue**.

---

## Step 8: Marketing

The platform takes you to the **"Marketing"** screen, where it uses your business signals to build stronger campaigns. This is the last step — review each field and adjust as needed.

![Marketing screen with Engagement, Consistency, Pain Points and What Makes You Different](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/marketing.png)

### Engagement
Shows the interaction level of your Instagram account as a progress bar. It's detected and calculated automatically from your recent activity.

### Consistency
Indicates how often you post on Instagram — for example, "1 post/week". Also detected automatically.

### Pain points
These are the main needs or frustrations your audience faces. The platform suggests some based on your information — remove the ones that don't apply with **×** and add new ones with the **+** button.

### What makes you different
Define the factors that set your business apart from competitors. Click **+** to add the ones that best represent you.

<Callout type="info" title="Mini-diagnosis">
  On the right panel you'll see a mini-diagnosis generated from your information. If there isn't enough data processed yet, you'll see the message "No diagnosis available yet."
</Callout>

When ready, click **Finish**.

---

Once you complete the process, you'll see the confirmation screen:

![All done! screen with Go to my brand button](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/todo-listo.png)

The platform confirms it has everything it needs to create your ad pieces and prepare your first campaign. Click **Go to my brand** to access the **My Business** section, where you can edit or review your full configuration at any time from the tabs: Identity, Voice, Audience, Marketing, Products, and Services.

![My Business view with configuration tabs](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/mi-negocio-tabs.png)

### Summary
- ✅ Business type and category
- ✅ Website analyzed by AI
- ✅ Products
- ✅ Brand identity & voice
- ✅ Audience
- ✅ Marketing
- ✅ Setup complete — ready to create campaigns
