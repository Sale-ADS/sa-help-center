## How to Add Your Networks

### Step 1: Enter the URLs

The process is simple: copy and paste the URLs of your profiles. You don't need to grant access or provide passwords.

1. In each field, paste the full URL of your profile
2. You must include `https://` at the beginning
3. You can leave fields empty if you don't have that network

**Correct example:** `https://instagram.com/cafejulio`

**Incorrect example:** `@cafejulio` or `instagram.com/cafejulio`

### Step 2: Automatic Scan

When you enter the URLs, I start an automatic scan of your public content. This analysis is what feeds the intelligence behind your copies:

1. **Starting scan...** - Preparing the analysis
2. **Scanning resources...** - Reading your public content
3. **Analyzing resources...** - Processing the information
4. **Processing data...** - Extracting insights
5. **Scan completed** - Ready to continue

> **Estimated time:** 30 seconds to 2 minutes depending on the amount of content on your profiles.

## Common Problems

### The scan takes too long

**Cause:** Profiles with a lot of content (hundreds of posts) may take longer to analyze.

**Solution:** Wait up to 3 minutes. If it doesn't complete, you can continue and the analysis will happen in the background. Don't worry, you won't lose any information.

### Error scanning a network

**Possible causes:** Incorrect URL, profile set to private, or the social network is experiencing temporary issues.

**Solution:**

1. Verify that the URL is correct (including `https://`)
2. Make sure the profile is public
3. Try with another social network and come back to this one later
