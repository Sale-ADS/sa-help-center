The **Add detailed location** option lets you select specific neighborhoods, addresses, or places with a 3 km radius to define exactly where you want your ads to appear. You can configure this in two moments:

- **While creating your business** — it appears in the **Audience** step during the initial setup process.
- **While editing your business** — from **My Business → Audience tab**, at any time.

## Where to find this setting

**If you are creating your business:**
Go through the setup process until you reach the **Audience** step and find the **Location where you want to sell** field.

**If you are editing your business:**
1. In the side menu, click **My Business**.
2. Go to the **Audience** tab.
3. Find the **Location where you want to sell** field.

In both cases, click the search box in the field and select **Add detailed location**.

## How to use the detailed location map

Selecting **Add detailed location** opens a dialog with an interactive map.

### Step 1: Search for a place

Type the name of a neighborhood, address, or place in the search box. A list of suggestions will appear — select the correct option.

![Detailed location dialog with search bar and empty map](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/ubicacion-detallada-mapa-vacio.png)

![Search box showing results for "sabaneta" with options from Colombia, Dominican Republic and Venezuela](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/ubicacion-detallada-busqueda.png)

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  If you search for a city with a common name (e.g. "Sabaneta"), the search will show several options with their country or region. Make sure to choose the right one.
</Callout>

### Step 2: Confirm the point on the map

Once you select a location, a **pin** appears on the map with a **3 km radius circle**. That is the area where your ads will be shown.

At the bottom of the dialog you will see the **Selected locations** section with the place name and the applied radius.

![Map with a pin and 3 km radius circle over Sabaneta, Antioquia](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/ubicacion-detallada-pin-mapa.png)

### Step 3: Add more locations (optional)

You can add multiple zones. Repeat the search to add another location — each new point will appear on the map and in the list below.

![Map with two selected locations: Sabaneta and El Poblado, Medellín](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/ubicacion-detallada-dos-ubicaciones.png)

### Step 4: Remove locations you don't need

Each location in the list has an **×** button on the right. Click it to remove that zone before confirming.

### Step 5: Confirm the locations

Once you have all the zones you want, click **Confirm locations**. The dialog closes and the locations appear as tags in the audience field, each with its own **×** button to remove it if needed.

If you want to keep adding zones afterward, click **Add +** next to the already confirmed tags.

![Audience tab with Sabaneta and El Poblado confirmed as location tags](/images/screenshots/03-configurar-negocio/audiencia-ubicaciones-confirmadas.png)

## Recommendations

- **Local businesses:** add your neighborhood or the neighborhoods where your most frequent customers are.
- **Multi-location businesses:** add one location per branch or delivery zone.
- **E-commerce:** if you have national coverage, it's better to use a city or country from the general location field instead of the detailed map.

<Callout type="info" title="Changes apply immediately">
  Locations you confirm will be used in your upcoming campaigns. If you need to adjust them, return to the **Audience** tab at any time.
</Callout>
