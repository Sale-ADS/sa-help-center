Your business information **is not filled in separate fields**. All information is provided in the **Brief**.

Tell me about your business in the Brief and I use that information to generate personalized strategies and copies.



---

## Where to Configure the Information

1. In the side menu, click **"Set Up Your Business"**
2. Your business information is defined in the **Brief** (create with audio or review and edit)

There are no separate "basic data" forms. Everything I need to know about your business (name, what you sell, who you target, value proposition, etc.) you provide in the Brief.

---

## How to Provide Information in the Brief

You can create or update your Brief in two ways:

* [**Create Brief with Audio**](brief-audio) -- Record an audio telling me about your business
* [**Review and Edit Brief**](revisar-brief) -- Review what I generated from the audio and edit or complete in text

In the Brief you tell me, for example:

* Business name and what products or services you offer
* Who you target (your audience)
* What makes you different or your value proposition
* Location or where you operate

**The more you tell me in the Brief, the better the copies and the targeting of your ads will be.**

---

## Change the Information Later

To modify your business information:

1. Go to **Set Up Your Business**
2. Go to **Review and Edit Brief** (or create the Brief with audio again)
3. Adjust the text or record a new audio
4. Save the changes

**You have full control** of your business information through the Brief.
